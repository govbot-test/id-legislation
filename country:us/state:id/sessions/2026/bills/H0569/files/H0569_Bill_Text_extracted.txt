26 warning flag that must be international orange or red in color and must be at
27 least one (1) foot square. When any person being towed by the vessel becomes
28 disengaged from the towline and is down in the water, a person in the vessel
29 must immediately hold the warning flag aloft, visible from all sides, as an
30 indicator to other vessels in the area that a person is down in the water. As
31 long as such downed person is in the water, the flag must remain displayed to
32 prevent danger to that person and hazards to passing vessels. Such warning
33 flag must be displayed only under these conditions or when other eminent dan-
34 ger exists.
