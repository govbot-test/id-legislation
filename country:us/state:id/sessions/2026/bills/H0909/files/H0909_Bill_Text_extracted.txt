LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 909
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY OF STATE; APPRO-
3 PRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR
4 FISCAL YEAR 2027; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
5 DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Office of the Secretary of State for the
9 Secretary of State Program the following amounts to be expended according to
10 the designated expense classes from the General Fund for the period July 1,
11 2026, through June 30, 2027:
12 FOR:
13 Personnel Costs $20,000
14 Operating Expenditures 330,000
15 TOTAL $350,000
16 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
18 July 1, 2026.
