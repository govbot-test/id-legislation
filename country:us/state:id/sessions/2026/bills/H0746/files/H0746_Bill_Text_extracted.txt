LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 746
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO FIRE; AMENDING CHAPTER 1, TITLE 38, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION
3 OF A NEW SECTION 38-131C, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING
4 DEFICIENCY WARRANTS FOR FIRE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING
5 AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 1, Title 38, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 38-131C, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 38-131C. DEFICIENCY WARRANTS FOR COSTS OF PROVIDING FIRE RESOURCES
11 FOR NON-FIRE SUPPRESSION ACTIVITIES. (1) The state board of land commis-
12 sioners may authorize the issuance of deficiency warrants for the purpose of
13 paying the reimbursable costs of providing fire resources for non-wildland
14 fire suppression activities as mobilized by established wildland fire and
15 emergency management dispatch and deployment processes and procedures.
16 (2) When so authorized, the state controller shall draw deficiency war-
17 rants against the general fund.
18 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
20 July 1, 2026.
