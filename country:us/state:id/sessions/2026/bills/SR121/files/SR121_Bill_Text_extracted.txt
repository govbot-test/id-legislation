LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 121
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL PENDING RULES OF THE
3 IDAHO TRANSPORTATION DEPARTMENT HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE
4 SENATE TRANSPORTATION COMMITTEE.
5 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
7 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
8 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
9 letin; and
10 WHEREAS, the Senate Transportation Committee reviewed pending rules
11 adopted by the Idaho Transportation Department.
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
13 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
14 all pending rules adopted by the Idaho Transportation Department, pursuant
15 to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted through the Office of
16 the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for review during
17 the 2026 legislative session have been reviewed and approved by the Senate
18 Transportation Committee.
