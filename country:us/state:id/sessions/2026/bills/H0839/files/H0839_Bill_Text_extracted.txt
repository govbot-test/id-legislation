LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 839
BY REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO TAXATION; AMENDING SECTION 63-3612, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A DEF-
3 INITION; AMENDING SECTION 63-3613, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A DEFINITION;
4 PROVIDING APPLICABILITY; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING
5 RETROACTIVE APPLICATION.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 63-3612, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 63-3612. SALE. (1) The term "sale" means any transfer of title, ex-
10 change or barter, conditional or otherwise, of tangible personal property
11 for a consideration and shall include any similar transfer of possession
12 found by the state tax commission to be in lieu of, or equivalent to, a trans-
13 fer of title, exchange or barter.
14 (2) "Sale" shall also include the following transactions when a consid-
15 eration is transferred, exchanged or bartered:
16 (a) Producing, fabricating, processing, printing, or imprinting of
17 tangible personal property for consumers who furnish, either directly
18 or indirectly, the tangible personal property used in the producing,
19 fabricating, processing, printing, or imprinting.
20 (b) Furnishing, preparing, or serving food, meals, or drinks and nonde-
21 preciable goods and services directly consumed by customers included in
22 the charge thereof.
23 (c) A transfer of possession of property where the seller retains the
24 title as security for the payment of the sales price.
25 (d) A transfer of the title or possession of tangible personal property
26 which has been produced, fabricated, or printed to the special order of
27 the customer, or of any publication.
28 (e) Admission to a place or for an event in Idaho, provided that an or-
29 ganization conducting an exempt function as defined in section 527 or
30 exempted by section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, as incor-
31 porated in section 63-3004, Idaho Code, and collecting any charges for
32 attendance at the aforementioned event, shall not have those admission
33 charges be defined as a sale if the event:
34 (i) Is not predominately recreational or commercial; and
35 (ii) Any included entertainment value is minimal when compared to
36 the charge for attendance; and
37 (iii) Such entity has paid sales and use tax on taxable property or
38 services used during the event.
39 (f) The use of or the privilege of using tangible personal property or
40 facilities for recreation.

21 SECTION 2. That Section 63-3613, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
22 amended to read as follows:
23 63-3613. SALES PRICE. (a) The term "sales price" means the total
24 amount for which tangible personal property, including services agreed to be
25 rendered as a part of the sale, is sold, rented or leased, valued in money,
26 whether paid in money or otherwise, without any deduction on account of any
27 of the following:
28 1. The cost of the property sold. However, in accordance with such
29 rules as the state tax commission may prescribe, a deduction may be
30 taken if the retailer has purchased property for some purpose other than
31 resale or rental, has reimbursed his vendor for tax which the vendor is
32 required to pay to the state or has paid the use tax with respect to the
33 property, and has resold or rented the property prior to making any use
34 of the property other than retention, demonstration or display while
35 holding it for sale in the regular course of business. If such a deduc-
36 tion is taken by the retailer, no refund or credit will be allowed to his
37 vendor with respect to the sale of the property.
38 2. The cost of materials used, labor or service cost, losses, or any
39 other expense.
40 3. The cost of transportation of the property prior to its sale.
41 4. The face value of manufacturer's discount coupons. A manufacturer's
42 discount coupon is a price reduction coupon presented by a consumer to
43 a retailer upon purchase of a manufacturer's product, the face value of
44 which may only be reimbursed by the manufacturer to the retailer.
45 (b) The term "sales price" does not include any of the following:
46 1. Retailer discounts allowed and taken on sales, but only to the extent
47 that such retailer discounts represent price adjustments as opposed to
48 cash discounts offered only as an inducement for prompt payment.

27 SECTION 3. The provisions of this act shall apply to all appeals pend-
28 ing on and after January 1, 2026, involving interpretation or application of
29 sections 63-3612 and 63-3613, Idaho Code.
30 SECTION 4. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
31 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
32 passage and approval, and retroactively to January 1, 2026.
