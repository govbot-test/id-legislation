12 (2) The State Department of Education shall distribute $450,000 for
13 a competitive grant program to assist school districts and public char-
14 ter schools in which English language learners are not reaching statewide
15 accountability interim targets or long-term goals, as defined by federal
16 law. This amount shall be distributed annually in three-year grant cycles,
17 contingent on appropriation and the ability of grantees to meet program ob-
18 jectives.
19 The State Department of Education shall develop the program elements
20 and objectives governing the use of these funds and include a program evalua-
21 tion component.
22 The purpose of these funds is to improve student English language skills
23 to allow for better access to the educational opportunities offered in pub-
24 lic schools. The State Department of Education shall report to the Joint
25 Finance-Appropriations Committee, the Senate Education Committee, and the
26 House Education Committee by no later than January 5, 2027, on the program
27 design, use of funds, and program effectiveness.

5 SECTION 30. DEFINITIONS. (1) As used in Sections 8, 9, 12, and 21 of this
6 act, "distributed" means moneys that are transferred to school districts and
7 public charter schools, with no funds withheld for any other contract or ad-
8 ministrative costs.
9 (2) As used in Sections 14, 15, and 17 of this act, "distributed" means
10 moneys that are transferred to school districts, public charter schools, and
11 the Idaho Digital Learning Academy, with no funds withheld for any other con-
12 tract or administrative costs.

14 moneys that pay for the cost of contracts that provide services to school
15 districts, public charter schools, or students or that pay for the State
