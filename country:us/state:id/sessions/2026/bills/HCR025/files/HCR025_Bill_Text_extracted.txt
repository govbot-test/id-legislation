LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 25
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND REQUESTING THAT CONGRESS CALL A CON-
3 VENTION FOR PROPOSING AMENDMENTS UNDER ARTICLE V OF THE CONSTITUTION OF
4 THE UNITED STATES LIMITED TO PROPOSING AN AMENDMENT TO THE CONSTITUTION
5 OF THE UNITED STATES REQUIRING THAT, IN THE ABSENCE OF A NATIONAL EMER-
6 GENCY, THE TOTAL OF ALL FEDERAL OUTLAYS MADE BY CONGRESS FOR ANY FIS-
7 CAL YEAR MAY NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL OF ALL ESTIMATED FEDERAL REVENUES FOR
8 THAT FISCAL YEAR, TOGETHER WITH ANY RELATED AND APPROPRIATE FISCAL RE-
9 STRAINTS.
10 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
11 WHEREAS, the United States Congress has run up a debt so massive that
12 it has endangered the United States government, the United States economy,
13 United States national defense, and all 50 states; and
14 WHEREAS, Article V of the Constitution of the United States mandates
15 that upon the "Application of the Legislatures of two thirds of the several
16 States" Congress shall "call a Convention for proposing Amendments"; and
17 WHEREAS, historically when the states have come within one application
18 of the two-thirds of the states, which is 34 out of the 50 states, necessary
19 to call a convention to propose a specific amendment, Congress has acted to
20 propose said amendment in order to prevent a convention from being called;
21 and
22 WHEREAS, President Ronald Reagan advocated the use of a "thirty-three
23 state strategy" or "preemption" to pressure Congress to propose a balanced
24 budget amendment for ratification by the states; and
25 WHEREAS, the Legislature deems that an amendment to the Constitution of
26 the United States that would require a balanced federal budget is necessary
27 for the good of the American people.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular Ses-
29 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and
30 the Senate concurring therein, that the Idaho Legislature applies to Con-
31 gress, under the provisions of Article V of the Constitution of the United
32 States, for the calling of a convention of the states limited to proposing
33 an amendment to the Constitution of the United States requiring that, in the
34 absence of a national emergency, the total of all federal outlays made by
35 Congress for any fiscal year may not exceed the total of all estimated fed-
36 eral revenues for that fiscal year, together with any related and appropri-
37 ate fiscal restraints.
38 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that copies of this application must be transmit-
39 ted to the President of the United States, the Secretary of the United States
40 Senate, the Speaker and Clerk of the United States House of Representatives,
41 and the members of the United States Senate and the United States House of
42 Representatives from this state.

2
1 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that copies of this application must also be
2 transmitted to the presiding officers of each of the legislative houses in
3 the several states, requesting their cooperation in this endeavor.
4 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this application constitutes a continuing
5 application in accordance with Article V of the Constitution of the United
6 States until the legislatures of at least two-thirds of the several states
7 have made applications on the same subject, or until December 31, 2033,
8 whichever occurs earlier. It supersedes all previous applications by this
9 legislature on the same subject.
