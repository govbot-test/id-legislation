LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 28
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE HAVE BEEN REVIEWED
4 AND APPROVED BY THE HOUSE AGRICULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE.
5 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
7 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
8 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
9 letin; and
10 WHEREAS, the House Agricultural Affairs Committee reviewed temporary
11 and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Department of Agriculture.
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
13 tatives, assembled in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
14 Legislature, that all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State
15 Department of Agriculture, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act
16 and submitted through the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator
17 to the Legislature for review during the 2026 legislative session have been
18 reviewed and approved by the House Agricultural Affairs Committee.
