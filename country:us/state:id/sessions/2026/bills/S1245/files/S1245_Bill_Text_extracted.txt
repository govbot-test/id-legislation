28 SECTION 1. IDAHO CODE CLEANUP -- LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent
29 of the Legislature to ensure that the state laws provided in Idaho Code are
30 streamlined, up-to-date, and essential for the citizens of Idaho, while best
31 serving the public health, safety, and welfare. In accordance with the Idaho
32 Code Cleanup Act, following comprehensive review, the Legislature has iden-
33 tified the provisions of this act as obsolete, outdated, or unnecessary.
