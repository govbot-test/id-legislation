LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 823
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO COUNTY FAIR BOARDS; AMENDING SECTION 22-202A, IDAHO CODE, TO RE-
3 VISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE DESIGNATION OF A COUNTY FAIR BOARD AS AN
4 ADVISORY BOARD TO THE BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS; AND DECLARING AN
5 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 22-202A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 22-202A. DESIGNATION OF COUNTY FAIR BOARD AS ADVISORY BODY. (1) In
10 counties with a population of two hundred thousand (200,000) persons or
11 more, on or before January 1, 2024, the board of county commissioners may
12 provide by ordinance that the county fair board shall function as an advisory
13 board to the board of county commissioners. If such an ordinance is adopted,
14 the board of county commissioners shall retain and may exercise the powers,
15 duties, and responsibilities otherwise charged to the county fair board
16 by the provisions of this chapter. Any such ordinance shall set forth the
17 powers, duties, responsibilities, compensation, and terms of office of the
18 county fair board and may provide for any such other rules and regulations
19 under which the county fair board shall advise the board of county commis-
20 sioners and conduct its operations. Any such ordinance may be repealed at
21 any time, and if repealed, the provisions of this chapter shall apply as if no
22 such ordinance had been adopted.
23 (2) The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to:
24 (a) fair Fair districts in two (2) or more counties organized pursuant
25 to chapter 3, title 22, Idaho Code.; or
26 (b) Counties in which the population reached two hundred thousand
27 (200,000) persons or more after January 1, 2024.
28 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
29 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
30 July 1, 2026.
