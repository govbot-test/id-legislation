LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 872
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ELECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 34-906, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE A PRO-
3 VISION REGARDING CERTAIN BALLOTS FOR GENERAL ELECTIONS; AND DECLARING
4 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 34-906, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 34-906. BALLOTS FOR GENERAL ELECTIONS. (1) There shall be a single
9 general election ballot on which the complete ticket of each political party
10 shall be printed. Each political party ticket shall include that party's
11 nominee for each particular office. The secretary of state shall design the
12 general election ballot to allow for write-in candidates when needed.
13 (2) The office titles shall be listed in order beginning with the high-
14 est federal office. The secretary of state has the discretion and authority
15 to arrange the above classifications of offices as provided by law.
16 (3) At any general election at which the electors are to vote upon con-
17 stitutional amendments or other issues, the secretary of state shall provide
18 separate general election ballot forms on which such amendments and issues
19 shall be printed.
20 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
22 July 1, 2026.
