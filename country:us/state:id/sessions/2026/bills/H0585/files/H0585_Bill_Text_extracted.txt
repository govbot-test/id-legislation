LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 585
BY BUSINESS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ELECTRICAL, PLUMBING, AND HEATING, VENTILATION, AND AIR CONDI-
3 TIONING INSPECTIONS; AMENDING CHAPTER 10, TITLE 54, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
4 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 54-1004A, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR CERTAIN
5 INSPECTION REQUIREMENTS; AMENDING CHAPTER 26, TITLE 54, IDAHO CODE,
6 BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 54-2626A, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR
7 CERTAIN INSPECTION REQUIREMENTS; AMENDING CHAPTER 50, TITLE 54, IDAHO
8 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 54-5020A, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE
9 FOR CERTAIN INSPECTION REQUIREMENTS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
10 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
12 SECTION 1. That Chapter 10, Title 54, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
13 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
14 ignated as Section 54-1004A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
15 54-1004A. INSPECTIONS -- TIMELINESS -- REFUNDS. (1) If an inspection
16 requested by a permit holder is not performed within forty-eight (48) busi-
17 ness hours, such permit holder shall be authorized to hire a third-party in-
18 spector to perform such inspection. The permit holder or third-party in-
19 spector shall notify the division or local government that such inspection
20 is being completed by a third-party inspector. The permit holder shall pro-
21 vide a copy of the results of the completed inspection to the division or lo-
22 cal government. A permit holder who obtains a third-party inspection under
23 this section shall be refunded any fee, or portion thereof, that the permit
24 holder paid to the division or local government for such inspection. If a lo-
25 cal government contracts with and pays the division, county, or third party
26 for an inspection that requires a refund pursuant to this section, the divi-
27 sion, county, or third party shall be responsible for refunding the permit
28 holder.
29 (2) The division or a local government shall refund ten percent (10%) of
30 the fees, or the portion thereof, paid by a permit holder for an inspection if
31 an inspector or electrical code administrator:
32 (a) Conducts an inspection on the work relating to the permit;
33 (b) Determines that the work has failed an inspection; and
34 (c) Fails to, within three (3) business days, provide the permit holder
35 or his agent with a reason for the failure of such inspection pursuant to
36 the provisions of this chapter.
37 (3) A third-party inspector under this section shall meet the qualifi-
38 cations prescribed by section 54-1019, Idaho Code, and shall conduct the in-
39 spection in substantial accord with the applicable jurisdiction's standards
40 provided by law, rule, or ordinance.
