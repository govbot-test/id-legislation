LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
Moved by Galloway
Seconded by Lakey
IN THE SENATE
SENATE AMENDMENT TO S.B. NO. 1330
1 AMENDMENT TO THE BILL
2 On page 1 of the printed bill, following line 21, insert:
3 "SECTION 2. That Section 1-2311, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
4 amended to read as follows:
5 1-2311. APPEAL TO LAWYER MAGISTRATE. If either party is dissatisfied,
6 he may, within thirty (30) days from the entry of said judgment against him,
7 appeal to a lawyer magistrate other than the magistrate who entered said
8 judgment; and if the final judgment is rendered against him by such lawyer
9 magistrate, then he shall pay, in addition to any judgment rendered in the
10 magistrate's division, an attorney's fee to the prevailing party in the sum
11 of twenty-five dollars ($25.00) as provided in section 12-120(6), Idaho
12 Code, provided, however, that appeals from such small claims department
13 shall be allowed only in such cases as appeals would be allowed if the action
14 were instituted in the magistrate's division as is now provided, and further
15 provided that the appeal shall be heard in the county wherein the original
16 small claim was filed. A fee of twenty dollars ($20.00) shall be paid by
17 the party taking the appeal, which shall be paid to the county treasurer who
18 shall, within fifteen (15) days after the end of the month, pay such fees to
19 the state treasurer for deposit into the court technology fund.";
20 and in line 22, delete "2" and insert: "3".
21 CORRECTION TO TITLE
22 On page 1, in line 4, following "CLAIM;" insert: "AMENDING SECTION
23 1-2311, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING ATTORNEY'S FEES;".
