LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1340, As Amended
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO RULES OF THE ROAD; AMENDING SECTION 49-630, IDAHO CODE, TO RE-
3 QUIRE VEHICLES TO BE DRIVEN ON THE RIGHT-HAND LANE ON MULTILANE HIGHWAYS
4 AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 49-655, IDAHO CODE,
5 TO PROVIDE A PENALTY; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFEC-
6 TIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 49-630, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 49-630. DRIVE ON RIGHT SIDE OF ROADWAY -- EXCEPTIONS. (1) Upon all
11 highways of sufficient width a vehicle shall be driven upon the right half of
12 the roadway except as follows:
13 (a) When overtaking and passing another vehicle proceeding in the same
14 direction under the rules governing such movement;
15 (b) When an obstruction exists making it necessary to drive to the left
16 of the center of the highway. Any person doing so shall yield the right-
17 of-way to all vehicles traveling in the proper direction upon the un-
18 obstructed portion of the highway within a distance as to constitute an
19 immediate hazard;
20 (c) Upon a highway divided into three (3) marked lanes for traffic under
21 the applicable rules; or
22 (d) Upon a highway restricted to one-way traffic.
23 (2)(a) Upon all multilane highways, no vehicle shall be driven in the
24 left-hand lane except when directed otherwise, preparing for a left
25 turn at an intersection or private road or driveway, overtaking or pass-
26 ing another vehicle or vehicles proceeding in the same direction, or
27 when right-hand lanes are obstructed by vehicles traveling at less than
28 the normal speed of traffic. However, no vehicle being driven in the
29 left lane except when directed otherwise or preparing for a left turn at
30 an intersection, private road, or driveway shall impede any other vehi-
31 cle that is traveling in the same lane and behind that vehicle.
32 (b) Upon all multilane highways, no vehicle traveling in the left-hand
33 lane shall be driven at a speed slower than any vehicle traveling to its
34 right on the same roadway.
35 (2) (c) Upon all multilane highways, any vehicle proceeding at less
36 than normal speed of traffic at the time and place and under the condi-
37 tions then existing, shall be driven in the right-hand lane available
38 for traffic, or as close as practicable to the right-hand curb or edge
39 of the highway, except when overtaking and passing another vehicle pro-
40 ceeding in the same direction or when preparing for a left turn at an
41 intersection or into a private road or driveway.

2
1 (3) No vehicle shall be driven to the left of the center line upon any
2 highway having four (4) or more lanes for moving traffic and providing for
3 two-way movement of traffic, except when authorized by a traffic-control de-
4 vice designating certain lanes to the left side of the center of the high-
5 way for use by traffic not otherwise permitted to use such lanes, or except
6 as permitted under subsection (1)(b) of this section. This subsection shall
7 not be construed as prohibiting the crossing of the centerline in making a
8 left turn into or from an alley, private road or driveway.
9 (4) A violation of subsection (2) of this section shall be an infraction
10 punishable by a fine of seventy-five dollars ($75.00).
11 (5) The Idaho transportation department is directed to place signs on
12 multilane highways, in an effort to make motorists aware of the provisions of
13 this section.
14 SECTION 2. That Section 49-655, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
15 amended to read as follows:
16 49-655. MINIMUM SPEED REGULATION. (1) No person shall drive a motor
17 vehicle at such a slow speed as to impede the normal and reasonable movement
18 of traffic except when reduced speed is necessary for safe operation or in
19 compliance with the law.
20 (2) Notwithstanding any minimum speed that may be authorized and posted
21 pursuant to this section, no person shall operate a vehicle in the extreme
22 left-hand lane of a controlled-access highway for a period of time that im-
23 pedes the flow of other traffic traveling at a lawful rate of speed. A vio-
24 lation of this subsection shall be an infraction punishable by a fine of sev-
25 enty-five dollars ($75.00).
26 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
27 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
28 July 1, 2026.
