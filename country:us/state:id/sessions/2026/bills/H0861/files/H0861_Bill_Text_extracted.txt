Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026

BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE; AMENDING SECTION 67-4703, IDAHO
3 CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR CERTAIN POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF
4 COMMERCE AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 67-4715,
5 IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE DUTIES AND POWERS OF
6 THE TRAVEL AND CONVENTION INDUSTRY COUNCIL; AMENDING SECTION 67-4717,

10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
11 SECTION 1. That Section 67-4703, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
12 amended to read as follows:
13 67-4703. POWERS AND DUTIES. The department of commerce shall have the
14 power and it shall be its duty to engage in advertising the state of Idaho,
15 its resources, both developed and undeveloped, its tourist resources and
16 attractions, its agricultural, mining, lumbering and manufacturing re-
17 sources, its health conditions and advantages, its scenic beauty and its
18 other attractions and advantages; and in general either directly, indi-
19 rectly or by contract do anything and take any action which will promote
20 and advertise the resources and products of the state of Idaho, develop its
21 resources and industries, promote tourist travel to and within the state of
22 Idaho, and further the welfare and prosperity of its citizens.
23 The department shall also have the following duties when it deals with
24 promoting economic development and tourism within the state:
25 (1) Survey To survey and investigate the social, economic and physi-
26 cal resources of the state, including land, water, minerals, facilities for
27 power, transportation, communications, recreation, health, education and
28 other resources and facilities; endeavor to aid the legislature and the cit-
29 izens of the state of Idaho in formulating a program for the development and
30 utilization of these resources and facilities, and for balancing our agri-
31 cultural, timber and mining economy with industrial capacity. It shall co-
32 operate with local and regional agencies within the state. It shall cooper-
33 ate with like agencies of other states, with agencies maintained by private
34 persons or corporations, and with agencies established or employed by the
35 United States to promote the development of the country and the welfare of
36 its people.
37 (2) To develop and promote a comprehensive international marketing
38 plan for Idaho's products.
39 (3) To collect and compile reliable data for general dissemination
40 which will tend to the development of the state of Idaho by inducing people
41 and capital to come within our borders.

31 SECTION 2. That Section 67-4715, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
32 amended to read as follows:
33 67-4715. DUTIES AND POWERS OF THE COUNCIL. (1) Consistent with the
34 general purposes of this chapter, the council shall advise the department
35 upon the establishment of policies to be followed in the accomplishments of
36 the purposes of this act.
37 (2) In the administration of this act, the council shall have the fol-
38 lowing duties, authorities and powers:
39 (a) To review and recommend acceptance or denial of local grant re-
40 quests submitted by nonprofit groups or organizations and state insti-
41 tutions of higher education, for uses consistent with the purposes of
42 this act. Such power shall include the right to approve disbursal of
43 the department moneys to such nonprofit groups and state institutions
44 of higher eduction under the grant program created by section 67-4717,
45 Idaho Code.
46 (b) To counsel and advise the department on matters concerning the pro-
47 motion and marketing of Idaho tourism, including, but not limited to the
48 following: (1) the type of promotion, (2) the media source of promo-

25 SECTION 3. That Section 67-4717, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
26 amended to read as follows:
27 67-4717. REGIONAL AND STATEWIDE GRANT PROGRAM. (1) There is hereby es-
28 tablished within the state of Idaho a travel and convention industry grant
29 program. Such program shall be administered jointly by the council and the
30 department. The source of funding for the program shall be from the assess-
31 ment imposed by section 67-4718, Idaho Code.
32 (2) Fifty percent (50%) of all funds derived from this act, less
33 one-half (1/2) of the administrative costs, shall be returned to local
34 nonprofit organizations having as their primary purpose the promotion of
35 travel and conventions within a planning region, to expend for the promotion
36 of travel and conventions within their area. Each planning region shall
37 be awarded grants totaling an amount equal to the regional proportion of
38 one-half (1/2) the total sum raised by the assessment imposed by section
39 67-4718, Idaho Code. No allocations to a planning region shall lapse, but
40 shall be continuously available for use for promotion purposes within the
41 planning region.
42 (3) The remaining fifty percent (50%) of all funds derived from this
43 act, less one-half (1/2) of the administrative costs, shall be expended by
44 the department for the promotion and development of statewide travel and
45 convention programs and to fund state institutions of higher education as
46 defined in section 67-2332A, Idaho Code, to promote economic development
47 within the state. The department shall be counseled and advised in its cre-
48 ation and execution of such programs by the council as provided by section
49 67-4712, Idaho Code.
