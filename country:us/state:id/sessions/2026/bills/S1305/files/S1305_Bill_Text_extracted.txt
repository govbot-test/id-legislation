LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1305
BY RESOURCES AND ENVIRONMENT COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO WATER DISTRICTS; AMENDING SECTION 42-612, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE
3 PROVISIONS REGARDING WATER DISTRICT BUDGETS AND TO PROVIDE THAT WATER
4 USERS MAY AUTHORIZE A WATER DISTRICT TO CHARGE A FIXED FEE TO COVER AD-
5 MINISTRATIVE COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH PROPERTY OWNERSHIP TRANSFERS; AND
6 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 42-612, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 42-612. PROPOSED WATER DISTRICT BUDGET FOR SUCCEEDING YEAR -- ADOP-
11 TION AND CONTENTS OF BUDGET -- DEBT OF WATER USER. (1) Each watermaster
12 shall, at least fourteen (14) days prior to the annual meeting of the water
13 users of the water district, prepare a proposed budget for the succeeding
14 year, together with a distribution of the pro rata amounts of the budget as-
15 sessed to the respective water users or water delivery organizations using
16 the actual volume of water delivered for the past season or seasons. The
17 proposed budget and distribution of pro rata assessments shall be presented
18 to the water users for consideration and approval at the next annual meeting.
19 (2) At any annual meeting, the water users must shall adopt a budget
20 covering the estimated expenses of delivering the water of the district for
21 the ensuing succeeding year and shall, by resolution, determine that how the
22 moneys composing the budget shall be collected. The compensation of the wa-
23 termaster and the watermaster's assistants and any other expenses of deliv-
24 ering the water of the district to the users thereof, including the costs
25 of the advisory committee in implementing resolutions adopted by the water
26 users of the district for activities other than the payment of the salary and
27 operating expenses of the watermaster and assistants, shall be paid in the
28 manner hereinafter, in this section, provided for by this section.
29 (3) To the extent possible, funding for advisory committee expenses
30 associated with implementing resolutions adopted by the water users for
31 activities other than the payment of the salary and operating expenses of the
32 watermaster and assistants shall come from funds available pursuant to sec-
33 tion 42-613A, Idaho Code. If funds available pursuant to section 42-613A,
34 Idaho Code, are not sufficient to cover expenses incurred in implementing
35 resolutions adopted by the water users, then such expenses shall come from
36 assessments.
37 (4) The budget shall show the aggregate amount to be collected from all
38 the water users in the district and the amount to be paid by each water de-
39 livery organization or other water user. For the purpose of computing the
40 respective amounts to be paid by each water user, the actual volume of water
41 delivered to each water delivery organization or other water user during the
42 past season or seasons, not exceeding five (5) seasons, shall be used as a ba-

33 sociated with transferring relevant water right documentation to new owner-

39 shall constitute a final determination of the amount due for that year with-

41 ing year.
