LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 581
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO SUPREME COURT; AMENDING SECTION 1-213, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 AUTHORIZE THE IDAHO LEGISLATURE TO REVIEW COURT RULES IN CERTAIN IN-
4 STANCES AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY
5 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 1-213, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 1-213. DUTY TO MAKE RULES -- LIMITATION. (1) The Supreme Court supreme
10 court shall prescribe, by general rules, for all the courts of Idaho, the
11 forms of process, writs, pleadings and motions, the manner of service, time
12 for appearance, and the practice and procedure in all actions and proceed-
13 ings. Said rules shall neither abridge, enlarge, nor modify the substantive
14 rights of any litigant.
15 (2) The Idaho legislature may review any court rules, including but not
16 limited to the Idaho rules of family law procedure, the Idaho court adminis-
17 trative rules, or the Idaho juvenile rules. Upon any finding of conflict be-
18 tween court rule and Idaho Code, of rules abridging, enlarging, or modifying
19 substantive rights, the legislature shall advise the supreme court of such
20 conflict through a house or senate resolution.
21 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2026.
