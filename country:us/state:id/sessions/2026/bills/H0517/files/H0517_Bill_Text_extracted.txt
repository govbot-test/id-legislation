Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 517
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE PACIFIC NORTHWEST ECONOMIC REGION; REPEALING CHAPTER 78, TI-
3 TLE 67, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE PACIFIC NORTHWEST ECONOMIC REGION;
4 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Chapter 78, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
7 hereby repealed.
8 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
10 July 1, 2026.
