LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 976
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS; AMENDING SECTION 5 OF HOUSE BILL NO. 968, IF EN-
3 ACTED BY THE SECOND REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLA-
4 TURE, TO PROVIDE A CASH TRANSFER TO THE LOCAL HIGHWAY DISTRIBUTION FUND;
5 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 5 of House Bill No. 968, if enacted by the Sec-
8 ond Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, be, and the same
9 is hereby amended to read as follows:
10 Section 5. CASH TRANSFER TO THE STRATEGIC INITIATIVES
11 PROGRAM FUND AND LOCAL HIGHWAY DISTRIBUTION FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR
12 2026. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State
13 Controller shall transfer 50% of the interest earnings generated
14 in fiscal years 2024, 2025, and 2026 to the American Rescue Plan
15 Act State and Local Fiscal Recovery Fund or $32,877,300, whichever
16 is greater, to be distributed as 60% to the Strategic Initiatives
17 Program (Dedicated) Fund and 40% to the Local Highway Distribution
18 Fund as soon as practicable for the period July 1, 2025, through
19 June 30, 2026. The amount shall be confirmed with the Legislative
20 Services Office Budget and Policy Analysis Division.
21 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
23 passage and approval.
