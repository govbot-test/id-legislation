LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1380
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE ENDOWMENT FUND INVESTMENT BOARD; APPRO-
3 PRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE ENDOWMENT FUND INVESTMENT BOARD FOR
4 FISCAL YEAR 2027; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
5 DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Endowment Fund Investment Board $3,700
9 from the Endowment Earnings Administrative Fund to be expended for capital
10 outlay for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027.
11 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
13 July 1, 2026.
