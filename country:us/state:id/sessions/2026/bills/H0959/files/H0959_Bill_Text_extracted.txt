14 34-1801D. INITIATIVE PROCEDURES FOR FIRE PROTECTION DISTRICTS AND
15 AMBULANCE SERVICE DISTRICTS. Each fire protection district and ambulance
16 service district shall allow direct legislation by the people through the
17 initiative to increase or decrease the property tax budget of the district.
18 Such districts shall follow the procedures set forth in this chapter subject
19 to the following provisions:
20 (1) The county prosecuting attorney of the county in which the district
21 office is located shall perform those duties assigned to the attorney gen-
22 eral.
23 (2) The district clerk shall perform those duties assigned to the sec-
24 retary of state.
25 (3) District initiative elections shall be held on the Tuesday follow-
26 ing the first Monday in November in odd-numbered years.
27 (4) Pursuant to section 34-1809, Idaho Code, the county prosecuting at-
28 torney of the county in which the district office is located shall prepare
29 recommendations concerning revision of the initiative, shall issue a cer-
30 tificate of review to the district clerk, and shall prepare the ballot title
31 and short title.
32 (5) An action brought pursuant to section 34-1809, Idaho Code, chal-
33 lenging the ballot title or short title shall be brought in the district
34 court in the county in which the district office is located.
35 (6) To be eligible to sign a petition for a district initiative, a
36 person shall be a qualified elector of the district at the time of signing
37 thereon.
38 (7) To perfect a petition for a district initiative, the petition shall
39 have signatures from at least twenty percent (20%) of the total number of
40 qualified electors voting in the most recent general district election ac-
41 tually held in November of an odd-numbered year.

40 (a) The proclamation by the district board provided in subsection (17)
41 of this section; or
42 (b) The approval by the district board of a property tax budget reduc-
43 tion that obviates the need to place the initiative on the ballot pur-
44 suant to subsection (14) of this section.
45 (19) This section does not apply to bond elections.
