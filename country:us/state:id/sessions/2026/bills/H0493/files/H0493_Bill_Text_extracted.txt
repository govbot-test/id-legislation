LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 493
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO COMMERCIAL SEXUAL ACTIVITY; AMENDING SECTION 18-5614, IDAHO
3 CODE, TO REVISE THE PENALTY FOR SOLICITING COMMERCIAL SEXUAL ACTIVITY
4 AND TO MAKE A TECHNICAL CORRECTION; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PRO-
5 VIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 18-5614, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 18-5614. SOLICITING COMMERCIAL SEXUAL ACTIVITY. (1) A person is
10 guilty of soliciting commercial sexual activity when he or she:
11 (a) Pays, offers to pay, or agrees to pay another person a fee for the
12 purpose of engaging in sexual contact; or
13 (b) Offers a third person to engage in, or agrees to provide a third per-
14 son to engage in, sexual contact in return for a fee.
15 (2) Soliciting commercial sexual activity shall be a misdemeanor for
16 any person eighteen (18) years of age or older but under twenty-one (21)
17 years of age, provided that a second or subsequent conviction therefor shall
18 be a felony. Any person convicted of a misdemeanor violation of this section
19 shall be sentenced to a minimum period of confinement of five (5) days in jail
20 and may be sentenced to up to one (1) year in jail or fined up to two thousand
21 dollars ($2,000), or both. The minimum period of confinement shall be served
22 in a county jail, and a court shall not have the power to suspend, withhold,
23 retain jurisdiction over, or commute the minimum period of confinement im-
24 posed pursuant to this section.
25 (3) Soliciting commercial sexual activity shall be a felony for any
26 person twenty-one (21) years of age or older. Any person convicted of a
27 felony violation of this section shall be sentenced to a minimum period of
28 confinement of six (6) months in jail and may be sentenced to up to three (3)
29 years in jail or fined up to ten thousand dollars ($10,000), or both. The
30 minimum period of confinement shall be served in a county jail, and a court
31 shall not have the power to suspend, withhold, retain jurisdiction over, or
32 commute the minimum period of confinement imposed pursuant to the provisions
33 of this section.
34 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
35 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
36 July 1, 2026.
