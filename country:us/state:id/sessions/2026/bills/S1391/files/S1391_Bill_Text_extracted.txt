LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1391
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PROPERTY; AMENDING SECTION 18-7108, IDAHO CODE, AS ADDED IN
3 SECTION 1 OF SENATE BILL NO. 1326, AS AMENDED, IF ENACTED BY THE SECOND
4 REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE, TO REVISE PROVI-
5 SIONS REGARDING SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 18-7108, Idaho Code, as added in Section 1 of
8 Senate Bill No. 1326, As Amended, if enacted by the Second Regular Session
9 of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, be, and the same is hereby amended to
10 read as follows:
11 18-7108. SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY. Nothing in this chapter shall:
12 (1) Prevent government agents from conducting lawful investigations
13 from public vantage points or entering private land in response to exigent
14 circumstances;
15 (2) Prevent government agents from entering private land only to the
16 extent necessary to serve civil process or conduct welfare checks;
17 (3) Interfere with the use or administration of water rights as pro-
18 vided for in Idaho law;
19 (4) Interfere with the ability of the department of water resources, or
20 a designated watermaster, to make reasonable entry upon any lands to carry
21 out duties imposed by law;
22 (5) Interfere with the right of the owner or operator of any right-of-
23 way or easement for any ditch, canal, lateral, drain, or other conduit, as
24 described by law;
25 (6) Limit lawful aerial observation conducted in compliance with sec-
26 tion 21-213, Idaho Code;
27 (7) Limit lawful entry to control noxious weeds as authorized in sec-
28 tion 22-2406, Idaho Code;
29 (8) Limit lawful entry by abatement district employees as authorized in
30 section 39-2804, Idaho Code; or
31 (9) Limit lawful entry as authorized in sections 7-705, 40-1310,
32 40-2301, and 54-1230, Idaho Code; or
33 (9) (10) Increase or diminish any rights, protections, or legal au-
34 thorizations with respect to any place of habitation or the associated
35 curtilage.
36 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
37 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
38 passage and approval.
