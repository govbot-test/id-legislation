9 SECTION 3. That Section 39-5703, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
10 amended to read as follows:
11 39-5703. POSSESSION, DISTRIBUTION, OR USE BY A MINOR. (1) It shall
12 be unlawful for a minor to possess, receive, purchase, use, or consume
13 alternative nicotine products, tobacco products, or electronic smoking de-
14 vices or to attempt any of the foregoing.
15 (2) It shall be unlawful for a minor to sell or distribute alternative
16 nicotine products, tobacco products, or electronic smoking devices or to at-
17 tempt either of the foregoing.
18 (3) It shall be unlawful for a minor to provide false identification
19 or make any false statement regarding their age in an attempt to obtain
20 alternative nicotine products, tobacco products, or electronic smoking de-
21 vices.
22 (4) A minor who is assisting with a random unannounced inspection in ac-
23 cordance with this chapter shall not be in violation of this chapter.
24 (5) A minor may possess but not sell or distribute alternative nico-
25 tine products, tobacco products, or electronic smoking devices in the course
26 of employment, for duties such as stocking shelves or carrying purchases to
27 customers' vehicles.
28 (6) Penalties for violations by a minor. A violation of subsection (1)
29 of this section by a minor shall constitute an infraction and shall be pun-
30 ishable by a fine of seventeen dollars and fifty cents ($17.50). The first
31 violation of subsection (2) or (3) of this section by a minor shall consti-
32 tute an infraction and shall be punishable by a fine of two hundred dollars
33 ($200). A subsequent violation of subsection (2) or (3) of this section by a
34 minor shall constitute a misdemeanor and shall be punishable by imprisonment
35 in an appropriate facility not exceeding thirty (30) days, a fine not exceed-
36 ing three hundred dollars ($300), or both such fine and imprisonment. The
37 court may, in addition to the penalties provided in this section, require the
38 minor and, if the minor is under eighteen (18) years of age, the minor's par-
39 ents or legal guardian to attend alternative nicotine product, tobacco prod-
40 uct, or and electronic smoking device awareness programs or to perform com-
41 munity service in programs related to alternative nicotine product, tobacco
42 product, or and electronic smoking device awareness.
