LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Moved by Anthon
Seconded by Den Hartog
IN THE SENATE
SENATE AMENDMENT TO S.B. NO. 1442
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 3
2 On page 2 of the printed bill, in line 40, delete "record and publish"
3 and insert: "file with the state controller"; in line 43, delete "investi-
4 gated, apprehended, detained, and"; in line 46, following "cable." insert:
5 "The state controller shall make such report available for inspection at all
6 times on the website of the state controller."; and delete lines 47 through
7 49, and insert:
8 "(4) If the attorney general determines that a local, county, or state
9 law enforcement agency is not in compliance with the provisions of this sec-
10 tion, he shall notify such agency of its noncompliance and that it has thirty
11 (30) days to cure the violation. If the law enforcement agency fails to cure
12 its noncompliance within thirty (30) days, the attorney general may seek
13 injunctive relief against the agency, and the agency may be subject to the
14 withholding of state funding by the legislature.".
