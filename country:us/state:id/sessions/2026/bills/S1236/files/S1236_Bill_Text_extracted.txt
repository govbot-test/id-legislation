Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1236
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO WOMEN'S COMMISSION; REPEALING CHAPTER 60, TITLE 67,
3 IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE IDAHO WOMEN'S COMMISSION; AND DECLARING AN
4 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Chapter 60, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
7 hereby repealed.
8 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
10 July 1, 2026.
