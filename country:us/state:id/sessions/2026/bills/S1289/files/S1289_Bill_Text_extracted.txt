LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1289
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING CHAPTER 37, TITLE 33, IDAHO CODE, BY THE AD-
3 DITION OF A NEW SECTION 33-3717D, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS
4 REGARDING LIMITATIONS ON SCHOLARSHIPS FOR CERTAIN STUDENT ATHLETES AT
5 IDAHO PUBLIC INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION; AND DECLARING AN EMER-
6 GENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 37, Title 33, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 33-3717D, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 33-3717D. IDAHO PUBLIC INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION -- LIMITA-
12 TIONS ON SCHOLARSHIPS. (1) For the purposes of this section, "scholarship"
13 means any form of financial aid, tuition waiver, grant, or other financial
14 assistance, whether full or partial, awarded by an Idaho public institution
15 of higher education, including those offered entirely or partly on the basis
16 of a student's academic performance or record, athletic ability, or partici-
17 pation in athletics.
18 (2) All Idaho public institutions of higher education shall give pref-
19 erence to Idaho residents in awarding scholarships.
20 (3) For student athletes in a given academic year, Idaho public insti-
21 tutions of higher education shall not award a scholarship to more than ten
22 percent (10%) of the total number of student athletes for all sports rosters,
23 excluding football, to students who are not United States citizens. Foot-
24 ball rosters shall have no more than five percent (5%) of student athletes
25 receiving a scholarship who are not United States citizens at any given time.
26 No men's or women's collegiate athletic team at an Idaho public institution
27 of higher education shall have more than fifty percent (50%) of its student
28 members receiving a scholarship who are not United States citizens at any
29 given time.
30 (4) Scholarships awarded to students who are not United States citizens
31 who are enrolled in an Idaho public institution of higher education prior to
32 the effective date of this act shall not be invalidated by the provisions
33 of this section. Such students shall not be counted toward the ten percent
34 (10%) cap provided for in subsection (3) of this section for a period not to
35 exceed four (4) full-time, consecutive academic years including those al-
36 ready completed or in progress.
37 (5) The Idaho state board of education shall promulgate rules, subject
38 to legislative approval, to implement the provisions of this section.
39 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
40 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
41 July 1, 2026.
