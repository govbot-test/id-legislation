LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1387
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE IDAHO STATE HISTORICAL SOCIETY; APPRO-
3 PRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE IDAHO STATE HISTORICAL SOCIETY FOR
4 FISCAL YEAR 2027; PROVIDING REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY; AND DECLARING
5 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Idaho State Historical Society the fol-
9 lowing amounts to be expended according to the designated expense classes
10 from the Miscellaneous Revenue Fund for the period July 1, 2026, through June
11 30, 2027:
12 FOR:
13 Operating Expenditures $450,000
14 Capital Outlay 36,300
15 TOTAL $486,300
16 SECTION 2. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY. There is hereby reappropriated
17 to the Idaho State Historical Society any unexpended and unencumbered bal-
18 ances appropriated or reappropriated to the Idaho State Historical Society
19 from the General Fund for fiscal year 2026, to be used for nonrecurring ex-
20 penditures related to moving costs for the period July 1, 2026, through June
21 30, 2027. The Office of the State Controller shall confirm the reappropria-
22 tion amount, by fund, expense class, and program, with the Legislative Ser-
23 vices Office prior to processing the reappropriation authorized herein.
24 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
25 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
26 July 1, 2026.
