11 72-1207. IDAHO LAUNCH GRANT PROGRAM RETURN ON INVESTMENT ANALY-
12 SIS. (1) At least biennially, the legislative services office shall conduct
13 a return on investment analysis of the Idaho launch grant program estab-

15 (a) Determine the alignment of the program's in-demand careers with em-

18 (b) Identify the program's eligible in-demand careers that are linked

22 gible coursework under the program as defined in section 72-1204, Idaho
23 Code;
24 (d) Provide information that can be used to improve the alignment and
25 quality of the program and increase access and successful outcomes for

27 (e) Provide information about the availability, quality, and student
28 outcomes of the program and its eligible institutions under the program
29 as defined in section 72-1204, Idaho Code, to support students and fami-
30 lies in making informed decisions about educational options.
