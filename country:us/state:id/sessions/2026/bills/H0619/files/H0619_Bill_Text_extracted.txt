15 SECTION 1. That the Heading for Chapter 24, Title 41, Idaho Code, be,
16 and the same is hereby amended to read as follows:
17 CHAPTER 24
18 PROPERTY INSURANCE CONTRACTS
19 SECTION 2. That Chapter 24, Title 41, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
20 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
21 ignated as Section 41-2402, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
22 41-2402. IDAHO WILDFIRE RISK MITIGATION FUND ACT -- LEGISLATIVE
23 INTENT -- SHORT TITLE. It is the intent of the legislature to promote
24 self-reliance and lower property insurance costs by reducing wildfire risks
25 and increasing competition among insurers. This shall be accomplished by
26 encouraging and assisting property owners in protecting their property
27 against wildfire risks and mitigating wildfire risks to property owners and
28 their insurance companies, thereby attracting and retaining insurers in the
29 property marketplace. The provisions of sections 41-2402 through 41-2405,
30 Idaho Code, shall be known and may be cited as the "Idaho Wildfire Risk Miti-
31 gation Fund Act."
32 SECTION 3. That Chapter 24, Title 41, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
33 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
34 ignated as Section 41-2403, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
35 41-2403. DEFINITIONS. For the purposes of sections 41-2402 through
36 41-2405, Idaho Code:
37 (1) "Act" means the Idaho wildfire risk mitigation fund act.
38 (2) "Department" means the Idaho department of insurance.
39 (3) "Director" means the director of the Idaho department of insurance.

1 SECTION 4. That Chapter 24, Title 41, Idaho Code, be, and the same is

4 41-2404. IDAHO WILDFIRE RISK MITIGATION FUND. (1) There is hereby cre-
5 ated and established in the state treasury the Idaho wildfire risk mitiga-
6 tion fund. The state treasurer shall invest the idle moneys of the fund, and
7 the interest earned on such investments shall be retained by the fund. The
8 fund is hereby continuously appropriated to be used solely for effectuating
9 the purposes of this act. The fund is established to:
10 (a) Promote self-reliance;
11 (b) Reduce risk of loss due to wildfire;
12 (c) Receive funding from various sources;
13 (d) Improve access to property insurance; and
14 (e) Mitigate wildfire risk, which shall include distributing grants,
15 not to exceed twenty thousand dollars ($20,000) per grant, and educa-
16 tional efforts.
17 (2) The fund shall consist of:
18 (a) The continuous appropriation of excess premium tax authorized pur-
19 suant to section 41-406(1)(e), Idaho Code;
20 (b) Any excess and unused moneys received for fees, licenses, or mis-
21 cellaneous charges collected by the department, not to exceed the de-
22 partment's annual appropriation;
23 (c) Any excess stamping fees, as determined and recommended by the sur-
24 plus line association or other delegated contracted organization and as
25 approved by the director;
26 (d) Private sector contributions;
27 (e) Grants or other contributions for fire prevention or mitigation;
28 and
29 (f) Such other moneys that may be provided by legislative appropria-
30 tion.
31 (3) The fund shall be free from taxation of any kind.

46 (iv) Providing grants to Idaho citizens for wildfire risk mitiga-

13 (a) The director may deposit up to twenty percent (20%) of the funds
14 received in the insurance refund account which is hereby created for

17 insurance refund account so much thereof as shall be necessary for the
18 payment of refunds. Any unencumbered balance remaining in the insur-
19 ance refund account on June 30 of each and every year in excess of forty

21 the state controller is hereby authorized and directed on such dates to
22 make such transfers unless the board of examiners, which is hereby au-
23 thorized to do so, changes the date of transfer or sum to be transferred.
24 (b) That portion of the premium tax, payable to the public employee re-
25 tirement fund as provided in section 59-1394, Idaho Code, shall be dis-

27 (c) That portion of the premium tax necessary to cover administrative
