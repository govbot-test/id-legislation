12 SECTION 2. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR DEDICATED FUNDS. There is
13 hereby reappropriated to the Department of Fish and Game any unexpended and
14 unencumbered balances appropriated or reappropriated to the Department
15 of Fish and Game from dedicated funds for the Administration, Fisheries,
16 Wildlife, and Communications programs for fiscal year 2026 to be used for
17 nonrecurring expenditures for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30,
18 2027. The Office of the State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation
19 amount, by fund, expense class, and program, with the Legislative Services
20 Office prior to processing the reappropriation authorized herein.
21 SECTION 3. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR FEDERAL FUNDS. There is
22 hereby reappropriated to the Department of Fish and Game any unexpended
23 and unencumbered balances appropriated or reappropriated to the Depart-
24 ment of Fish and Game from federal funds for the Administration, Fisheries,
25 Wildlife, and Communications programs for fiscal year 2026 to be used for
26 nonrecurring expenditures for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30,
27 2027. The Office of the State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation
28 amount, by fund, expense class, and program, with the Legislative Services
29 Office prior to processing the reappropriation authorized herein.
30 SECTION 4. USE OF APPROPRIATION. Notwithstanding any law to the con-
31 trary, of the moneys appropriated in Section 1 of this act, $200,000 from
32 the Fish and Game (Other) Fund shall be used to enhance wolf trapping to man-
33 age wolf predation. The balance of the $200,000 not expended for this pur-
34 pose shall revert to the fund. The Department of Fish and Game shall provide
35 a report to the Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee detailing the use of
36 funds by June 30, 2027. The format and content of this report will be de-
37 termined in consultation with the Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the
38 Legislative Services Office.
39 SECTION 5. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
40 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
41 strictions specified in this act.
