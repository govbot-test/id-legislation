LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 930
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CAMPAIGN FINANCE; AMENDING SECTION 67-6604, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE DUTIES OF POLITICAL TREASURERS; AND
4 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 67-6604, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 67-6604. ACCOUNTS DUTIES OF POLITICAL TREASURER. (1) The political
9 treasurer for each candidate or political committee shall keep detailed
10 accounts, current within not more than seven (7) days after the date of
11 receiving the contribution or making an expenditure, of all contributions
12 received and all expenditures made by or on behalf of the candidate or polit-
13 ical committee that are required to be set forth in a statement filed under
14 this chapter serve as the official point of contact for the secretary of
15 state for any candidate's campaign and for any political committee.
16 (2) The political treasurer for each candidate or political committee
17 required to file reports pursuant to section 67-6607, Idaho Code, shall
18 maintain at least one (1) checking account with a financial institution. All
19 moneys received by a candidate's campaign or a political committee shall
20 be deposited in such account. The political treasurer shall keep detailed
21 accounts of all contributions received and all expenditures made by or on be-
22 half of the candidate or political committee. The political treasurer shall
23 keep the accounts current within seven (7) days after the date of receiving a
24 contribution or making an expenditure. Candidate campaign funds and politi-
25 cal committee funds shall be segregated from, and may not be commingled with,
26 any other account. Candidate campaign funds and political committee funds
27 may be invested only in certificates of deposit, money market accounts, or
28 other cash-equivalent accounts held in the name of the campaign or political
29 committee; such funds shall not be invested in stocks or other equity securi-
30 ties. A candidate who loans personal funds to such candidate's campaign in
31 an amount exceeding one thousand dollars ($1,000) shall transfer such funds
32 to the candidate campaign account.
33 (2) (3) The political treasurer shall be responsible for making and
34 filing all reports that are required of a candidate under this chapter.
35 Accounts kept by the political treasurer for a candidate or political com-
36 mittee may be inspected before the election to which the accounts refer by
37 the secretary of state, or county clerk for local government offices or mea-
38 sures, or his agent or employee, who is making an investigation pursuant to
39 section 67-6615, Idaho Code.
40 (3) (4) Accounts kept by a political treasurer shall be preserved by him
41 for at least one (1) year after the date of the election to which the accounts

2
1 refer or at least one (1) year after the date the last statement is filed un-
2 der section 67-6607, Idaho Code, whichever is later.
3 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
4 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
5 July 1, 2026.
