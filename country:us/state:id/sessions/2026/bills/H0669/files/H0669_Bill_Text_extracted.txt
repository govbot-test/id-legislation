14 SECTION 2. That Chapter 43, Title 42, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
15 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
16 ignated as Section 42-4301, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
17 42-4301. CLOUD SEEDING. (1) The legislature finds that:
18 (a) Idaho's economy and the welfare of its citizens depend on a reliable
19 and clean water supply;
20 (b) Data accumulated and analysis undertaken since the initiation of
21 the cloud seeding program demonstrates that cloud seeding lacks prov-
22 able outcomes. In 2024, the government accountability office found,
23 "when uncertainty was considered in estimates of cloud seeding effec-
24 tiveness, the estimated effect was not distinguishable from zero with
25 a high degree of statistical confidence (e.g., with 95 percent confi-
26 dence)"; and
27 (c) Cloud seeding, weather modification, and other forms of geo-en-
28 gineering violate the Idaho constitution, section 1, article I, which
29 provides for the inalienable rights of man, and that all men are by na-
30 ture free and equal, and have certain inalienable rights, among which
31 are enjoying and defending life and liberty, acquiring, possessing and
32 protecting property, and pursuing happiness and securing safety.
33 (2) All cloud seeding programs along with all other weather modifi-
34 cation activities are hereby prohibited and banned from occurring in the
35 state of Idaho. Any and all aerial or ground-based cloud seeding projects
36 or programs or any other form of weather modification shall be ceased indef-
37 initely.
38 (3) Any person, including any public or private entity, who conducts
39 cloud seeding or attempts a weather modification activity in violation of
40 the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a felony punishable by five

2
1 (5) years in prison and a one-hundred-thousand-dollar ($100,000) fine per
2 violation.
