LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1332
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS FOR FISCAL YEARS 2026 AND 2027; TRANSFERRING MON-
3 EYS FROM THE STRATEGIC INITIATIVES PROGRAM FUND TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR
4 FISCAL YEAR 2026; TRANSFERRING MONEYS FROM THE OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIP
5 PROGRAM ACCOUNT TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; TRANSFERRING
6 MONEYS FROM THE PERMANENT BUILDING FUND TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL
7 YEAR 2026; TRANSFERRING MONEYS FROM THE WATER POLLUTION CONTROL FUND
8 TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; TRANSFERRING MONEYS FROM THE
9 IN-DEMAND CAREERS FUND TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; TRANS-
10 FERRING MONEYS FROM THE IN-DEMAND CAREERS FUND TO THE GENERAL FUND FOR
11 FISCAL YEAR 2027; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE
12 DATES.
13 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
14 SECTION 1. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE STRATEGIC INITIATIVES PROGRAM FUND TO
15 THE GENERAL FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State
16 Controller shall transfer $45,000,000 from the Strategic Initiatives Pro-
17 gram Fund to the General Fund as soon as practicable for the period July 1,
18 2025, through June 30, 2026.
19 SECTION 2. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM AC-
20 COUNT TO THE GENERAL FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the
21 State Controller shall transfer $3,000,000 from the Opportunity Scholarship
22 Program Account to the General Fund as soon as practicable for the period
23 July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
24 SECTION 3. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE PERMANENT BUILDING FUND TO THE
25 GENERAL FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State Con-
26 troller shall transfer $33,745,000 from the Permanent Building Fund to the
27 General Fund as soon as practicable for the period July 1, 2025, through June
28 30, 2026.
29 SECTION 4. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE WATER POLLUTION CONTROL FUND TO THE
30 GENERAL FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State Con-
31 troller shall transfer $15,000,000 from the Water Pollution Control Fund to
32 the General Fund as soon as practicable for the period July 1, 2025, through
33 June 30, 2026.
34 SECTION 5. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE IN-DEMAND CAREERS FUND TO THE GENERAL
35 FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State Controller
36 shall transfer $10,000,000 from the In-Demand Careers Fund to the General
37 Fund as soon as practicable for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
38 2026.

2
1 SECTION 6. CASH TRANSFER FROM THE IN-DEMAND CAREERS FUND TO THE GENERAL
2 FUND. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State Controller
3 shall transfer $10,000,000 from the In-Demand Careers Fund to the General
4 Fund once awards have been obligated and reconciled on December 31, 2026, or
5 as soon thereafter as practicable.
6 SECTION 7. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
7 declared to exist, Sections 1 through 5 of this act shall be in full force and
8 effect on and after passage and approval, and Section 6 of this act shall be
9 in full force and effect on and after July 1, 2026.
