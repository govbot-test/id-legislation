7 SECTION 2. That Section 67-5309D, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 67-5309D. OTHER PAY DELIVERY OPTIONS. (1) In addition to pay increases
10 authorized in section 67-5309B, Idaho Code, the department director may
11 grant a classified employee bonus pay not to exceed two thousand dollars
12 ($2,000) in any given fiscal year based on exemplary performance. Excep-
13 tions to the two-thousand-dollar ($2,000) limit provided in this subsection
14 may be granted in extraordinary circumstances if approved in advance by the
15 state board of examiners. Departments shall submit a report to the division
16 of financial management and the legislative services office by October 1 on
17 all bonuses granted in the preceding fiscal year.
18 (2) In addition to pay increases authorized in section 67-5309B, Idaho
19 Code, the department director may grant a classified employee an award pay-
20 ment based on suggestions or recommendations made by the employee that re-
21 sulted in taxpayer savings as a result of cost savings or greater efficien-
22 cies to the department or to the state of Idaho in excess of the amount of the
23 award and in compliance with the rules for employee suggestion awards pro-
24 mulgated by the division of human resources. The award may be an amount up to
25 twenty-five percent (25%) of the amount determined to be the dollar savings
26 to the state, but not in excess of two thousand dollars ($2,000). Exceptions
27 to the two-thousand-dollar ($2,000) limit provided in this subsection may be
28 granted in extraordinary circumstances if approved in advance by the state
29 board of examiners. Departments shall submit a report to the division of fi-
30 nancial management and the legislative services office by October 1 on all
31 employee suggestion awards granted in the preceding fiscal year. Such re-
32 port shall include any changes made as a direct result of an employee's sug-
33 gestion and savings resulting therefrom.
34 (3) In addition to pay increases authorized in section 67-5309B, Idaho
35 Code, the department director may grant award pay to a classified employee
36 for recruitment or retention purposes. Retention bonuses require comple-
37 tion of at least six (6) months of achieving performance standards prior to
38 issuance. The department director and the administrator of the division of
39 human resources are authorized to seek legal remedies available, including
40 deductions from an employee's accrued vacation funds, from an employee who
41 resigns during the designated period of time after receipt of a recruitment
42 or retention bonus. Departments shall submit a report to the division of fi-

44 such awards granted in the preceding fiscal year.
