LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 907
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DIVISION OF CAREER TECHNICAL EDUCATION;
3 APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE DIVISION OF CAREER TECHNICAL
4 EDUCATION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027; APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO
5 THE DIVISION OF CAREER TECHNICAL EDUCATION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AND
6 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
9 there is hereby appropriated to the Division of Career Technical Education
10 for the Secondary and General Programs program $1,877,300 from the Idaho
11 Career Ready Students Program Fund to be expended for trustee and benefit
12 payments for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027.
13 SECTION 2. In addition to the appropriation made in Section 1, Chap-
14 ter 261, Laws of 2025, and any other appropriation provided by law, there is
15 hereby appropriated to the Idaho Division of Career Technical Education for
16 the Secondary and General Programs program $957,600 from the Idaho Career
17 Ready Students Program Fund to be expended for trustee and benefit payments
18 for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
19 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, Section 2 of this act shall be in full force and effect
21 on and after passage and approval, and Section 1 of this act shall be in full
22 force and effect on and after July 1, 2026.
