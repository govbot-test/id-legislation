LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 20
BY MONKS AND RUBEL
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 PROVIDING FOR A JOINT SESSION OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE
3 OF THE SECOND REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE FOR
4 THE PURPOSE OF HEARING A MESSAGE FROM THE GOVERNOR.
5 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, the Governor has informed the House of Representatives and the
7 Senate that he desires to deliver a message to a Joint Session of the House
8 of Representatives and the Senate of the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-
9 eighth Idaho Legislature in the Chamber of the House of Representatives at 1
10 p.m. on Monday, January 12, 2026.
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular
12 Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives
13 and the Senate concurring therein, that the House of Representatives and the
14 Senate meet in Joint Session on Monday, January 12, 2026, at 1 p.m. for the
15 purpose of hearing the message from the Governor.
