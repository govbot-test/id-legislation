Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 578
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO INCARCERATED JUVENILES TRANSITION SERVICES; AMENDING CHAPTER
3 22, TITLE 56, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 56-2208, IDAHO
4 CODE, TO PROVIDE LEGISLATIVE APPROVAL FOR A STATE PLAN AMENDMENT RE-
5 GARDING INCARCERATED JUVENILES TRANSITION SERVICES; AND DECLARING AN
6 EMERGENCY.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 22, Title 56, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 56-2208, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 56-2208. LEGISLATIVE APPROVAL -- INCARCERATED JUVENILES TRANSITION
12 SERVICES. (1) Pursuant to section 1902 of the social security act, the de-
13 partment of health and welfare is authorized to seek and shall submit a state
14 plan amendment by July 1, 2026, that makes the services authorized in this
15 section available for eligible juveniles who are within thirty (30) days of
16 the date they are scheduled to be released from a correctional facility, as
17 that term is defined by section 18-101A, Idaho Code, following adjudication.
18 Services for eligible juveniles shall include:
19 (a) Any screening or diagnostic service that meets reasonable stan-
20 dards of medical, behavioral health, or dental practices, as determined
21 by the state, or as indicated as medically necessary; and
22 (b) Targeted case management services, including referrals for appro-
23 priate care and services available in the geographic region of the home
24 or residence of such eligible juvenile, where feasible, under the state
25 plan or waiver of such plan.
26 (2) This section shall not be construed to authorize services beyond
27 those that are required by section 1902 of the social security act for eligi-
28 ble juveniles on or before July 1, 2026.
29 (3) The services included in any state plan submitted pursuant to this
30 section shall not commence before July 1, 2027, and are subject to legisla-
31 tive appropriation.
32 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
33 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
34 passage and approval.
