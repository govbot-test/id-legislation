BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PEND-
3 ING RULES OF THE IDAHO COMMISSION ON AGING, THE IDAHO COMMISSION FOR

8 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
9 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
10 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
11 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-

14 pending rules adopted by the Idaho Commission on Aging, the Idaho Commission

16 and the Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses; and
17 WHEREAS, the House Health and Welfare Committee did not review and

23 of the department; and

26 at the request of the department.
