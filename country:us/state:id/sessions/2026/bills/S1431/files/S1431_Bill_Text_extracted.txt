3 SECTION 4. FILING FEES. Notwithstanding Section 42-1414(1)(c), Idaho
4 Code, it is the intent of the Legislature that moneys appropriated in Sec-
5 tion 1 of this act for Idaho's general stream adjudications from the General
6 Fund count toward the filing fees required under Section 42-1414(1)(a) and
7 (b), Idaho Code, for water right claims filed in Idaho's general stream ad-
8 judications by the Idaho Water Resource Board and the Governor of the State
9 of Idaho based on instream flow, public lake level maintenance, or recre-
10 ation. If the General Fund appropriation exceeds the amount required for the
11 Idaho Water Resource Board's and the Governor's filing fees in the current
12 fiscal year, the excess shall be counted toward future filing fees required
13 for those same purposes. If the General Fund appropriation does not meet or
14 exceed the fee required for claims of the Idaho Water Resource Board or the
15 Governor, the Director of the Department of Water Resources shall grant an
16 extension of time to the Idaho Water Resource Board or the Governor to file
17 the claim, and the General Fund appropriation for the following fiscal year
18 shall be counted toward the balance of the filing fee.
19 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
21 July 1, 2026.
