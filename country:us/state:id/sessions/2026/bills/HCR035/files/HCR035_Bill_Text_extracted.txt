LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 35
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND RECOGNIZING ALL OF THE IDAHO ATH-
3 LETES WHO PARTICIPATED IN THE 2026 WINTER OLYMPICS.
4 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
5 WHEREAS, sports have always been recognized as a unifying event; and
6 WHEREAS, every four years the world gathers to watch the Olympics; and
7 WHEREAS, the 2026 Winter Olympics took place in Milan and Cortina d'Am-
8 pezzo, Italy; and
9 WHEREAS, it is an amazing feat for any athlete to make the USA Olympic
10 team; and
11 WHEREAS, it is an honor and privilege to represent the United States of
12 America; and
13 WHEREAS, we as Idahoans are proud of our Olympic athletes and appreciate
14 their sacrifices; and
15 WHEREAS, the Legislature wishes to acknowledge the Olympic athletes
16 from Idaho: John Steel Hagenbuch, Breezy Johnson, Chase Josey, Jaelin Kauf,
17 Hilary Knight, Ryder Sarchett, and Sammy Smith; and
18 WHEREAS, it should also be noted that Idaho had three athletes who won
19 medals; and
20 WHEREAS, Breezy Johnson received a gold medal in alpine skiing; and
21 WHEREAS, Jaelin Kauf received two silver medals in ski moguls; and
22 WHEREAS, Hilary Knight received a gold medal as a key member of the ice
23 hockey team; and
24 WHEREAS, Brad Little, the Governor of the State of Idaho, has recognized
25 and congratulated these Idaho athletes.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular
27 Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives
28 and the Senate concurring therein, that the Legislature recognizes the ath-
29 letic achievements of all Idaho athletes who participated in the 2026 Winter
30 Olympics.
