LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 36
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND EXPRESSING SUPPORT AND COMMENDATION
3 FOR THE IDAHO NATIONAL GUARD.
4 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
5 WHEREAS, the Idaho National Guard serves as a cornerstone of both state
6 and national defense, fulfilling its dual mission as a state resource for
7 emergency response and as a federal combat reserve force for the United
8 States Army and Air Force; and
9 WHEREAS, the National Guard is an indispensable component of the United
10 States National Defense Strategy, providing trained, equipped, and ready
11 forces capable of rapid mobilization to defend the sovereignty and security
12 of the nation at home and abroad; and
13 WHEREAS, the Idaho National Guard has a proud history of service to the
14 state and nation, supporting combat operations overseas while remaining
15 ready to respond to emergencies and disasters here at home; and
16 WHEREAS, the Idaho Air National Guard has distinguished itself through
17 decades of dedicated service operating the A-10 Thunderbolt II, a formida-
18 ble close air support aircraft that has protected American forces and allies
19 across numerous combat operations and has been a source of pride for the cit-
20 izens of Idaho; and
21 WHEREAS, the Idaho Air National Guard is now considering a historic
22 transformation by transitioning from the A-10 Thunderbolt II to the advanced
23 F-16 Fighting Falcon, a multirole, fourth-generation fighter jet that would
24 significantly enhance Idaho's ability to support modern air combat and joint
25 operations; and
26 WHEREAS, the Idaho Air National Guard has maintained an unbroken tra-
27 dition of flying fighter aircraft in Idaho since 1947, developing highly
28 trained airmen and distinguished combat aviation formations, and, in tran-
29 sitioning to the F-16 Fighting Falcon, will continue to honor and preserve
30 its long-standing combat aviation heritage that has been part of the organ-
31 ization since its earliest days; and
32 WHEREAS, the Idaho Army National Guard is also undertaking an important
33 force structure modernization, transitioning from an Armor Brigade Combat
34 Team to a Mobile Brigade Combat Team, reflecting the evolving requirements
35 of the Army's operational doctrine and the need for more agile and adaptive
36 ground combat formations; and
37 WHEREAS, the Idaho Army National Guard has maintained an armored tradi-
38 tion in Idaho since 1949, developing generations of highly trained soldiers
39 and distinguished armored formations, and, in transitioning to a Mobile
40 Brigade Combat Team, will continue to honor and preserve its long-standing
41 cavalry heritage that has been part of the organization since its earliest
42 days; and

2
1 WHEREAS, these transformations, both in the air and on the ground, re-
2 flect the Idaho National Guard's continued commitment to readiness, modern-
3 ization, and alignment with the strategic priorities of the United States
4 Department of War and the State of Idaho; and
5 WHEREAS, the men and women of the Idaho National Guard have demonstrated
6 extraordinary professionalism, dedication, and sacrifice in service to the
7 State of Idaho and the United States of America, and their willingness to em-
8 brace transformational change reflects their enduring commitment to excel-
9 lence; and
10 WHEREAS, the Idaho Legislature recognizes the vital importance of sus-
11 taining robust investment, training resources, and community support for
12 the Idaho National Guard as it navigates these transitions, ensuring that
13 Idaho's guardsmen remain ready to answer the call whenever and wherever they
14 are needed.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular
16 Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives
17 and the Senate concurring therein, that the Legislature hereby expresses its
18 wholehearted support for the Idaho National Guard's efforts to modernize its
19 force structure, recognizing the importance of both the proposed transition
20 of the Idaho Air National Guard from the A-10 Thunderbolt II to the F-16
21 Fighting Falcon and the Idaho Army National Guard's transition from an Armor
22 Brigade Combat Team to a Mobile Brigade Combat Team.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature commends the soldiers and
24 airmen of the Idaho National Guard for their continued service, sacrifice,
25 and commitment to defending the State of Idaho and the United States of Amer-
26 ica, and remains committed to providing the best training, equipment, and
27 support to our Idaho National Guard.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature calls on all citizens of
29 Idaho to join in recognizing and honoring the members of the Idaho National
30 Guard, our neighbors, family members, and fellow Idahoans, who stand ready
31 to serve with honor and valor.
