25 SECTION 1. That Section 40-1404A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
26 amended to read as follows:
27 40-1404A. ELECTIONS, TERMS AND SALARIES OF COMMISSIONERS IN CERTAIN
28 COUNTYWIDE HIGHWAY DISTRICTS. (1) In countywide highway districts located
29 in a county with a population of more than two hundred thousand (200,000)
30 persons in which the voters have chosen to establish a countywide highway
31 district at a previous election, the county shall be divided by the county
32 commissioners immediately upon the effective date of this act into five (5)
33 subdistricts, which shall be as nearly equal in population as practicable.
34 No precincts shall be divided. A highway district commissioner shall be a
35 resident of the subdistrict which that he represents. Voters in each sub-
36 district shall vote only for one (1) candidate seeking to represent that
37 each subdistrict. County commissioners, mayors and city council members
38 shall not be eligible to hold office as a countywide highway district com-
39 missioner. At the election held in 1998, commissioners representing subdis-
40 tricts two and five shall be elected for two (2) year terms and commissioners
41 representing subdistricts three and four shall be elected for four (4) year
42 terms. Thereafter, all commissioners shall be elected for four (4) year

2
1 terms. Any incumbent in office on the effective date of this act may complete
2 the term to which they were elected and shall represent the subdistrict in
3 which they reside. Any incumbent in office on the effective date of this act
4 whose term expires on January 1, 2000, shall retain that office until January
5 1, 2000, shall be assigned the subdistrict in which they reside by the county
6 commissioners, which subdistrict shall be numbered one as provided in this
7 section, and that commissioner need not stand for election in 1998.
8 (2) A qualified voter of the countywide highway district shall be eli-
9 gible to vote for a each of the countywide highway district commissioner re-
10 siding in the elector's subdistrict commissioner offices, and the election
11 shall be conducted as provided by Idaho statutes relating to holding general
12 elections at the county level.
13 (3) Beginning with the elections of 2028, the election of highway dis-
14 trict commissioners in a highway district subject to the provisions of this
15 section shall include primary elections pursuant to the provisions of chap-
16 ter 7, title 34, Idaho Code.
17 (4) The highway commissioners shall take office on January 1 of the year
18 immediately following their election, and each may be compensated in accor-
19 dance with the provisions of section 40-1314, Idaho Code, or receive a salary
20 not to exceed one thousand two hundred dollars ($1,200) per calendar month,
21 with the exception of the president of the highway commissioners who may re-
22 ceive a salary not to exceed one thousand four hundred dollars ($1,400) per
23 calendar month.
