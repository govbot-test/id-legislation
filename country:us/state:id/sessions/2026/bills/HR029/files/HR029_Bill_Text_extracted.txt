LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 29
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING FOR THE
3 AMENDMENT OF RULE 18 OF THE RULES OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
4 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
5 WHEREAS, the House of Representatives deems it necessary and desirable
6 that Rule 18 of the Rules of the House of Representatives be amended.
7 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
8 tatives, assembled in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
9 Legislature, that Rule 18 of the Rules of the House of Representatives shall
10 be amended to read as follows:
11 RULE 18
12 Call of the House. -- One-third of the members present may order a call
13 of the House in the following manner:
14 A call being moved and seconded, the Speaker shall require those desir-
15 ing a call to rise, and if one-third of the members present shall rise, there
16 shall be a call of the House. A call of the House being ordered, the Sergeant
17 at Arms shall close and lock the doors entrances to the east wing of the third
18 floor of the state capitol building and no member shall be allowed to leave
19 the Chamber. The Speaker shall immediately cause the roll of the members to
20 be taken and note the absentees whose names shall be read and entered upon the
21 Journal in such manner as to show who are absent with leave and who are absent
22 without leave, and the Sergeant at Arms shall proceed to bring in such absen-
23 tees; but arrest of members of absence shall not be made unless ordered by a
24 majority of members present.
25 While the House is under call, no business shall be transacted except
26 to receive and act on the report of the Sergeant at Arms, and no other mo-
27 tion shall be in order except a motion to suspend further proceedings under
28 the call or to excuse absentees, which motion shall be determined by ayes
29 and nays; and the motion to suspend further proceedings under the call or to
30 excuse members shall not be adopted unless a majority of the entire member-
31 ship vote in favor thereof. While the House is under call, all members shall
32 remain at their floor desks unless the presiding officer puts the House at
33 ease.
34 When the Sergeant at Arms will make a report showing that all who were
35 absent without leave are present, the call of the House may be dispensed with
36 or the House may proceed under the call on a majority vote of the entire mem-
37 bership, with its regular business.
38 No motion for call of the House shall be entered after the House has com-
39 menced voting by ayes and nays.
40 The form of warrant for the arrest of absent members shall be as follows:
