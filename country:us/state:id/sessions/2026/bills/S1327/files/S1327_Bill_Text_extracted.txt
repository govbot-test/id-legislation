LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1327
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ELECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 34-624, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE
3 THAT POLITICAL PARTIES MAY ADD ADDITIONAL QUALIFICATIONS FOR PRECINCT
4 COMMITTEEMEN AND TO MAKE A TECHNICAL CORRECTION; AND DECLARING AN EMER-
5 GENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 34-624, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 34-624. ELECTION OF PRECINCT COMMITTEEMEN -- QUALIFICATIONS. (1) At
10 the primary election, 1980, and every two (2) years thereafter, a precinct
11 committeeman for each political party shall be elected in every voting
12 precinct within each county. The term of office of a precinct committeeman
13 shall be from the eighth day following the primary election until the eighth
14 day following the next succeeding primary election.
15 (2) No person shall be elected to the office of precinct committeeman
16 unless he has attained the age of eighteen (18) years of age at the time of his
17 election, is a citizen of the United States, and is a registered elector of
18 the voting precinct for a period of six (6) months next preceding his elec-
19 tion.
20 (3) Each candidate shall file a declaration of candidacy with the
21 county clerk.
22 (4) No filing fee shall be charged any candidate at the time of his fil-
23 ing his declaration of candidacy.
24 (5) A political party may add additional qualifications for such
25 party's precinct committeemen that are not in conflict with the provisions
26 of this section.
27 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
28 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
29 July 1, 2026.
