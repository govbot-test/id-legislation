LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 587
BY RESOURCES AND CONSERVATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO RANGELAND IMPROVEMENT; AMENDING SECTION 58-1504, IDAHO CODE,
3 TO ALLOW FOR CONTINUOUS APPROPRIATION OF THE RANGELAND IMPROVEMENT
4 ACCOUNT AND TO PROVIDE FOR REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; AND DECLARING AN
5 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 58-1504, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 58-1504. RANGELAND IMPROVEMENT ACCOUNT -- ADMINISTERED BY DEPART-
10 MENT. (1) There is hereby established in the state treasury an account to
11 be known as the rangeland improvement account. Moneys in the rangeland im-
12 provement account are hereby continuously appropriated to be used solely for
13 the purposes provided by this chapter.
14 (2) The account shall consist of moneys received by the state from
15 sales, leases, fees, grants, or appropriations from the state or federal
16 government, and grants from private foundations. Interest earned on moneys
17 in the account shall be retained in the account.
18 (3) The department shall administer the account and distribute account
19 moneys in accordance with section 58-1505, Idaho Code.
20 (4) The department shall provide an annual report to the joint finance-
21 appropriations committee, senate resources and environment committee, and
22 house resources and conservation committee. The report shall provide for
23 all sources and uses of the moneys in the rangeland improvement account for
24 the preceding fiscal year.
25 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
26 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
27 July 1, 2026.
