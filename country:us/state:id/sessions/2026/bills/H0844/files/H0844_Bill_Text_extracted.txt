22 (c) The individual ceases to be an eligible individual as defined in

46 SECTION 2. That Section 41-5214, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
47 amended to read as follows:
48 41-5214. ENHANCED SHORT-TERM PLANS. (1) A carrier may allow enroll-
49 ment in enhanced short-term plans:

3
1 (a) On a year-round basis, at any point in the calendar year;
2 (b) During an annual open enrollment period; or
3 (c) During a special enrollment period.
4 (2) The director shall adopt reasonable rules to establish specific
5 standards for enhanced short-term plans. The standards shall be in addi-
6 tion to and in accordance with applicable laws of this state, including this
7 chapter. The Notwithstanding any provision of law or federal waiver to the
8 contrary, the standards:
9 (1) Shall include requirements for renewability that are consistent
10 with federal law regarding short-term, limited duration insurance; and
11 (a) Shall not be more restrictive than the requirements established in
12 this section;
13 (b) Shall provide that the maximum duration of an enhanced short-term
14 plan shall be the greater of twelve (12) consecutive months or the maxi-
15 mum duration described in 45 CFR 144 or its succeeding regulation;
16 (c) Shall provide that enhanced short-term plans may be eligible for
17 renewal for a maximum total duration that is the greater of thirty-six
18 (36) months or the maximum duration described in 45 CFR 144 or its suc-
19 ceeding regulation; and
20 (2) (d) May include, but need not be limited to:
21 (a) (i) A scope of covered benefits, which may be as broad as the
22 scope of covered benefits required to be included in individual
23 health benefit plans that are not deemed short-term, limited dura-
24 tion insurance under federal law; provided, however, the director
25 may not establish minimum standards for covered benefits in en-
26 hanced short-term plans;
27 (b) (ii) Restrictions on premium rate increases when an enhanced
28 short-term plan ceases to be renewable and the individual policy-
29 holder reapplies for coverage from the same carrier; and
30 (c) (iii) Conversion of enhanced short-term plans into fully re-
31 newable coverage upon a finding by the director that the conver-
32 sion complies with law and is in the best interests of the public.
33 (3) Enhanced short-term plans shall not be subject to the rating re-
34 quirements established in section 41-5206, Idaho Code.
