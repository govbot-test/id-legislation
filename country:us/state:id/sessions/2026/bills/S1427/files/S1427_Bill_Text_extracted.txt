1 SECTION 4. EXEMPTIONS FROM PROGRAM TRANSFER LIMITATIONS. The Forest
2 and Range Fire Protection Program is hereby exempted from the provisions
3 of Section 67-3511(2), Idaho Code, allowing unlimited transfers into the
4 Forest and Range Fire Protection Program from other programs within the De-
5 partment of Lands for all moneys appropriated to it for the period July 1,
6 2026, through June 30, 2027.
7 SECTION 5. LIMITATIONS FOR GENERAL FUND BONUSES. Notwithstanding any
8 other provision of law to the contrary, the use of General Fund appropria-
9 tions for bonuses shall be limited to firefighters with an incident qualifi-
10 cation card including an operational qualification. The Department of Lands
11 shall provide a report to the Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee and the
12 Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the Legislative Services Office on
13 the award of bonuses for the 2026 fire season. The report shall be submitted
14 no later than January 5, 2027.
15 SECTION 6. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
16 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
17 strictions specified in this act.
