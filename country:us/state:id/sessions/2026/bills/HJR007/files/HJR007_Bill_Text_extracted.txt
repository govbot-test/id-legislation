LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. 7
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION
2 PROPOSING AN AMENDMENT TO THE CONSTITUTION OF THE STATE OF IDAHO RELATING TO
3 THE PROHIBITION OF SECTARIAN APPROPRIATIONS; REPEALING SECTION 5, AR-
4 TICLE IX OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE STATE OF IDAHO, RELATING TO THE PRO-
5 HIBITION OF SECTARIAN APPROPRIATIONS; STATING THE QUESTION TO BE SUB-
6 MITTED TO THE ELECTORATE; DIRECTING THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL TO PREPARE
7 THE STATEMENTS REQUIRED BY LAW; AND DIRECTING THE SECRETARY OF STATE TO
8 PUBLISH THE AMENDMENT AND ARGUMENTS AS REQUIRED BY LAW.
9 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
10 SECTION 1. That Section 5, Article IX of the Constitution of the State
11 of Idaho, be, and the same is hereby repealed.
12 SECTION 2. The question to be submitted to the electors of the State of
13 Idaho at the next general election shall be as follows:
14 "Shall Section 5, Article IX of the Constitution of the State of Idaho
15 prohibiting sectarian appropriations be repealed?"
16 SECTION 3. The Legislative Council is directed to prepare the state-
17 ments required by Section 67-453, Idaho Code, and file the same.
18 SECTION 4. The Secretary of State is hereby directed to publish this
19 proposed constitutional amendment and arguments as required by law.
