LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 22
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE, DECLARING 2026 TO BE THE YEAR OF VOL-
3 UNTEERISM AND THE AMERICA250 IN IDAHO SERVICE CHALLENGE, RECOGNIZING A
4 THEME SONG TO CELEBRATE AMERICA'S 250TH BIRTHDAY, AND ENCOURAGING ALL
5 IDAHOANS TO COMMEMORATE AMERICA'S 250TH BIRTHDAY BY SIGNING IDAHO'S
6 REPLICA OF THE DECLARATION OF INDEPENDENCE.
7 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, the Idaho Legislature has invited all Idahoans to promote an
9 awareness and understanding of the principles contained in the nation's
10 founding documents, including representative government, personal respon-
11 sibility, individual liberty, and equal and inalienable rights; and
12 WHEREAS, the Idaho Legislature has encouraged Idahoans to reflect on
13 American and state history and heritage, develop deeper connections within
14 their communities, and advance Idaho's contributions and values for the
15 benefit of our children and future generations; and
16 WHEREAS, Idaho is proudly joining the nation in celebrating America's
17 250th birthday by implementing America250 in Idaho, a statewide effort to
18 honor our nation's founding, engage families and students, and create last-
19 ing memories for future generations; and
20 WHEREAS, using Spuddy Buddy, the official mascot of the Idaho Potato
21 Commission, the Revolutionary Spud characters were created to honor his-
22 toric revolutionary leaders, and each was given a personality as well as
23 handcrafted costumes to make history fun, approachable, and unforgettable
24 for Idahoans of all ages; and
25 WHEREAS, the Revolutionary Spuds are official America250 in Idaho am-
26 bassadors and have traveled thousands of miles throughout the state visiting
27 community events and collecting signatures for the historic capitol rotunda
28 Declaration of Independence; and
29 WHEREAS, thousands of Idahoans have already signed Idaho's replica of
30 the Declaration of Independence, which hangs in the capitol rotunda; and
31 WHEREAS, "Forever Idaho" is the official theme song for America250 in
32 Idaho ambassadors and the Revolutionary Spuds and was created as a gift for
33 all Idahoans; and
34 WHEREAS, the song "Forever Idaho" celebrates Idaho's breathtaking
35 landscapes, resilient communities, and shared love of home as we come to-
36 gether to honor America's 250th birthday and beyond; and
37 WHEREAS, the America250 in Idaho ambassadors are setting a bold vision
38 and challenging Idahoans to collectively perform 250,000 acts of ser-
39 vice from January until July 4, 2026, inviting every generation, from our
40 youngest students to our wisest seniors, to join hands in this shared en-
41 deavor and fill our communities with hope and kindness.
42 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular Ses-
43 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and

2
1 the Senate concurring therein, that the Idaho Legislature declares 2026 to
2 be the Year of Volunteerism and the America250 in Idaho Service Challenge.
3 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of the Legislature recognize
4 "Forever Idaho" as the theme song of the America250 in Idaho ambassadors and
5 the Revolutionary Spuds and invite all Idahoans to include this song in their
6 celebrations throughout 2026 and beyond.
7 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of the Legislature encourage all
8 Idahoans to commemorate America's 250th birthday by signing Idaho's replica
9 of the Declaration of Independence, so that their names will be included in
10 this powerful display of American patriotism.
