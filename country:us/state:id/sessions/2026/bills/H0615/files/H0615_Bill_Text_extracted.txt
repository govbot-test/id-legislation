LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 615
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO DISTURBING THE PEACE; AMENDING SECTION 18-6409, IDAHO CODE,
3 TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING DISTURBING THE PEACE; AND DECLARING AN
4 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 18-6409, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 18-6409. DISTURBING THE PEACE. (1) Every person who maliciously and
9 willfully disturbs the peace or quiet of any religious gathering, house of
10 worship, neighborhood, family, or person, by loud or unusual noise, or by
11 tumultuous or offensive conduct, or by threatening, traducing, quarreling,
12 challenging to fight or fighting, or fires by firing any gun or pistol, or
13 uses any vulgar, profane or indecent language within the presence or hearing
14 of children, in a loud and boisterous manner, is guilty of a misdemeanor.
15 (2) Every person who maliciously and willfully disturbs the dignity or
16 reverential nature of any funeral, memorial service, funeral procession,
17 burial ceremony or viewing of a deceased person is guilty of a misdemeanor.
18 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
20 July 1, 2026.
