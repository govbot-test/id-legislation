LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 731
BY REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING CHAPTER 1, TITLE 33, IDAHO CODE, BY THE AD-
3 DITION OF A NEW SECTION 33-144, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS RE-
4 GARDING PARTICIPATION IN THE FEDERAL TAX CREDIT SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM;
5 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 1, Title 33, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 33-144, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 33-144. PARTICIPATION IN FEDERAL TAX CREDIT SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM. (1)
11 The state of Idaho hereby elects to participate in the federal tax credit
12 scholarship program established under section 70411, P.L. 119-21.
13 (2) The state department of education shall:
14 (a) No later than the first day of January of each year, submit to the
15 United States secretary of the treasury and publish on the department's
16 website a list of qualified scholarship granting organizations that
17 meet the requirements under section 70411, P.L. 119-21, and are located
18 in this state;
19 (b) Certify to the United States secretary of the treasury the state de-
20 partment of education's authority to submit the list on behalf of the
21 state;
22 (c) Accept submissions from scholarship granting organizations
23 throughout the year for inclusion on the list submitted pursuant to
24 paragraph (a) of this subsection; and
25 (d) Comply with any other requirement under section 70411, P.L. 119-
26 21, including any applicable federal regulations or guidance, to ensure
27 that the state is eligible to participate in taxable years beginning in
28 tax year 2027.
29 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
30 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
31 July 1, 2026.
