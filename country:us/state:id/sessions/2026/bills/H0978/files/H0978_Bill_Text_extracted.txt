6 SECTION 5. REQUIRING A REPORT FOR THE SUICIDE PREVENTION AND AWARENESS
7 PROGRAM. The Department of Health and Welfare shall submit a report to the
8 Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee by December 31, 2026, identifying
9 outcomes and return on investment for moneys appropriated for the Suicide
10 Prevention and Awareness Program. The format and content of this report will
11 be determined in consultation with the Budget and Policy Analysis Division
12 of the Legislative Services Office.
13 SECTION 6. REQUIRING A REPORT FOR THE HIV PREVENTION AND SURVEILLANCE
14 PROGRAM. The Department of Health and Welfare and the public health dis-
15 tricts shall submit a report to the Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee
16 by December 31, 2026, identifying outcomes and return on investment for
17 moneys appropriated for the HIV Prevention and Surveillance Program. The
18 format and content of this report will be determined in consultation with the
19 Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the Legislative Services Office.
20 SECTION 7. REQUIRING A REPORT FOR THE HEPATITIS PREVENTION AND SURVEIL-
21 LANCE PROGRAM. The Department of Health and Welfare shall submit a report to
22 the Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee by December 31, 2026, identify-
23 ing outcomes and return on investment for moneys appropriated for the Hep-
24 atitis Prevention and Surveillance Program. The format and content of this
25 report will be determined in consultation with the Budget and Policy Analy-
26 sis Division of the Legislative Services Office.
27 SECTION 8. REQUIRING A REPORT FOR THE IMMUNIZATION ASSESSMENT FUND. The
28 Department of Health and Welfare shall submit a report to the Joint Finance-
29 Appropriations Committee by December 31, 2026, identifying the vaccine uti-
30 lization rate and a cost-savings analysis for the State of Idaho and ser-
31 vice providers regarding vaccines purchased using the Immunization Assess-
32 ment Fund. The format and content of this report will be determined in con-
33 sultation with the Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the Legislative
34 Services Office.
35 SECTION 9. REQUIRING A REPORT REGARDING IMMIGRATION STATUS FOR THE HIV
36 PREVENTION AND SURVEILLANCE PROGRAM. The Department of Health and Welfare
37 shall compile a report from the public health districts to submit to the
38 Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee by December 31, 2026, on the docu-
39 mented immigration status of individuals utilizing HIV prevention services
40 through the HIV Prevention and Surveillance Program. The format and content
41 of this report will be determined in consultation with the Budget and Policy
42 Analysis Division of the Legislative Services Office.
