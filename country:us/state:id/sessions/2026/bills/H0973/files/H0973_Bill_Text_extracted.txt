LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 973
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE MILITARY DIVISION; APPROPRIATING ADDI-
3 TIONAL MONEYS TO THE MILITARY DIVISION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AND DECLAR-
4 ING AN EMERGENCY.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. In addition to the appropriation made in Section 1, Chapter
7 239, Laws of 2025, and any other appropriation provided by law, there is
8 hereby appropriated to the Military Division for the Federal/State Agree-
9 ments Program $6,500,000 from the Federal Grant Fund to be expended for
10 operating expenditures for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026,
11 for the purpose of replacing climate control systems in Air National Guard
12 buildings at Gowen Field.
13 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
15 passage and approval.
