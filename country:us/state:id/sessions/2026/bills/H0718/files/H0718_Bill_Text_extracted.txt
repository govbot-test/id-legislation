LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 718
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE FREEDOM OF CHOICE OF DENTURES ACT; AMENDING SECTION 54-3312,
3 IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING FEES; AND DECLARING AN EMER-
4 GENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 54-3312, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 54-3312. FEES. The board shall be entitled to charge and collect the
9 following fees subject to adjustment as prescribed by section 54-3309(c),
10 Idaho Code:
11 (a) An application fee (which shall include the cost of an examination
12 when required) of not to exceed three hundred dollars ($300) one thousand
13 dollars ($1,000);
14 (b) An initial license fee of not to exceed six hundred dollars ($600)
15 one thousand dollars ($1,000);
16 (c) A biennial renewal fee of not to exceed two thousand dollars
17 ($2,000) two thousand five hundred dollars ($2,500).
18 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
20 July 1, 2026.
