5 SECTION 2. That Chapter 55, Title 54, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
6 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
7 ignated as Section 54-5504A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
8 54-5504A. MEDICATIONS. A licensed midwife may obtain and administer
9 any medication indicated for maternal care or neonatal care defined in the
10 formulary and protocol for which the midwife possesses the requisite educa-
11 tion, training, and experience. Administration of such medication shall be
12 performed within the acceptable community standard of care.
