LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 20
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, CONDEMNING POLITICAL
3 VIOLENCE, HONORING THE LIVES, LEADERSHIP, AND LEGACIES OF VICTIMS OF
4 POLITICAL VIOLENCE, AND CALLING UPON ALL IDAHOANS, REGARDLESS OF BACK-
5 GROUND, PARTY AFFILIATION, OR CREED, TO REJECT ALL FORMS OF POLITICAL
6 VIOLENCE TODAY AND IN THE FUTURE.
7 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, the foundation of a free and civil society is the ability to en-
9 gage in open and respectful political discourse without fear of violence or
10 retribution; and
11 WHEREAS, increased political violence has occurred throughout the
12 United States; and
13 WHEREAS, the recent murder of political activist and leader Charles
14 "Charlie" James Kirk while speaking at a peaceful political event at Utah
15 Valley University in Orem, Utah, was unconscionable; and
16 WHEREAS, the recent murder of Minnesota State Representative Melissa
17 Hortman and her husband Mark Hortman in their home as part of a strategic plan
18 to kill elected officials in Minnesota was reprehensible; and
19 WHEREAS, our own state is not immune from such acts of violence; and
20 WHEREAS, in Idaho, law enforcement, first responders, public and civil
21 servants, and elected officials are subjects of targeted attacks by those
22 who wish them harm, thus eroding civility and creating a barrier between the
23 people and their government.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Repre-
25 sentatives, assembled in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth
26 Idaho Legislature, that the Idaho House of Representatives hereby condemns
27 all forms of political violence and commends and honors the dedicated law
28 enforcement and emergency personnel for their tireless efforts in finding
29 the suspects responsible and the courts involved for bringing swift legal
30 justice.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Idaho House of Representatives honors
32 the life, leadership, and legacy of the victims of political violence and
33 calls upon all Idahoans, regardless of background, party affiliation, or
34 creed, to reject all forms of political violence, to recommit to respectful
35 debate, to uphold American values, and to respect and honor one another as
36 fellow Americans today and in the future.
