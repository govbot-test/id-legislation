15 (c) Should be used only after consulting with a health care provider;
16 (d) Should be used only after consulting with a health care provider if
17 that consumer is or may be pregnant or breast-feeding;
18 (e) Should be kept out of reach from children;
19 (f) Is not for sale to persons under twenty-one (21) years of age;
20 (g) May be habit forming; and
21 (h) May interact with certain medications, drugs, and controlled sub-
22 stances.".
