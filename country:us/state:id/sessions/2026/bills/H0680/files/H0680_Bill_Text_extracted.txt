LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 680
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO INTERVIEWS OF ALLEGED CHILD ABUSE VICTIMS; AMENDING SECTION
3 16-1618, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE THAT ALL INTERVIEWS OF ALLEGED VICTIMS
4 OF CHILD ABUSE SHALL BE DOCUMENTED BY AUDIO OR VIDEO TAPING; AND DECLAR-
5 ING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 16-1618, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 16-1618. INVESTIGATIVE INTERVIEWS OF ALLEGED CHILD ABUSE VICTIMS. Un-
10 less otherwise demonstrated by good cause, all investigative or risk
11 assessment interviews of alleged victims of child abuse will, including
12 but not limited to safety assessments, investigative interviews, and risk
13 assessment interviews, shall be documented by audio or video taping whether
14 conducted by personnel of law enforcement entities, the department of health
15 and welfare or child advocacy centers. The absence of such audio or video
16 taping shall not limit the admissibility of such evidence in any related
17 court proceeding.
18 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
20 July 1, 2026.
