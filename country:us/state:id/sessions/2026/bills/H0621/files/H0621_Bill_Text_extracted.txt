LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 621
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CONCEALED WEAPONS; AMENDING SECTION 18-3302C, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING PROHIBITED CONDUCT AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL
4 CORRECTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
5 DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 18-3302C, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 18-3302C. PROHIBITED CONDUCT. (1) Any person obtaining a license un-
10 der the provisions of section 18-3302, Idaho Code, or carrying a concealed
11 deadly weapon pursuant to the provisions of section 18-3302(4)(f), Idaho
12 Code, shall not:
13 (1) (a) Carry a concealed weapon in the courtroom portions of a court-
14 house or in any area of a courthouse being used as a jail or holding fa-
15 cility for the detention of prisoners or any person undergoing trial or
16 awaiting sentencing; in a juvenile detention facility or jail; or in
17 a public or private school, except as provided in subsection (4)(g) of
18 section 18-3302D, Idaho Code; provided that this subsection shall not
19 apply to:
20 (a) (i) Peace officers while acting within the scope of their em-
21 ployment;
22 (b) (ii) Security personnel while actually engaged in their em-
23 ployment; or
24 (c) (iii) Any person who is authorized to carry a weapon by a per-
25 son, board, or other entity having authority over the building or
26 facility; or
27 (2) (b) Provide information on the application for a permit to carry a
28 concealed weapon knowing the same to be untrue.
29 (2) Any person violating the provisions of this section shall be guilty
30 of a misdemeanor.
31 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
32 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
33 July 1, 2026.
