1 AN ACT

36 SECTION 2. That Section 28-8-503, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
37 amended to read as follows:
38 28-8-503. PROPERTY INTEREST OF ENTITLEMENT HOLDER IN FINANCIAL ASSET
39 HELD BY SECURITIES INTERMEDIARY. (1) To the extent necessary for a secu-
40 rities intermediary to satisfy all security entitlements with respect to
41 a particular financial asset, all interests in that financial asset held
42 by the securities intermediary are held by the securities intermediary for
43 the entitlement holders, are not property of the securities intermediary,
44 and are not subject to claims of creditors of the securities intermediary,
45 except as otherwise provided in section 28-8-511.
46 (2) An entitlement holder's property interest with respect to a partic-
47 ular financial asset under subsection (1) of this section is a pro rata prop-
48 erty interest in all interests in that financial asset held by the securities
49 intermediary, without regard to the time the entitlement holder acquired the

3
1 security entitlement or the time the securities intermediary acquired the
2 interest in that financial asset.
3 (3) An entitlement holder's property interest with respect to a par-
4 ticular financial asset under subsection (1) of this section may be enforced
5 against the securities intermediary only by exercise of the entitlement
6 holder's rights under sections 28-8-505 through 28-8-508, Idaho Code.
7 (4) An entitlement holder's property interest with respect to a par-
8 ticular financial asset under subsection (1) of this section may be enforced
9 against a purchaser of the financial asset or interest therein only if:
10 (a) Insolvency proceedings have been initiated by or against the secu-
11 rities intermediary;
12 (b) The securities intermediary does not have sufficient interests in
13 the financial asset to satisfy the security entitlements of all of its
14 entitlement holders to that financial asset;
15 (c) The securities intermediary violated its obligations under section
16 28-8-504, Idaho Code, by transferring the financial asset or interest
17 therein to the purchaser; and
18 (d) The purchaser is not protected under subsection (5) of this sec-
19 tion.
20 The trustee or other liquidator, acting on behalf of all entitlement holders
21 having security entitlements with respect to a particular financial asset,
22 may recover the financial asset, or interest therein, from the purchaser. If
23 the trustee or other liquidator elects not to pursue that right, an entitle-
24 ment holder whose security entitlement remains unsatisfied has the right to
25 recover its interest in the financial asset from the purchaser.
26 (5) An action based on the entitlement holder's property interest with
27 respect to a particular financial asset under subsection (1) of this sec-
28 tion, whether framed in conversion, replevin, constructive trust, equitable
29 lien or other theory, may not be asserted against any purchaser of a finan-
30 cial asset or interest therein who gives value, obtains control, and does not
31 act in collusion with the securities intermediary in violating the securi-
32 ties intermediary's obligations under section 28-8-504, Idaho Code.
