LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 937
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO LOCAL LAND USE PLANNING; AMENDING CHAPTER 65, TITLE 67, IDAHO
3 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 67-6541, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH
4 PROVISIONS FOR THE REGULATION OF DEVELOPMENT ON RELIGIOUS LAND; AND
5 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 65, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 67-6541, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 67-6541. REGULATION OF DEVELOPMENT ON RELIGIOUS LAND. (1) As used in
11 this section:
12 (a) "Heavy industrial use" means a storage, processing, or manufactur-
13 ing use that:
14 (i) Uses flammable or explosive materials;
15 (ii) Presents hazardous conditions; or
16 (iii) Is noxious or offensive due to odors, smoke, noise, fumes, or
17 vibrations.
18 (b) "Mixed-use" means the use and development of a site consisting of
19 residential and nonresidential uses in which residential uses occupy at
20 least sixty-five percent (65%) of the total square footage of the devel-
21 opment.
22 (c) "Multifamily" means the use and development of a site for three (3)
23 or more dwelling units within one (1) or more buildings, including con-
24 dominiums.
25 (d) "Religious land" means land owned by a religious organization or
26 land leased by a religious organization for a term of no less than forty
27 (40) years.
28 (e) "Religious organization" means a nonprofit religious entity or-
29 ganized primarily for religious purposes, including those qualifying
30 under section 501(c)(3) or section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue
31 Code.
32 (2) This section shall not apply to any portion of religious land lo-
33 cated within one-quarter (1/4) mile of a heavy industrial use, an airport, or
34 a military base.
35 (3)(a) A city shall permit multifamily and mixed-use development as al-
36 lowable uses on religious land.
37 (b) A city shall not require a zoning change, variance, conditional use
38 permit, or other special approval in order to permit such uses on reli-
39 gious land.
40 (4) For multifamily or mixed-use development on religious land, a city
41 shall not:

2
1 (a) Restrict building heights at or below three (3) stories but may re-
2 strict building heights above three (3) stories; or
3 (b) Require the conversion of an existing building to exceed the inter-
4 national building code standards otherwise applicable.
5 (5) Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit a city from
6 applying generally applicable:
7 (a) Sewer and water access requirements;
8 (b) Stormwater management requirements; or
9 (c) Building codes not otherwise restricted by this section.
10 (6)(a) A city shall approve an application for a multifamily or mixed-
11 use development on religious land if it complies with applicable regu-
12 lations other than those prohibited in subsection (4) of this section.
13 (b) Approval under this section shall be ministerial.
14 (7) Nothing in this section shall be construed to:
15 (a) Allow for the building of homeless shelters;
16 (b) Direct any entity to approve a permit for a homeless shelter; or
17 (c) Infringe on any jurisdiction's ability to regulate or prohibit
18 homeless shelters.
19 (8) Housing developments on religious land provided for in this section
20 shall be subject to regular property tax laws and shall not be eligible for
21 property tax exemptions as provided in section 63-602B, Idaho Code.
22 (9) Nothing in this section shall prohibit a city from enacting regula-
23 tions, standards, or conditions that are less restrictive than the require-
24 ments of this section.
25 (10) The provisions of this section shall apply to any application for
26 development on religious land received by a city on and after July 1, 2026.
27 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
28 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
29 July 1, 2026.
