LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 931
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE PORTABLE BENEFIT PLAN ACT; AMENDING SECTION 44-2802, IDAHO
3 CODE, AS ADDED IN SECTION 1 OF HOUSE BILL NO. 645, IF ENACTED BY THE SEC-
4 OND REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE, TO REMOVE A
5 DEFINITION; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Sections 44-2802, Idaho Code, as added in Section 1 of
8 House Bill No. 645, if enacted by the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-
9 eighth Idaho Legislature, be, and the same is hereby amended to read as fol-
10 lows:
11 44-2802. DEFINITIONS. For the purposes of this chapter:
12 (1) "Bank" means a state bank, national bank, credit union, out-of-
13 state state-chartered bank, or foreign bank organized under the laws of a
14 territory of the United States, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Guam, Amer-
15 ican Samoa, the United States Virgin Islands, or Greenland with deposits
16 insured by the federal deposit insurance corporation.
17 (2) "Department" means the Idaho department of insurance.
18 (3) (2) "Hiring party" means a person or entity who hires or enters into
19 a contract with an independent contractor.
20 (4) (3) "Independent contractor" means any person who renders service
21 for a specified recompense for a specified result, under the right to control
22 or actual control of the independent contractor's principal as to the result
23 of the independent contractor's work only and not as to the means by which
24 such result is accomplished.
25 (5) (4) "Portable benefit account" means an account owned by an inde-
26 pendent contractor to fund the purchase of one (1) or more benefit plans.
27 (6) (5) "Portable benefit account provider" means the administrator of
28 a portable benefit account and includes:
29 (a) A bank;
30 (b) An investment management firm;
31 (c) A technology provider or program manager that offers services
32 through a bank or investment management firm; or
33 (d) Any other person who demonstrates to the satisfaction of the
34 department that the manner in which the person will administer the
35 portable benefit account will be consistent with the portable benefit
36 account requirements under this chapter.
37 (7) (6) "Portable benefit plan" means a benefit plan:
38 (a) Administered by a third party benefit plan provider chosen by an in-
39 dependent contractor and assigned to a beneficiary rather than to a hir-
40 ing party; and
41 (b) Includes but is not limited to:
42 (i) Disability insurance;

2
1 (ii) Health insurance;
2 (iii) Income replacement insurance;
3 (iv) Life insurance;
4 (v) Retirement benefits; or
5 (vi) Unemployment insurance.
6 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
7 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
8 July 1, 2026.
