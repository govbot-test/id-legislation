LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 892
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO BOARDS OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS; AMENDING SECTION 59-906A, IDAHO
3 CODE, TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING HOW VACANCIES ARE FILLED FOR A
4 BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS; AND
5 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 59-906A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 59-906A. BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS -- VACANCIES -- HOW FILLED. In
10 the event of a vacancy on a board of county commissioners, such vacancy shall
11 be filled as herein provided pursuant to the provisions of this section. The
12 county central committee of the same political party, if any, of the former
13 member whose seat is vacant shall submit, within fifteen (15) days, a list
14 of three (3) nominations to the governor. For counties with a population of
15 less than fifty thousand (50,000), the county central committee shall sub-
16 mit to the governor a list of one (1) nominee. For counties with a popula-
17 tion greater than fifty thousand (50,000) but less than one hundred thousand
18 (100,000), the county central committee shall submit to the governor a list
19 of two (2) nominees. For counties with a population greater than one hundred
20 thousand (100,000), the county central committee shall submit to the gover-
21 nor a list of three (3) nominees. The governor shall fill the vacancy by ap-
22 pointment from the list of three (3) nominations within fifteen (15) days.
23 If no appointment has been made within fifteen (15) days, the county central
24 committee shall designate one (1) of the three (3) nominees to fill the va-
25 cancy. The vacancy shall be so filled until the expiration of the term in
26 which the vacancy occurs. The county central committee of the same politi-
27 cal party, if any, of the former member, shall select a person who possesses
28 the constitutional qualifications to fill the vacant office to which he the
29 person is nominated, and who is affiliated with the same political party, if
30 any, as the former member whose seat is vacant. Upon failure of the committee
31 to make such selection before the expiration of the fifteen (15) day period,
32 the governor shall, within five (5) days, fill said vacancy by appointing a
33 person having the qualifications above set forth in the provisions of this
34 section.
35 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
36 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
37 July 1, 2026.
