28 SECTION 2. That Section 33-2139, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
29 amended to read as follows:
30 33-2139. STATE COMMUNITY COLLEGE ACCOUNT CREATED. There is hereby
31 created a state community college account in the state operating fund in the
32 state treasurer's office to which shall be credited all moneys that may be
33 transferred pursuant to section 23-404(1)(b)(iii)(ii), Idaho Code. The
34 state treasurer shall make such disbursements from the account as may be
35 ordered by the state board of education in accordance with the provisions of
36 this act.
