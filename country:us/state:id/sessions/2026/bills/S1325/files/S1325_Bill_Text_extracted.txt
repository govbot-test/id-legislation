34 SECTION 2. That Chapter 4, Title 49, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
35 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
36 ignated as Section 49-417G, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
37 49-417G. WHITE AND BLACK LICENSE PLATES. (1) On and after July 1, 2026,
38 any person who is the owner of a vehicle registered under the provisions of
39 section 49-402, Idaho Code, or registered under any other section of law for
40 which the purchase of special plates is allowed, may apply for and, upon de-
41 partment approval, receive special white and black license plates in lieu of
42 regular license plates.
43 (2) The provisions of this section shall not apply to any vehicle with
44 a registered maximum gross weight over twenty-six thousand (26,000) pounds.
45 Availability of the white and black license plates for other vehicles shall
46 be subject to the rules, policies, and procedures of the department.
47 (3) In addition to the regular registration fee required pursuant
48 to this chapter, the applicant shall be charged a fee of seventy dollars
49 ($70.00) for the initial issuance of plates and fifty dollars ($50.00) for

4
1 each succeeding annual registration. Five dollars ($5.00) of the initial
2 fee and five dollars ($5.00) of the renewal fee shall be deposited in the
3 state highway account and shall be used to fund the cost of administration
4 of this special license plate program. Sixty-five dollars ($65.00) of each
5 initial fee and forty-five dollars ($45.00) of each renewal fee shall be
6 transferred by the state treasurer to the Idaho law enforcement fund created
7 pursuant to section 67-2914, Idaho Code, and shall be used exclusively for
8 the purposes described in section 49-454(3), Idaho Code.
9 (4) Whenever title or interest in a vehicle registered under the pro-
10 visions of this section is transferred or assigned, the owner may transfer
11 the special plates to another vehicle upon payment of the required transfer
12 fees. The owner may only display the plates on another vehicle upon receipt
13 of the new registration from the department.
14 (5) Notwithstanding the provisions of section 49-402C, Idaho Code, the
15 white and black license plate shall have a white background and black charac-
16 ters. The design shall be approved by the department, utilizing a numbering
17 system as determined by the department. Initial costs of the plate program,
18 including costs of plate design, shall be paid by the Idaho law enforcement
19 fund.
20 (6) The white and black license plate established in this section shall
21 be exempt from the requirements of section 49-402D, Idaho Code.
22 (7) Sample white and black license plates may be purchased for a fee of
23 thirty dollars ($30.00), five dollars ($5.00) of which shall be deposited in
24 the state highway account and twenty-five dollars ($25.00) of which shall be
25 transferred to the Idaho law enforcement fund and shall be used exclusively
26 for the purposes described in section 49-454(3), Idaho Code. No additional
27 fee shall be charged for personalizing sample plates.
28 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
