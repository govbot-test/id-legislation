LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1350
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CASH ROUNDING; AMENDING CHAPTER 36, TITLE 63, IDAHO CODE, BY
3 THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 63-3643, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR CASH
4 ROUNDING; AMENDING SECTION 67-2361, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION
5 REGARDING CASH PAYMENT BY STATE AGENCIES; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY
6 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 36, Title 63, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 63-3643, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 63-3643. CASH ROUNDING. (1) If a seller is unable to settle a cash
12 transaction to the whole cent using denominations of United States coin or
13 currency on hand, the seller may round either the total amount due for the
14 transaction, or the amount of change due back to the purchaser, to the near-
15 est multiple of five cents (5¢) as follows:
16 (a) If the final digit of such amount is 1, 2, 6, or 7, the amount shall
17 be rounded down to the nearest multiple of five cents (5¢); and
18 (b) If the final digit of such amount is 3, 4, 8, or 9, the amount shall
19 be rounded up to the nearest multiple of five cents (5¢).
20 (2) The provisions of this section shall also apply to any cash refunds
21 of purchases.
22 (3) For the purposes of this section, "total amount due" means the sum
23 of the total sales price plus any applicable taxes, fees, or other charges
24 calculated to the nearest cent. Taxes shall be computed based on the total
25 sales price before any rounding occurs under this subsection.
26 SECTION 2. That Section 67-2361, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
27 amended to read as follows:
28 67-2361. CASH PAYMENT -- STATE AGENCIES. (1) Any official, depart-
29 ment, board, commission, or agency of the state of Idaho that accepts payment
30 for transactions shall accept cash as a method of payment along with any
31 other methods of payment the official, department, board, commission, or
32 agency may accept.
33 (2) No official, department, board, commission, or agency of the state
34 of Idaho may charge any additional fee for cash payment. Any amounts charged
35 or withheld due to cash rounding pursuant to section 63-3643, Idaho Code,
36 shall not constitute a fee under this section.
37 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
38 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
39 July 1, 2026.
