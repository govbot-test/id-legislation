18 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
19 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the Legislature to
20 provide for greater efficiencies between the Department of Finance and the
21 Department of Insurance. To this end, the Legislature hereby establishes
22 that, on and after July 1, 2027, the Director of the Department of Insurance
23 shall concurrently serve as the director of the Department of Finance. The
24 director shall find and implement cross-departmental efficiencies between
25 the Department of Insurance and the Department of Finance in areas that in-
26 clude but are not limited to administrative services, financial and account-
27 ing support, and information technology.
28 SECTION 2. That Section 67-2701, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
29 repealed.
