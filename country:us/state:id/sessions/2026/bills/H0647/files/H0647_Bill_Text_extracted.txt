19 SECTION 2. That Chapter 6, Title 55, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
20 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
21 ignated as Section 55-619, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
22 55-619. MANUFACTURED HOMES -- RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS PROHIBITED. (1)
23 On and after July 1, 2026, no restrictive covenant shall be entered into that
24 prohibits or unreasonably restricts siting of manufactured homes, as de-
25 fined in section 39-4105, Idaho Code, on lots where single-family dwellings
26 are permitted. Any such covenant is hereby declared to be against public
27 policy and is void and unenforceable. Any person attempting to create or
28 enforce such a covenant shall be liable for any attorney's fees, court costs,
29 and any other damages incurred by the other party.
30 (2) This section shall not apply to restrictive covenants against manu-
31 factured homes on lots where single-family dwellings are permitted that are
32 entered into prior to July 1, 2026.
33 SECTION 3. That Section 67-6509A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby

43 (2) Manufactured homes on individual lots zoned for single-family res-
44 idential uses as provided in subsection (1) of this section shall be in ad-
