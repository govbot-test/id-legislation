25 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
26 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the Legislature that
27 on and after July 1, 2030, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC inspections no
28 longer be performed by the Division of Occupational and Professional Li-
29 censes. Instead, the Legislature finds that it would be advantageous and
30 more efficient for such inspections, if not being otherwise provided for by
31 local jurisdictions, to be performed by private inspectors who are certified
32 by the Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses.
33 This legislation provides for a transition period between October 1,
34 2026, and July 1, 2030. During the transition period, electrical, plumb-
35 ing, and HVAC inspections may be performed either by state inspectors or by
36 state-certified private inspectors.
37 It is further the intent of the Legislature that nothing in this act
38 shall be construed to alter or repeal any building, electrical, plumbing, or
39 HVAC code adopted by the state or any political subdivision. Any inspection
40 conducted pursuant to this act shall comply with the applicable code in ef-
41 fect at the time of inspection.
