24 SECTION 1. That Section 33-5502, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
25 amended to read as follows:
26 33-5502. CREATION -- LEGISLATIVE FINDINGS -- GOAL. (1) There is hereby
27 created the Idaho digital learning academy, a public school-choice learning
28 environment which that joins the best technology with the best instructional
29 practices. The Idaho digital learning academy as provided for in this chap-
30 ter, is not a single department of state government unto itself, nor is it a
31 part of any of the twenty (20) departments of state government authorized by
32 section 20, article IV, of the constitution of the state of Idaho, or of the
33 departments prescribed in section 67-2402, Idaho Code. It is legislative
34 intent that the Idaho digital learning academy operate and be recognized not
35 as a state agency or department, but as a governmental entity whose creation
36 has been authorized by the state, much in the manner as other single purpose
37 districts.
38 (2) The legislature finds that it is in the best public interest to cre-
39 ate the Idaho digital learning academy based on findings that indicate:
40 (a) Technology continues to impact all facets of life, including the
41 education of students of school age and adult learners the education of
42 students in this state;

2
1 (b) Systems for delivery of education are as diverse as the learners;
2 and
3 (c) Public school systems Idahoans are seeking high quality educa-
4 tional choices within the public system, and schools and other edu-
5 cation providers are aligning curriculum and assessment with state
6 achievement standards; and.
7 (d) The development of a comprehensive digital learning environment is
8 cost prohibitive for individual school districts.
9 (3) The goal of the digital learning academy is to provide choice, ac-
10 cessibility, flexibility, quality, and equity in curricular offerings for
11 students in this state.

35 for the Idaho digital learning academy shall be agreed upon and approved by
