LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 952
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE FUNDING OF THE OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY OF STATE; APPROPRIAT-
3 ING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR FISCAL
4 YEAR 2027; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
7 there is hereby appropriated to the Office of the Secretary of State the
8 following amounts to be expended according to the designated expense classes
9 from the General Fund for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027:
10 FOR:
11 Personnel Costs $20,000
12 Operating Expenditures 215,800
13 TOTAL $235,800
14 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
16 July 1, 2026.
