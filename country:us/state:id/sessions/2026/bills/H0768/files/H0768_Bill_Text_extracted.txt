25 SECTION 2. That Section 54-5215, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
26 amended to read as follows:
27 54-5215. AUTHORITY TO INVESTIGATE AND DISCIPLINE -- SUSPENSION OR
28 REVOCATION OF REGISTRATION. (1) The board may investigate any person engaged
29 in contracting within the state of Idaho or any person believed to have acted
30 as a contractor without being duly registered as required by this chapter.
31 Upon receipt of a written complaint from a person who claims to have been in-
32 jured or defrauded by such person, or upon information received by the board,
33 the board shall perform an investigation of the facts alleged against such
34 person. A contractor shall provide necessary documents and shall promptly
35 answer all reasonable investigative questions asked by the board. Upon
36 request, a contractor shall promptly make available to the board all of the
37 documents that a contractor is required to disclose to owners and purchasers
38 of residential real property pursuant to section 45-525, Idaho Code. Such
39 documents shall be made available at the contractor's cost and at the loca-
40 tion and in the manner requested by the board; provided, however, that the
41 board may not request any trade secrets as defined in section 74-107(1),
42 Idaho Code, or more than the minimum necessary amount of information needed
43 to complete its investigation. If the board investigation reveals that the
44 facts alleged or received are sufficient to proceed with a formal action, the
45 board may authorize the filing of an administrative complaint against such
46 person and may seek injunctive relief prohibiting such person from engaging
47 in construction.
48 (2) The board shall have the authority to issue informal letters of rep-
49 rimand, suspend, refuse to renew, or revoke a registration, impose a civil

17 SECTION 3. That Section 54-5216, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
18 amended to read as follows:
19 54-5216. REINSTATEMENT OF REGISTRATION AFTER DISCIPLINE. (1) The
20 board may reinstate a suspended or revoked registration upon a showing that:
21 (1) (a) The grounds for such suspension or revocation have been elimi-
22 nated;
23 (2) (b) Such a violation is not likely to reoccur in the future; and
24 (3) (c) The public interest is not jeopardized by reinstating the reg-
25 istration.
26 (2) An individual who is prohibited from registration pursuant to the
27 provisions of section 54-5215(3), Idaho Code, shall be entitled to submit
28 an application for new registration pursuant to the provisions of section
29 67-2614(10), Idaho Code.
30 SECTION 4. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
31 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
32 July 1, 2026.
