LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 120
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE, HONORING AND COMMEMORATING STATE POLICE K9
3 KIMBER FOR HER OUTSTANDING SERVICE, DEDICATION, AND UNWAVERING COMMIT-
4 MENT TO PROTECTING THE PEOPLE AND INSTITUTIONS OF THE STATE OF IDAHO.
5 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, under the dedicated leadership and guidance of her original
7 handler, State Police Trp. Rob Heise, K9 Kimber progressed from having no
8 formal training to achieving full certification in November 2022, demon-
9 strating exceptional aptitude, discipline, and commitment; and
10 WHEREAS, in early 2024, K9 Kimber transitioned to her new handler, State
11 Police Trp. Jayce Miller, continuing her service with professionalism, re-
12 liability, and steadfast devotion to duty; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout more than three years of service, K9 Kimber has
14 played a vital role in enhancing the safety and security of state employees,
15 members of the Idaho Legislature, and citizens of this great state; and
16 WHEREAS, K9 Kimber's loyalty, courage, and service reflect the highest
17 standards of law enforcement and exemplify the valued partnership between
18 handler and K9 officer.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
20 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
21 the Senate recognizes and commends K9 Kimber for her outstanding service,
22 dedication, and unwavering commitment to protecting the people and institu-
23 tions of the State of Idaho and extends sincere appreciation for her contin-
24 ued service.
