LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 662
BY AGRICULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO MILK TESTING; AMENDING SECTION 37-506, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE
3 PROVISIONS REGARDING THE METHOD OF TESTING MILK AND CREAM; AMENDING
4 SECTION 37-516, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE RULES
5 FOR ADMINISTRATION AND DEFINITIONS OF DIRECTOR AND DEPARTMENT; AND
6 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 37-506, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 37-506. METHOD OF TESTING MILK AND CREAM. (1) All milk and cream pur-
11 chased or sold in the state of Idaho at a price based upon on or determined
12 by the milkfat, protein, lactose, solids nonfat, or somatic cell counts
13 thereof, or by any other quality parameter or other analyte thereof, shall be
14 tested by methods approved by the director of the department of agriculture
15 of the state of Idaho.
16 (2) Samples must be taken from every shipment of milk and cream by a milk
17 hauler authorized by the department pursuant to chapter 3, title 37, Idaho
18 Code. Accurate thermometers must be provided at all times. Milk and cream
19 samples must be protected and in a tamper-proof place between thirty-three
20 (33) and forty-five (45) degrees Fahrenheit.
21 (3) Such samples may be examined and tested by the buyer, seller, or de-
22 partment of agriculture at any time. The department of agriculture is autho-
23 rized to conduct audits of a person's, corporation's, cooperative's, or com-
24 pany's payments for milk or cream to determine if such payments comply with
25 established requirements.
26 SECTION 2. That Section 37-516, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
27 amended to read as follows:
28 37-516. RULES FOR ADMINISTRATION -- "DEPARTMENT" AND "DIRECTOR" DE-
29 FINED. (1) As used in this chapter:
30 (a) "Department" means the Idaho state department of agriculture.
31 (b) "Director" means the director of the department or the director's
32 authorized designee.
33 (2) The director of the department of agriculture is empowered to pre-
34 scribe rules and regulations, subject to legislative approval, in the admin-
35 istration of this act chapter not inconsistent with its provisions. The term
36 "department," as used in this act, means the department of agriculture.
37 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
38 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
39 July 1, 2026.
