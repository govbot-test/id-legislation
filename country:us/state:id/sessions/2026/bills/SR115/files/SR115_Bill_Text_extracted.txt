BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE POLICE, IDAHO PUBLIC SAFETY COMMUNICATIONS
4 COMMISSION, IDAHO PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION, SECRETARY OF STATE,

14 munications Commission, Idaho Public Utilities Commission, Secretary of
15 State, Idaho Department of Administration, and Idaho State Lottery.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
17 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
18 all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Police, Idaho Pub-
19 lic Safety Communications Commission, Idaho Public Utilities Commission,
20 Secretary of State, Idaho Department of Administration, and Idaho State Lot-
