LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 684
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO COUNTY SHERIFFS; AMENDING CHAPTER 6, TITLE 20, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 20-629, IDAHO CODE, TO AUTHORIZE A SHERIFF'S
4 OFFICE TO SEEK REIMBURSEMENT FOR COSTS OF RECAPTURE; AND DECLARING AN
5 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 6, Title 20, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 20-629, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 20-629. COSTS FOR RECAPTURE. Whenever any Idaho county sheriff's of-
11 fice is required to expend money from its budget in order to travel to another
12 state to collect a prisoner, probationer, or parolee who has absconded from
13 the supervision of the Idaho department of correction, the sheriff, upon
14 completion of the collection of the prisoner, probationer, or parolee and
15 the placement of such person in the county jail, may seek reimbursement for
16 the cost of returning the person to Idaho from the agency from which the
17 person absconded as indicated in court records. Such reimbursement shall
18 be sought by submitting an invoice detailing the cost of such recapture,
19 including any mileage or travel costs, costs for personnel, overtime costs
20 associated with coverage for traveling personnel, or any other reasonable
21 costs associated with returning the absconding person to custody in Idaho.
22 The Idaho department of correction shall appropriate moneys annually for
23 expenses as provided in this section and shall, upon receiving the invoice
24 from a county sheriff's office, ensure reimbursement occurs within thirty
25 (30) days of receiving such invoice.
26 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
27 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
28 July 1, 2026.
