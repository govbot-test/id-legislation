LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 548
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO SAFE BOATING ACT; AMENDING SECTION 67-7028, IDAHO
3 CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING ENFORCEMENT; AND DECLARING AN
4 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 67-7028, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 67-7028. ENFORCEMENT. The sheriffs and deputy sheriffs of the respec-
9 tive counties shall be primarily responsible for the enforcement of this
10 chapter and in the exercise of their authority may, upon reasonable suspi-
11 cion that a violation has occurred, stop and board any vessel subject to law.
12 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
14 July 1, 2026.
