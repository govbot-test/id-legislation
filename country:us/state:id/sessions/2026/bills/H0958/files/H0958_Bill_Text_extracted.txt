LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 958
BY RUBEL
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CHILD CARE; AMENDING CHAPTER 2, TITLE 56, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 56-204C, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS
4 REGARDING INCOME ELIGIBILITY FOR THE IDAHO CHILD CARE PROGRAM; AND
5 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 2, Title 56, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 56-204C, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 56-204C. IDAHO CHILD CARE PROGRAM -- INCOME ELIGIBILITY. A family with
11 a countable income at or below one hundred seventy-five percent (175%) of the
12 federal poverty level at the time of application shall meet the income eli-
13 gibility requirements for child care assistance through the Idaho child care
14 program.
15 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
17 July 1, 2026.
