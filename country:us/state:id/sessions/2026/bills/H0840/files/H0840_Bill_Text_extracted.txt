LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 840
BY REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS; AMENDING SECTION 67-7201, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE
3 OBSOLETE PROVISIONS; AMENDING SECTION 63-2552A, IDAHO CODE, TO RE-
4 VISE PROVISIONS REGARDING ADDITIONAL TAXES IMPOSED; AMENDING SECTION
5 63-3029A, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE OBSOLETE LANGUAGE AND TO MAKE TECHNICAL
6 CORRECTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 67-7201, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 67-7201. COMMISSION CREATED -- APPOINTMENT OF MEMBERS. There is
11 hereby created the Idaho Commission on Hispanic Affairs, hereafter referred
12 to as the commission. The commission shall consist of nine (9) members,
13 two (2) to be appointed by the president pro tempore of the senate from the
14 members of the senate; two (2) to be appointed by the speaker of the house of
15 representatives from the members of the house; and five (5) public members
16 to be selected from the Hispanic community who reside in and represent the
17 various geographical areas of the state which contain a significant Hispanic
18 population. The five (5) public members shall be appointed by the governor.
19 Of the five (5) public members first to be appointed, two (2) shall be ap-
20 pointed for a term of one (1) year, two (2) shall be appointed for a term of
21 two (2) years and one (1) shall be appointed for a term of three (3) years.
22 The governor shall specify the term of each public member when making the
23 initial appointments. All initial appointments shall commence on July 1,
24 1987. All subsequent terms shall be for three (3) years. Vacancies shall be
25 filled in the same manner as the original appointments and for the balance of
26 the unexpired term.
27 SECTION 2. That Section 63-2552A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby

32 distribution of all tobacco products in this state at the rate of five per-

34 however, that the combined tax on a cigar imposed by this section and section

40 for sale in this state; or
