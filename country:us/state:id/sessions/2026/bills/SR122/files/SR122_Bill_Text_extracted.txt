LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 122
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE POLICE, THE DIVISION OF VETERANS SERVICES,
4 THE SEXUAL OFFENDER MANAGEMENT BOARD, AND THE OFFICE OF ADMINISTRATIVE
5 HEARINGS HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE SENATE JUDICIARY AND
6 RULES COMMITTEE, WITH AN EXCEPTION.
7 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
9 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
10 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
11 letin; and
12 WHEREAS, the Senate Judiciary and Rules Committee reviewed temporary
13 and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Police, the Division of Veter-
14 ans Services, the Sexual Offender Management Board, and the Office of Admin-
15 istrative Hearings.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
17 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
18 all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Police, the Di-
19 vision of Veterans Services, the Sexual Offender Management Board, and the
20 Office of Administrative Hearings pursuant to the Administrative Procedure
21 Act and submitted through the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator
22 to the Legislature for review during the 2026 legislative session have been
23 reviewed and approved by the Senate Judiciary and Rules Committee with the
24 exception of the following temporary rule that was not heard by the committee
25 and thus not approved: IDAPA 57.01.01, Sexual Offender Management Board,
26 Rules of the Sexual Offender Management Board, Docket No. 57-0101-2502.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate Judiciary and Rules Committee
28 held that the following pending rule docket become effective upon sine die
29 of the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature: IDAPA
30 57.01.01, Sexual Offender Management Board, Rules of the Sexual Offender
31 Management Board, Docket No. 57-0101-2501.
