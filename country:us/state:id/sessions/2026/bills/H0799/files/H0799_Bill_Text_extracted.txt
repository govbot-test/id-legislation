LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 799
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES; PROVIDING LEGISLATIVE INTENT; REPEALING
3 SECTION 23-103, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO PRIOR OFFENSES AND RIGHTS NOT
4 AFFECTED; REPEALING SECTION 23-215, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO PRICE LISTS
5 TO BE FURNISHED BY SELLERS; REPEALING SECTION 23-216, IDAHO CODE, RE-
6 LATING TO THE ATTORNEY OR AGENT OF SELLERS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY
7 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. IDAHO CODE CLEANUP -- LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent
10 of the Legislature to ensure that the state laws provided in Idaho Code are
11 streamlined, up-to-date, and essential for the citizens of Idaho, while best
12 serving the public health, safety, and welfare. In accordance with the Idaho
13 Code Cleanup Act, following comprehensive review, the Legislature has iden-
14 tified the provisions of this act as obsolete, outdated, or unnecessary.
15 SECTION 2. That Section 23-103, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
16 repealed.
17 SECTION 3. That Section 23-215, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
18 repealed.
19 SECTION 4. That Section 23-216, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
20 repealed.
21 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2026.
