LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 19
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, CONDEMNING POLITICAL VI-
3 OLENCE AND CRIMES AGAINST THE STATE THAT OCCURRED IN 2025, HONORING THE
4 LIVES, LEADERSHIP, AND LEGACIES OF THE PUBLIC AND CIVIL SERVANTS AND PO-
5 LITICAL ACTIVISTS WHO WERE KILLED OR INJURED, AND CALLING UPON ALL IDA-
6 HOANS, REGARDLESS OF BACKGROUND, PARTY AFFILIATION, OR CREED, TO REJECT
7 ALL FORMS OF SUCH VIOLENCE.
8 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
9 WHEREAS, the foundation of a free and civil society is the ability to en-
10 gage in open and respectful political discourse without fear of violence or
11 retribution; and
12 WHEREAS, reprehensible political violence and crimes against the state
13 occurred in 2025 throughout the United States and in the State of Idaho; and
14 WHEREAS, political activist and leader Charles "Charlie" James Kirk was
15 inexcusably and horribly murdered while speaking at a peaceful political
16 event at Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah; and
17 WHEREAS, Minnesota State Representative Melissa Hortman and her hus-
18 band Mark Hortman were stalked and ruthlessly murdered in their home as part
19 of a strategic plan to kill elected officials in Minnesota; and
20 WHEREAS, our own state is not immune from such acts of violence; and
21 WHEREAS, in 2025, Idaho law enforcement, first responders, public and
22 civil servants, and elected officials were the subject of targeted attacks
23 by those who wished harm upon them, thus eroding civility and creating a bar-
24 rier between the people and their government.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
26 tatives, assembled in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
27 Legislature, that the Idaho House of Representatives hereby condemns these
28 murders and the crimes targeting the state that occurred in 2025, commends
29 and honors the dedicated law enforcement and emergency personnel for their
30 tireless efforts in finding the suspects responsible for these horrific
31 acts, and urges the courts to bring swift legal justice for all involved.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Idaho House of Representatives honors
33 the life, leadership, and legacy of the public and civil servants and polit-
34 ical activists who were killed and calls upon all Idahoans, regardless of
35 background, party affiliation, or creed, to reject all forms of political
36 violence and crimes against the state, to recommit to respectful debate, to
37 uphold American values, and to respect and honor one another as fellow Ameri-
38 cans.
