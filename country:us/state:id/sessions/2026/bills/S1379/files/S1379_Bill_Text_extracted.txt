LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1379
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE PROJECT CHOICE PROGRAM FEE; AMENDING SECTION 49-454, IDAHO
3 CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING THE PROJECT CHOICE PROGRAM FEE;
4 PROVIDING SEVERABILITY; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EF-
5 FECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 49-454, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 49-454. PROJECT CHOICE FEE. (1) A project choice program fee of three
10 dollars ($3.00) eight dollars ($8.00) shall be collected in addition to each
11 registration fee assessed pursuant to section 49-402(1), (2) or (3), 49-411,
12 49-412 or 49-434(1), Idaho Code. Such fees shall be deposited to the Idaho
13 law enforcement fund established in section 67-2914, Idaho Code.
14 (2) The project choice program fee shall be collected and deposited
15 pursuant to subsection (1) of this section for registrations for calendar
16 year 2007 and thereafter.
17 (3) The project choice fee shall be used, subject to appropriation,
18 exclusively for the purposes of creating a career ladder within the Idaho
19 state police and to provide salaries to encourage the hiring and retention
20 of trained and qualified employees for Idaho state police positions. Idaho
21 state police personnel who have participated in and benefited from the use of
22 the project choice fee, as of July 1, 2010, shall continue to participate in
23 and benefit from the project choice fee while employed by the Idaho state po-
24 lice. Provided however, that beginning July 1, 2010, the use of the project
25 choice fee shall be restricted in the following manner: the project choice
26 fee shall be used exclusively for the purposes of creating a career ladder
27 for commissioned officers, dispatch personnel and forensic personnel within
28 the Idaho state police; and to provide salaries to encourage the hiring and
29 retention of trained and qualified commissioned officers, dispatch person-
30 nel and forensic personnel.
31 SECTION 2. SEVERABILITY. The provisions of this act are hereby declared
32 to be severable and if any provision of this act or the application of such
33 provision to any person or circumstance is declared invalid for any reason,
34 such declaration shall not affect the validity of the remaining portions of
35 this act.
36 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
37 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
38 July 1, 2026.
