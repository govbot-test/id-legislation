Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 591
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO INDIGENT ELIGIBILITY; REPEALING SECTION 31-3502, IDAHO CODE,
3 RELATING TO ELIGIBILITY FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE; AND DECLARING AN
4 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 31-3502, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 repealed.
8 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
10 July 1, 2026.
