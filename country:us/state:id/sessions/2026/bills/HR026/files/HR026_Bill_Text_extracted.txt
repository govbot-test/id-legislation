Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 26
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING THAT ALL
3 PENDING AND TEMPORARY RULES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY
4 AND THE DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL LICENSES HAVE BEEN
5 REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE HOUSE ENVIRONMENT, ENERGY, AND TECHNOLOGY
6 COMMITTEE, WITH EXCEPTIONS.
7 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
9 of the Legislature shall review any pending or temporary rule that is ger-
10 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
11 letin; and
12 WHEREAS, the House Environment, Energy, and Technology Committee re-
13 viewed pending and temporary rules adopted by the Department of Environmen-
14 tal Quality and the Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
16 tatives, assembled in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
17 Legislature, that all pending and temporary rules adopted by the Department
18 of Environmental Quality and the Division of Occupational and Profes-
19 sional Licenses, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted
20 through the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legisla-
21 ture for review during the 2026 legislative session have been reviewed and
22 approved by the House Environment, Energy, and Technology Committee, except
23 for the following, which were not approved:
24 (1) IDAPA 24.05.01, Division of Occupational and Professional Li-
25 censes, Rules of the Board of Drinking Water and Wastewater Profession-
26 als, Docket No. 24-0501-2501, Section 400., only; and
27 (2) IDAPA 58.01.11, Department of Environmental Quality, Ground Water
28 Quality Rules, Docket No. 58-0111-2501, Section 003. and Section 200.,
29 Subsection 01.a., only.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the following pending rule dockets become
31 effective upon sine die of the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth
32 Idaho Legislature: IDAPA 58.01.08, Department of Environmental Quality,
33 Rules for Public Drinking Water Systems, Docket No. 58-0108-2502.
