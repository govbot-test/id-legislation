LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 781
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO AGRICULTURAL PESTS; AMENDING SECTION 25-2601, IDAHO CODE, TO RE-
3 VISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE POWER OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS TO CONTROL
4 AGRICULTURAL PESTS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFEC-
5 TIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 25-2601, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 25-2601. CONTROL OF PESTS -- POWERS OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS. The
10 board of county commissioners of each and every county of this state are
11 all hereby granted full power and authority to declare any predatory ani-
12 mal, including coyote, that feeds upon, preys upon or destroys any poultry
13 or livestock of any kind upon any public or private lands within their re-
14 spective counties, or any rodent, jack-rabbit, gopher, ground squirrel,
15 cricket, locust, grasshopper and other insect pests or plant disease causing
16 organisms/agents or any other invertebrate organism that feeds, preys upon,
17 or destroys any livestock, natural grasses, or cultivated crops of any kind
18 upon any public or private lands within their respective counties, of the
19 following to be agricultural pests, and to take all steps that they may deem
20 necessary to control such pests.:
21 (1) Any predatory animal that feeds on, preys on, or destroys any poul-
22 try or livestock on any public or private lands within their respective coun-
23 ties;
24 (2) Any rodent, including but not limited to mice, gophers, ground
25 squirrels, jackrabbits, or rats, such as the Norway rat and roof rat, on any
26 public or private lands within their respective counties;
27 (3) Any insect, including but not limited to crickets, locusts, and
28 grasshoppers, or any other invertebrate organism that feeds on, preys on,
29 or destroys poultry or livestock operations, natural grasses, cultivated
30 crops of any kind, or the earth on any public or private lands within their
31 respective counties; and
32 (4) Any plant disease-causing organism or agent that feeds on, preys
33 on, or destroys poultry or livestock operations, natural grasses, culti-
34 vated crops of any kind, or the earth on any public or private lands within
35 their respective counties.
36 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
37 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
38 July 1, 2026.
