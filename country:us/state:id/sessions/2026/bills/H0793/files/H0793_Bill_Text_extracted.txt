LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 793
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO BEER; AMENDING SECTION 23-940, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR COL-
3 LECTIONS FROM THE BEER EXCISE TAX; AMENDING SECTION 23-1008, IDAHO
4 CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING DISTRIBUTION OF REVENUE AND TO
5 REMOVE OBSOLETE LANGUAGE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN
6 EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 23-940, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 23-940. ALCOHOL BEVERAGE CONTROL FUND. (1) There is hereby created in
11 the state treasury the alcohol beverage control fund. All moneys from li-
12 cense and transfer fees that are collected by the director pursuant to the
13 provisions of this chapter, as well as any collections from the beer excise
14 tax as provided in section 23-1008, Idaho Code, shall be paid over to the
15 state treasurer for deposit in the alcohol beverage control fund. Expendi-
16 tures of moneys in the fund shall be subject to legislative appropriation for
17 the use of the Idaho state police alcohol beverage control bureau in carrying
18 out the provisions of title 23, Idaho Code, and the rules promulgated by the
19 director thereunder. At the beginning of each fiscal year, those moneys in
20 the alcohol beverage control fund that exceed two hundred percent (200%) of
21 that fiscal year appropriation, as certified by the state treasurer, shall
22 be transferred to the general fund.
23 (2) All other moneys collected by the director pursuant to the provi-
24 sions of this chapter shall be paid over to the state treasurer for deposit in
25 the general fund.
26 SECTION 2. That Section 23-1008, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
27 amended to read as follows:
28 23-1008. TAX -- DISTRIBUTION -- RULES -- REPORTS. (1) A tax of four
29 dollars and sixty-five cents ($4.65) per barrel of thirty-one (31) gallons,
30 and a like rate for any other quantity or fraction thereof, is hereby levied
31 and imposed on each and every barrel of beer containing not more than five
32 percent (5%) of alcohol by volume sold for use within the state of Idaho.
33 A tax of thirteen dollars and ninety-five cents ($13.95) per barrel of
34 thirty-one (31) gallons, and a like rate for any other quantity or fraction
35 thereof, is hereby levied and imposed on each and every barrel of beer con-
36 taining more than five percent (5%) of alcohol by volume.
37 Any wholesaler who shall sell beer, upon which the tax herein imposed
38 has not been paid, and any person who shall purchase, receive, transport,
39 store or sell any beer upon which the tax herein imposed has not been paid
40 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor, and any beer so purchased, received, trans-

9 Beer and all barrels, kegs, cases, cartons or cans so forfeited to
10 the state of Idaho shall be sold by the commission at public auction to any
11 brewer, wholesaler, or retailer licensed under the provisions of this chap-
12 ter making the highest bid. Such sale shall be held at such place and time as
13 may be designated by the commission after reasonable notice thereof given
14 in such manner and for such time as the commission may by rule prescribe.
15 From the purchase price received upon such sale, the commission shall first
16 deduct an amount sufficient to pay the tax due on such beer and to pay all
17 costs incurred in connection with such sale. The commission shall deposit
18 the balance remaining with the state treasurer, who shall place the same in
19 the general fund of the state of Idaho, and it shall become a part thereof.
20 (2) The revenues received from the taxes, interest, penalties, or defi-
21 ciency payments imposed by this section shall be distributed as follows:
22 (a) An amount of money shall be distributed to the state refund account
23 sufficient to pay current refund claims. All refunds authorized by law
24 to be paid by the state tax commission shall be paid through the state
25 refund account and those moneys are continuously appropriated.
26 (b) For revenue deriving from beer containing not more than five per-
27 cent (5%) of alcohol by volume, the balance remaining after distribut-
28 ing the amount in paragraph (a) of this subsection shall be distributed

30 (i) Twenty percent (20%) shall be distributed to the substance
31 abuse treatment fund, which is created in section 23-408, Idaho
32 Code;
33 (ii) Thirty-three percent (33%) shall be distributed to the
34 permanent building fund Idaho state police, of which no less than
35 sixty percent (60%) shall be dedicated to the Idaho law enforce-
36 ment fund for use in the project choice program, as established in
37 section 49-454, Idaho Code, and no more than forty percent (40%) to
38 the alcohol beverage control fund; and
39 (iii) The remainder shall be distributed to the general fund.
40 (c) For revenue deriving from beer containing more than five percent
41 (5%) of alcohol by volume, the balance remaining after distributing the
42 amount in paragraph (a) of this subsection shall be distributed as fol-
43 lows:
44 (i) Twelve percent (12%) shall be distributed to the substance
45 abuse treatment fund created in section 23-408, Idaho Code;
46 (ii) In fiscal year 2022 only, one and one-half percent (1.5%)
47 shall be distributed to the Idaho hop grower's commission fund
48 created in section 22-3112, Idaho Code, and three and one-half
49 percent (3.5%) shall be distributed to the Idaho grape growers and
