LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1369
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PLACE OF TRIAL OF CIVIL ACTIONS; REPEALING SECTION 5-404, IDAHO
3 CODE, RELATING TO OTHER ACTIONS, VENUE, AND CERTAIN EXCEPTIONS; AMEND-
4 ING CHAPTER 4, TITLE 5, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION
5 5-404, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING OTHER ACTIONS,
6 VENUE, AND CERTAIN EXCEPTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING
7 AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Section 5-404, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
10 repealed.
11 SECTION 2. That Chapter 4, Title 5, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
12 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
13 ignated as Section 5-404, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
14 5-404. OTHER ACTIONS -- VENUE -- EXCEPTIONS. (1) Except as otherwise
15 provided by this chapter or other provisions of law, a plaintiff shall bring
16 an action in the county in which:
17 (a) The cause of action arises; or
18 (b) Any defendant resides at the commencement of the action.
19 (2) If none of the defendants reside in the state of Idaho at the time of
20 the cause of the action, or prior to the filing of the complaint, the action
21 may be brought in any county, which the plaintiff may designate in the com-
22 plaint.
23 (3) Actions against life or fire insurance companies may be filed in the
24 county where the death occurred or the loss was sustained.
25 (4) Actions against any corporation organized under the laws of the
26 state of Idaho shall be filed in any county where the defendant has its prin-
27 cipal place of business or in the county in which the cause of action arose.
28 (5) Nothing in this chapter shall limit the power of the court to change
29 the place of trial, as otherwise provided by law.
30 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
31 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
32 July 1, 2026.
