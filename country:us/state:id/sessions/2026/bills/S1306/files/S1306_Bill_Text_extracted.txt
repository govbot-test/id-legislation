9 amended to read as follows:
10 43-1104. HEARING ON PETITION -- ORDER OF EXCLUSION. (1) The board of
11 directors of the irrigation district may conduct its own investigation of
12 the facts alleged in the petition and, by resolution duly adopted, which may
13 address the allegations of several petitions, accept the facts as alleged
14 and determine that no hearing is required prior to granting the petition or
15 petitions for exclusion. If the allegations are not thus accepted such pe-
16 tition must be heard by the board of directors of such irrigation district
17 within one hundred fifty (150) days of filing of the petition. If no hearing
18 is held within one hundred fifty (150) days, the land described in the peti-
19 tion is excluded from the district. If a hearing is ordered, the petitioner
20 or petitioners must establish by competent evidence the allegations of the
21 petition, and the chairman or presiding member of the board is hereby empow-
22 ered to administer oaths for the purpose of the hearing. a petition. Within
23 one hundred fifty (150) days of receiving a petition, the board shall adopt a
24 resolution that addresses one (1) or more petitions and:
25 (a) Accepts the alleged facts and grants the petition or petitions as
26 requested; or
27 (b) Rejects or otherwise disputes the alleged facts and denies the pe-
28 tition or petitions as requested. In such cases, the resolution shall
29 be accompanied by the board's reasoned explanation for such decision,
30 and the resolution and explanation shall be physically mailed to the pe-
31 titioner.
32 (2)(a) If a petitioner disagrees with the board's decision, the peti-
33 tioner may request a hearing before the board to review the decision.
34 Such request must be made in writing by the petitioner within forty-five
35 (45) days of the date the board's decision was mailed to the petitioner.
36 The board's adopted resolution shall become a final decision after the
37 forty-five (45) day period has ended.
38 (b) If a hearing is requested, the board shall:
39 (i) Set a date for the hearing that is no later than sixty (60)
40 days from the date the board received the petitioner's written re-
41 quest; and
42 (ii) Notify the petitioner of the date and time of the hearing.
