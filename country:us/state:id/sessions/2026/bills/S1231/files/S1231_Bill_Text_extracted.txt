4 ING LICENSES ISSUED TO ESTABLISHED CATERERS; AMENDING SECTION 23-904,

7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 9, Title 23, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 23-903d, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 23-903d. LICENSES ISSUED TO ESTABLISHED CATERERS. (1) Upon registra-
12 tion with the director of the Idaho state police as an established caterer,
13 upon application to the director for an established caterer liquor license,
14 and upon meeting all licensing and regulatory requirements imposed by a
15 county or incorporated city, nothing in this chapter shall prohibit the is-
16 suance of an established caterer liquor license to the owner, operator, or
17 lessee of an established caterer for use at events catered by the established
18 caterer, subject to the provisions of this section. "Established caterer"
19 means a catering business whose business address is located at a publicly
20 accessible premises and that caters no fewer than fifty (50) events per year.
21 (2) An established caterer selling liquor pursuant to an established
22 caterer liquor license shall abide by the following:
23 (a) Liquor shall be dispensed and prepared for consumption by the es-
24 tablished caterer licensed pursuant to this section only in areas ap-
25 proved by the local licensing authority;
26 (b) All liquor sales shall cease at the time food services cease and
27 shall be conducted pursuant to the provisions of section 23-927, Idaho
28 Code. The local licensing authority may impose additional date and time
29 restrictions on liquor sales;
30 (c) The license shall be for use only at an event catered by the estab-
31 lished caterer; and
32 (d) No established caterer licensed pursuant to this section shall pro-
33 mote or operate the catering event as a bar and lounge.
34 (3) Established caterer liquor licenses shall not count toward the lim-
35 itation on the number of licenses issued according to population, as pro-
36 vided in section 23-903(1), Idaho Code.
37 (4) An established caterer liquor license may not be sold or leased and
38 shall not be transferable to any other caterer.
39 (5) The fees for licenses granted pursuant to this section shall be as
40 set forth in section 23-904(11), Idaho Code.
