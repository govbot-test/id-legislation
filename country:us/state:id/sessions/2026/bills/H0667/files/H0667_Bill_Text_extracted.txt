11 49-115. DEFINITIONS -- N. (1) "National network" means highways
12 available to vehicles authorized by the provisions of the federal surface
13 transportation assistance act of 1982, as amended, and listed in 23 CFR part
14 658, appendix A.

18 Code)

20 mercial driver's license" means a commercial learner's permit or a commer-
21 cial driver's license, respectively, issued by a state or other jurisdiction
22 under either of the following conditions:
23 (a) To an individual domiciled in a foreign country meeting the re-
24 quirements of 49 CFR 383.23(b)(1); or
25 (b) To an individual domiciled in another state meeting the require-
26 ments of 49 CFR 383.23(b)(2).
27 (5) (4) "Nonresident" means every person who is not a resident of this
28 state.
29 (6) (5) "Nonresident's operating privilege" means the privilege con-
30 ferred upon on a nonresident by the laws of this state pertaining to the oper-
31 ation by that person of a motor vehicle, or the use of a vehicle owned by that
32 person, in this state.
