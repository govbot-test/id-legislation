LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 498
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE LEGISLATURE; AMENDING CHAPTER 4, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 67-465A, IDAHO CODE, TO AUTHORIZE THE PRESI-
4 DENT PRO TEMPORE OF THE SENATE AND THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESEN-
5 TATIVES TO EMPLOY COUNSEL IN ADDITION TO THE ATTORNEY GENERAL TO PUR-
6 SUE ACTIONS AGAINST THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT ON BEHALF OF THE STATE; AND
7 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Chapter 4, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
10 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
11 ignated as Section 67-465A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
12 67-465A. EMPLOYMENT OF PRIVATE COUNSEL IN ACTIONS AGAINST THE FEDERAL
13 GOVERNMENT. When, in the judgment of the president pro tempore of the senate
14 and the speaker of the house of representatives, the interest of the state
15 requires counsel in addition to the attorney general to pursue an action
16 against the federal government on behalf of the state, the president pro
17 tempore of the senate and the speaker of the house of representatives may
18 jointly employ such counsel to pursue such actions.
19 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect thirty days af-
21 ter its passage and approval.
