LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 518
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO HUMAN TRAFFICKING; AMENDING SECTION 18-8605, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 PROVIDE THAT THE ATTORNEY GENERAL SHALL HAVE CERTAIN AUTHORITY; AND
4 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 18-8605, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 18-8605. AUTHORITY OF THE ATTORNEY GENERAL. (1) The attorney general
9 shall employ such attorneys, investigators, and other personnel as neces-
10 sary to carry out the following responsibilities using funds allocated to
11 the office of the attorney general for such purpose:
12 (1) (a) To issue, by January 1 of each even-numbered year, a report
13 outlining the status of the state's efforts to combat human trafficking
14 and recommending next steps one (1) or more bodies of state government
15 should take to eradicate human trafficking in Idaho;
16 (2) (b) To compile and maintain curriculum for the training of prosecu-
17 tors, law enforcement, victim advocates, and the public on identifying,
18 investigating, and prosecuting human trafficking; and
19 (3) (c) To assist, upon request, county prosecuting attorneys and other
20 law enforcement agencies in the investigation and prosecution of human
21 trafficking.
22 (2) Any state or local law enforcement agency may submit the findings
23 of a human trafficking investigation to the attorney general, who shall have
24 authority, in his sole discretion, to investigate and prosecute human traf-
25 ficking under this act.
26 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
27 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
28 passage and approval.
