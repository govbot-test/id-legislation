Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1309
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO JUVENILE CORRECTIONS; AMENDING CHAPTER 32, TITLE 31, IDAHO CODE,
3 BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 31-3201K, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PRO-
4 VISIONS REGARDING THE DISTRIBUTION OF PAYMENTS IN CASES UNDER THE JU-
5 VENILE CORRECTIONS ACT; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EF-
6 FECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 32, Title 31, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 31-3201K, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 31-3201K. DISTRIBUTION OF PAYMENTS IN CASES UNDER THE JUVENILE COR-
12 RECTIONS ACT. When ordered by the court to make one (1) of the following pay-
13 ments in a case filed under the juvenile corrections act, chapter 5, title
14 20, Idaho Code, the person so ordered shall make the payment to the clerk
15 of the court in which the judgment was entered. The judgment shall be sat-
16 isfied accordingly by entry in the electronic docket of the court, and the
17 clerk of the court shall remit daily all such payments to the county auditor,
18 who shall, at least monthly, distribute the payments received as required by
19 statute. The distributions shall first completely satisfy the amounts due
20 in the following order before distribution of payments for any other amounts
21 owed to the court, and any payment applied to a category as provided in this
22 section in which more than one (1) payment was ordered shall be distributed
23 in proportion to the relative amounts of such ordered payments:
24 (1) Restitution to or making whole any victim who suffers an economic
25 loss as a result of the juvenile offender's conduct paid pursuant to section
26 20-520(3), Idaho Code;
27 (2) Detention/probation training academy fee paid pursuant to section
28 20-520(1)(q), Idaho Code;
29 (3) Juvenile drug court or mental health court fee paid pursuant to sec-
30 tion 31-3201E, Idaho Code;
31 (4) Juvenile pretrial supervision fee paid pursuant to section
32 20-516A, Idaho Code;
33 (5) County juvenile probation supervision fee paid pursuant to section
34 20-520(1)(s), Idaho Code;
35 (6) Court-ordered tests of breath or bodily fluids pursuant to section
36 19-2608, Idaho Code;
37 (7) Community service fee paid pursuant to section 20-520(1)(r), Idaho
38 Code;
39 (8) Probationary contract violation assessment paid pursuant to sec-
40 tion 20-522, Idaho Code;
41 (9) Detention costs paid pursuant to section 20-524(2), Idaho Code;
