LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 855
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO FISH AND GAME; AMENDING SECTION 36-413, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE
3 PROVISIONS REGARDING LIFETIME LICENSE CERTIFICATES; AND DECLARING AN
4 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 36-413, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 36-413. LIFETIME LICENSE CERTIFICATE -- FEE. (a) The fish and game
9 commission shall issue rules and regulations to administer a lifetime li-
10 cense certificate system.
11 (b) A lifetime license certificate may be sold to any person who quali-
12 fies as a resident or is granted resident license privileges as provided in
13 subsection (s) of section 36-202, Idaho Code, if such person has been a resi-
14 dent of this state for at least five (5) years immediately preceding the date
15 of application for the lifetime license certificate. If the applicant is un-
16 der the age of five (5) years, a legal guardian of the child shall have been a
17 resident of this state for at least five (5) years immediately preceding the
18 date of application.
19 (c) A lifetime certificate may be obtained by a person one (1) day of age
20 through one (1) year of age possessing the qualifications therein described
21 upon payment of: twenty-five (25) times the fee prescribed for a combined
22 hunting and fishing license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a combined
23 hunting and fishing license certificate; twenty-five (25) times the fee pre-
24 scribed for a hunting license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a hunting
25 license certificate; twenty-five (25) times the fee prescribed for a fishing
26 license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a fishing license certificate;
27 or twenty-five (25) times the fee prescribed for a trapping license in sec-
28 tion 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a trapping license certificate.
29 (d) A lifetime certificate may be obtained by a person two (2) years of
30 age through fifty (50) years of age possessing the qualifications therein
31 described upon payment of: thirty-five (35) times the fee prescribed for a
32 combined hunting and fishing license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for
33 a combined hunting and fishing license certificate; thirty-five (35) times
34 the fee prescribed for a hunting license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code,
35 for a hunting license certificate; thirty-five (35) times the fee prescribed
36 for a fishing license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a fishing license
37 certificate; or thirty-five (35) times the fee prescribed for a trapping li-
38 cense in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a trapping license certificate.
39 (e) A lifetime certificate may be obtained by a person fifty-one (51)
40 years of age or older possessing the qualifications therein described upon
41 payment of: twenty (20) times the fee prescribed for a combined hunting and
42 fishing license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a combined hunting

2
1 and fishing license certificate; twenty (20) times the fee prescribed for
2 a hunting license in section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a hunting license
3 certificate; twenty (20) times the fee prescribed for a fishing license in
4 section 36-416(a), Idaho Code, for a fishing license certificate; or twenty
5 (20) times the fee prescribed for a trapping license in section 36-416(a),
6 Idaho Code, for a trapping license certificate.
7 (f) Holders of lifetime license certificates shall be subject to the
8 provisions of title 36, Idaho Code.
9 (g) The director shall promptly transmit to the state treasurer all
10 moneys received by him from the sale of lifetime license certificates and
11 the state treasurer shall deposit all such moneys in the fish and game trust
12 fund. All such moneys shall be expended at the direction of the commission
13 to carry out the purposes of the Idaho fish and game code or any law or regu-
14 lation promulgated for the protection of wildlife, and shall be used for no
15 other purpose.
16 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
18 July 1, 2026.
