LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 833
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING CHAPTER 16, TITLE 33, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 33-1639, IDAHO CODE, TO REQUIRE PUBLIC SCHOOLS
4 TO PROVIDE DAILY RECESS FOR STUDENTS IN KINDERGARTEN THROUGH GRADE FIVE
5 AND TO ENCOURAGE SCHOOLS TO PROVIDE UNSTRUCTURED ACTIVITY BREAKS FOR
6 STUDENTS IN GRADES SIX THROUGH EIGHT; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
7 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Chapter 16, Title 33, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
10 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
11 ignated as Section 33-1639, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
12 33-1639. DAILY RECESS AND UNSTRUCTURED ACTIVITY. (1) Each school dis-
13 trict, specially charted district, and public charter school shall provide
14 to students in kindergarten through grade 5 a period of daily recess out-
15 doors, guaranteeing every student time to move, play, and grow. At least one
16 (1) recess period shall occur daily and outdoors and shall be for not less
17 than twenty (20) minutes, weather and scheduling permitting. If weather
18 does not permit students to be outdoors, then daily recess shall be accom-
19 modated indoors, such as in a gymnasium. Daily recess provided pursuant to
20 this section shall not be withheld for any disciplinary purposes, unless the
21 cause for discipline occurred during a recess period.
22 (2) Each school district, specially charted district, and public char-
23 ter school is encouraged to provide to students in grades 6 through 8 daily
24 unstructured activity breaks.
25 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
26 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
27 July 1, 2026.
