21 SECTION 2. That Section 67-802, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
22 amended to read as follows:
23 67-802. OFFICE OF GOVERNOR -- DUTIES OF GOVERNOR. (1) The office of the
24 governor shall be composed of: the state liquor division, as provided by
25 chapter 2, title 23, Idaho Code; the military division, as provided by ti-
26 tle 46, Idaho Code; the division of financial management; and such other di-
27 visions and units as are established or assigned by law, or created through
28 administrative action of the governor.
29 (2) The governor shall appoint an administrator for each division, with
30 the advice and consent of the senate. Administrators shall serve at the
31 pleasure of the governor, and shall be exempt from the provisions of chapter
32 53, title 67, Idaho Code. Other subordinate staff necessary to accomplish a
33 division's mission shall be subject to the provisions of chapter 53, title
34 67, Idaho Code.
35 (3) When making an appointment or filling a vacancy that is subject to
36 the advice and consent of the senate, the governor shall make the appoint-
37 ment or fill the vacancy within ninety (90) days of when the term of such ap-
38 pointment ends or of when the position becomes vacant for any reason or by the
39 start of the next legislative session, whichever is sooner.
40 (4) The supreme executive power of the state is vested by section 5,
41 article IV, of the constitution of the state of Idaho, in the governor, who
42 is expressly charged with the duty of seeing that the laws are faithfully
43 executed. In order that he may exercise a portion of the authority so vested,
44 the governor is authorized and empowered to implement and exercise those
45 powers and perform those duties by issuing executive orders from time to time
46 which shall have the force and effect of law when issued in accordance with
47 this section and within the limits imposed by the constitution and laws of
48 this state. Such executive orders, when issued, shall be serially numbered
49 for each calendar year and may be referred to and cited by such numerical

7 SECTION 3. That Section 67-303, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 67-303. HOLDING OFFICE AFTER EXPIRATION OF TERM. Every Subject to the
10 provisions of section 67-802(3), Idaho Code, every officer elected or ap-
11 pointed for a fixed term shall hold office until his successor is elected
12 or appointed and qualified, unless the statute under which he is elected or
13 appointed expressly declares the contrary. This section shall not be con-
14 strued in any way to prevent the removal or suspension of such officer, dur-
15 ing or after his term, in cases provided by law.
