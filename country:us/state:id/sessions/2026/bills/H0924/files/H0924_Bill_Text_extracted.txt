LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 924
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE STATE LIQUOR DIVISION; APPROPRIATING
3 ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE STATE LIQUOR DIVISION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027; AND
4 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
7 there is hereby appropriated to the State Liquor Division the following
8 amounts to be expended according to the designated expense classes from the
9 Liquor Control Fund for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027:
10 FOR:
11 Operating Expenditures $498,300
12 Capital Outlay 1,174,700
13 TOTAL $1,673,000
14 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
16 July 1, 2026.
