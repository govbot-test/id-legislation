LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1318
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE AUDIT OF REFUGEE RESETTLEMENT; AMENDING CHAPTER 4, TITLE 67,
3 IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 67-467, IDAHO CODE, TO ES-
4 TABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING AN AUDIT OF THE RESETTLEMENT OF REFUGEES;
5 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 4, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 67-467, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 67-467. AUDIT OF REFUGEE RESETTLEMENT. (1) As used in this section:
11 (a) "Entity" means any corporation and foreign corporation; business
12 corporation and foreign business corporation; for-profit and nonprofit
13 unincorporated association; corporation sole; business trust, estate,
14 partnership, trust, and two (2) or more persons having a joint or com-
15 mon economic interest; and state, the United States, and foreign gov-
16 ernment.
17 (b) "Illegal alien" means a person who has overstayed the person's visa
18 or entered the United States without authorization irrespective of any
19 temporary status the person may have been granted by the department
20 of homeland security allowing the person to be present in the United
21 States.
22 (c) "Refugee" means any person who is outside any country of such per-
23 son's nationality or, in the case of a person having no nationality, is
24 outside any country in which such person last habitually resided, and
25 who is unable or unwilling to return to, and is unable or unwilling to
26 avail himself of the protection of, that country because of persecution
27 or a well-founded fear of persecution on account of race, religion, na-
28 tionality, membership in a particular social group, or political opin-
29 ion.
30 (d) "Resettlement services" means any service provided by an entity
31 that entered into a grant, contract, or cooperative agreement with the
32 United States department of state or other appropriate federal agency
33 to provide for the reception and initial placement of refugees in the
34 United States.
35 (2) Any entity incorporated under the laws of the state of Idaho pro-
36 viding resettlement services for refugees shall provide the legislature an
37 annual audit of such services in accordance with the provisions of sections
38 67-466 and 67-705, Idaho Code.
39 (3) Such audit shall detail the following:
40 (a) The total number of refugees served;
41 (b) The nationality and gender of refugees served;
42 (c) The language abilities of refugees served;

2
1 (d) The successful rate of participation in language remediation pro-
2 gramming for refugees served;
3 (e) The counties and municipalities where refugees served are housed;
4 (f) The housing units taken by refugees served; and
5 (g) The vital statistics, including the rates of communicable disease,
6 among refugees served.
7 (4) Any entity incorporated under the laws of the state of Idaho pro-
8 viding resettlement services for refugees is prohibited from aiding illegal
9 aliens or released aliens awaiting removal.
10 (5) Any entity providing refugee resettlement services that has pro-
11 vided assistance to illegal aliens shall account for the names and identi-
12 fying demographics of such aliens for the purposes of providing a catalog of
13 names that may be matched against federal immigration databases at the de-
14 partment of homeland security, department of health and human services, and
15 department of state.
16 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
18 July 1, 2026.
