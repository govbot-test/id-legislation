LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 114
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION AND THE DIVISION OF CAREER TECH-
4 NICAL EDUCATION HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE SENATE EDUCATION
5 COMMITTEE.
6 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
7 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
8 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
9 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
10 letin; and
11 WHEREAS, the Senate Education Committee reviewed temporary and pend-
12 ing rules adopted by the State Board of Education and the Division of Career
13 Technical Education.
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
15 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
16 all temporary and pending rules adopted by the State Board of Education and
17 the Division of Career Technical Education, pursuant to the Administrative
18 Procedure Act and submitted through the Office of the Administrative Rules
19 Coordinator to the Legislature for review during the 2026 legislative ses-
20 sion have been reviewed and approved by the Senate Education Committee.
