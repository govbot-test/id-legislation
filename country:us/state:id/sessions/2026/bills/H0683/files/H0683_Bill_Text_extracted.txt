9 18-8303. DEFINITIONS. As used in this chapter:
10 (1) "Aggravated offense" means any of the following crimes: 18-1506A
11 (ritualized abuse of a child); 18-1508 (lewd conduct); 18-4003(d) (murder
12 committed in the perpetration of rape); 18-4502 (first-degree kidnapping
13 committed for the purpose of rape, committing any lewd and lascivious act
14 upon any child under the age of sixteen years or for purposes of sexual grati-
15 fication or arousal); 18-4503 (second-degree kidnapping where the victim is
16 an unrelated minor child and the kidnapping is committed for the purpose of
17 rape, committing any lewd and lascivious act upon any child under the age of

19 (rape, but excluding section 18-6101(1) where the victim is at least twelve

21 18-6101(2) where the victim is sixteen (16) or seventeen (17) years of age

23 18-6604 (forcible penetration by use of a foreign object); chapter 86, title

30 section 18-8312, Idaho Code.

40 this state, as described in section 18-8314, Idaho Code.
41 (5) "Department" means the Idaho state police.

1 SECTION 2. That Section 18-8329, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
2 amended to read as follows:
3 18-8329. ADULT CRIMINAL SEX OFFENDERS -- PROHIBITED ACCESS TO SCHOOL
4 CHILDREN -- EXCEPTIONS. (1) If a person is currently registered or is re-
5 quired to register under the sex offender registration act as provided in
6 chapter 83, title 18, Idaho Code, it is a misdemeanor for such person to:
7 (a) Be upon or to remain on the premises of any school building or school
8 grounds in this state, upon the premises or grounds of any daycare, or
9 upon other properties posted with a notice that they are used by a school
10 or daycare, when the person has reason to believe children under the age
11 of eighteen (18) years are present and are involved in a school or day-
12 care activity, or when children are present within thirty (30) minutes
13 before or after a scheduled school or daycare activity.
14 (b) Knowingly loiter on a public way within five hundred (500) feet from
15 the property line of school or daycare grounds in this state, includ-
16 ing properties posted with a notice that they are used by a school or
17 daycare, when children under the age of eighteen (18) years are present
18 and are involved in a school or daycare activity, or when children are
19 present within thirty (30) minutes before or after a scheduled school or
20 daycare activity.
21 (c) Be in any conveyance owned or leased by a school or daycare to trans-
22 port students to or from school or daycare or a school- or daycare-re-
23 lated activity when children under the age of eighteen (18) years are
24 present in the conveyance.
25 (d) Reside within five hundred (500) feet of the property on which a
26 school or daycare is located, measured from the nearest point of the ex-
27 terior wall of the offender's dwelling unit to the school's or daycare's
28 property line, provided however, that this paragraph shall not apply
29 if such person's residence was established prior to July 1, 2006, for
30 a school, and prior to July 1, 2020, for a daycare in existence on that
31 date. This paragraph shall not apply to such person whose residence is
32 established prior to the establishment of a daycare within five hundred
33 (500) feet of his dwelling unit. The provisions of this paragraph shall
34 apply when the person resides at a residential or long-term care facil-
35 ity.
36 (e) For purposes of this chapter, "school" means any public or private
37 school. "Daycare" means any licensed daycare as defined in chapter 11,
38 title 39, Idaho Code.
39 The posted notices required in this subsection shall be at least one hundred
40 (100) square inches, shall make reference to section 18-8329, Idaho Code,
41 shall include the term "registered sex offender" and shall be placed at all
42 public entrances to the property.
43 (2) The provisions of subsection (1)(a) and (b) of this section shall
44 not apply when the person:
45 (a) Is a student in attendance at the school; or
46 (b) Is exercising his right to vote in public elections; or
47 (c) Is taking delivery of his mail through an official post office lo-
