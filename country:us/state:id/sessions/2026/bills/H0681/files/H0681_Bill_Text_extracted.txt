LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 681
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CRIMINAL PROCEDURE; AMENDING SECTION 19-401, IDAHO CODE, TO PRO-
3 VIDE THAT THERE SHALL BE NO STATUTE OF LIMITATIONS FOR CERTAIN FELONIES;
4 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 19-401, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 19-401. NO STATUTE OF LIMITATIONS FOR CERTAIN FELONIES. Notwithstand-
9 ing any other provision of law, there is no limitation of time within which a
10 prosecution for the following crimes must be commenced:
11 (1) Murder;
12 (2) Voluntary manslaughter;
13 (3) Rape pursuant to section 18-6101(3) through (10), Idaho Code;
14 (4) Sexual abuse of a child or, lewd conduct with a child, or aggravated
15 lewd conduct with a child as set forth in sections 18-1506 and, 18-1508,
16 18-1508B, and 18-1508C, Idaho Code; or
17 (5) Producing or making sexually exploitative material as set forth in
18 section 18-1507(2)(b), Idaho Code; or
19 (5) (6) An act of terrorism as set forth in sections 18-8102, 18-8103,
20 18-3322, 18-3323 and 18-3324, Idaho Code.
21 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2026.
