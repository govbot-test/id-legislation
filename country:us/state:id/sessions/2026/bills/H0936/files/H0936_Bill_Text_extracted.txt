Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 936
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PROPERTY; AMENDING SECTION 18-7102, IDAHO CODE, AS ADDED IN
3 SECTION 1 OF SENATE BILL NO. 1326, AS AMENDED, IF ENACTED BY THE SEC-
4 OND REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE, TO REVISE
5 A DEFINITION; AMENDING SECTION 18-7104, IDAHO CODE, AS ADDED IN SEC-
6 TION 1 OF SENATE BILL NO. 1326, AS AMENDED, IF ENACTED BY THE SECOND
7 REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE, TO REVISE PROVI-
8 SIONS REGARDING FEDERAL GOVERNMENT AGENTS AND LOCAL COORDINATION; AND
9 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
11 SECTION 1. That Sections 18-7102, Idaho Code, as added in Section 1 of
12 Senate Bill No. 1326, As Amended, if enacted by the Second Regular Session
13 of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, be, and the same is hereby amended to
14 read as follows:
15 18-7102. DEFINITIONS. As used in this chapter:
16 (1) "Exigent circumstances" means only those limited, judicially rec-
17 ognized exceptions to the warrant requirement under section 17, article I of
18 the constitution of the state of Idaho and the fourth amendment to the United
19 States constitution.
20 (2) "Government agent" means any employee or representative of the
21 state of Idaho, or any political subdivision thereof, or the federal govern-
22 ment, acting in an official capacity.
23 (3) "Private land not open to the public" or "private land" means any
24 privately owned real property, not including any place of habitation or the
25 associated curtilage, where a person knows or has reason to know that the
26 person's presence is not permitted pursuant to the provisions of section
27 18-7008(2)(a), Idaho Code.
28 (4) "Search warrant" or "warrant" has the same meaning as that term is
29 defined in section 19-4401, Idaho Code, and shall include an arrest warrant
30 as described in section 19-507, Idaho Code.
31 SECTION 2. That Sections 18-7104, Idaho Code, as added in Section 1 of
32 Senate Bill No. 1326, As Amended, if enacted by the Second Regular Session
33 of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, be, and the same is hereby amended to
34 read as follows:
35 18-7104. FEDERAL GOVERNMENT AGENTS AND LOCAL COORDINATION. Prior to
36 executing a search warrant on private land pursuant to section 18-7103,
37 Idaho Code, federal government agents shall should notify the county sher-
38 iff, unless exigent circumstances exist.
