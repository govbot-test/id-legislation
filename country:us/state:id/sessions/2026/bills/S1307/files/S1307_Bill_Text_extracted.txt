23 42-1210. INJURING DITCH OR HEADGATE -- TRIPLE DAMAGES. (1) No person
24 shall, without the consent of a water delivery organization, as that term is
25 defined in section 42-1301, Idaho Code, a lateral manager of a lateral water
26 users' association established pursuant to chapter 13, title 42, Idaho Code,
27 or a watermaster of a water district established pursuant to chapter 6, title
28 42, Idaho Code, wrongfully:
29 (a) Divert any water from the ditch or channel where it was placed,
30 caused to be, or left to run by the water delivery organization, a lat-
31 eral manager, a watermaster, or an authorized representative thereof;
32 (b) Shut or open any ditch, gate, pump, diversion structure, or dam with
33 intent to:
34 (i) Divert any water without a right to do so; or
35 (ii) Deprive any person of the use of the water during any part of
36 the time that such person is entitled to the use of such water;
37 (c) Cut or alter any ditch; or
38 (d) Break or destroy any gate, pump, diversion structure, or dam.
39 (2) A person who violates the provisions of this section shall be liable
40 in a civil action to the person injured at three (3) times the actual damage
41 sustained in consequence of any such wrongful act or acts.

5 SECTION 15. That Chapter 13, Title 42, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
6 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
7 ignated as Section 42-1302, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
8 42-1302. LATERAL WATER USERS' ASSOCIATION ORGANIZATION -- BOARD OF
9 DIRECTORS AND LATERAL MANAGER. (1) Water users who share a common lateral or
10 laterals shall be members of and shall constitute a water users' association
11 known as a lateral water users' association.
12 (2) The association shall meet annually between January 1 and the last
13 Monday in March of each year.
14 (a) The secretary-treasurer of the association shall call the annual
15 meeting by providing ten (10) days' written notice before the annual
16 meeting date.
17 (b) If for any reason the secretary-treasurer fails to call a meeting,
18 the annual meeting shall be held on the last Monday in March.
19 (3) At the annual meeting, the association shall:
20 (a) Elect a chair, vice chair, and a secretary-treasurer to serve as the
21 association's board of directors for one (1) year or until successors
22 are elected;
23 (b) Elect a lateral manager to manage the distribution of water; and
24 (c) Set the compensation of such elected officers.
25 (4) The association may:
26 (a) Adopt rules and regulations for the management of the lateral or
27 laterals and the delivery of water therefrom;
28 (b) By majority vote, if it is considered to be in the best interests of
29 the lateral water users' association, combine one (1) or more laterals
30 or abandon any lateral not in use; and
31 (c) Take any lawful action if doing so is in the best interests of the
32 association.
33 (5) Each water user in the association shall be entitled to cast one (1)
34 vote, in person, for each inch of water the water user is entitled to receive
35 from the lateral and a fractional vote equal to the fraction of an inch of
36 water the water user is entitled to receive. Corporations or other legally
37 recognized entities may designate a representative to cast the vote accord-
38 ing to this subsection.
