LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 119
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PEND-
3 ING RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE AND THE IDAHO HOP
4 GROWERS' COMMISSION HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE SENATE AGRI-
5 CULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE, WITH AN EXCEPTION.
6 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
7 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
8 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
9 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
10 letin; and
11 WHEREAS, the Senate Agricultural Affairs Committee reviewed temporary
12 and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Department of Agriculture and
13 the Idaho Hop Growers' Commission.
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
15 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
16 all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Department of
17 Agriculture and the Idaho Hop Growers' Commission, pursuant to the Adminis-
18 trative Procedure Act and submitted through the Office of the Administrative
19 Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for review during the 2026 legislative
20 session have been reviewed and approved by the Senate Agricultural Affairs
21 Committee with the exception of IDAPA 02.06.01, Department of Agricul-
22 ture, Rules Governing the Production and Distribution of Seed, Docket No.
23 02-0601-2501, Section 112., new subsections .01 through .05, only, which
24 were not approved.
