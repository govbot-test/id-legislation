11 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the Legislature, in
12 recognition of Idaho's long-standing relationship with the Idaho Heritage
13 Trust, to create a specific and limited exception to section 49-402C, Idaho
14 Code, which prohibits new special license plates or special license plate
15 programs for the benefit of any entity that is not the state of Idaho or an
16 agency of the state of Idaho. This legislation discontinues a plate fee that
17 was being paid to the Idaho Heritage Trust for the use of the red, white, and
18 blue copyrighted design described in section 49-443(1), Idaho Code. Here-
19 after, the Idaho Heritage Trust will receive funds from the newly created
20 America250 license plates.

1 SECTION 3. That Chapter 4, Title 49, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
2 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
3 ignated as Section 49-416A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
4 49-416A. AMERICA250 PLATES. (1) Notwithstanding the provisions of
5 section 49-402C, Idaho Code, on and after July 1, 2026, any person who is the
6 owner of a vehicle registered under the provisions of section 49-402, Idaho
7 Code, or registered under any other section of law for which the purchase
8 of special plates is allowed, may apply for and, upon department approval,
9 receive special America250 license plates in lieu of regular license plates.
10 (2) The provisions of this section shall not apply to any vehicle with
11 a registered maximum gross weight over twenty-six thousand (26,000) pounds.
12 Availability of America250 license plates for other vehicles shall be sub-
13 ject to the rules, policies, and procedures of the department.
14 (3) In addition to the regular registration fee, the applicant shall be
15 charged a fee of thirty-five dollars ($35.00) for the initial issuance of the
16 plates and twenty-five dollars ($25.00) upon each succeeding annual regis-
17 tration. Ten dollars ($10.00) of the initial fee and ten dollars ($10.00)
18 of the renewal fee shall be deposited in the state highway account and shall
19 be used to fund the cost of the administration of this special license plate
20 program. Twenty-five dollars ($25.00) of each initial fee and fifteen dol-
21 lars ($15.00) of each renewal fee shall be transferred by the state treasurer
22 to the Idaho heritage trust.
23 (4) Whenever title or interest in a vehicle registered under the pro-
24 visions of this section is transferred or assigned, the owner may transfer
25 the special plates to another vehicle upon payment of the required transfer
26 fees. The owner may only display the plates on another vehicle upon receipt
27 of the new registration from the department.
28 (5) The America250 license plate shall have a red, white, and blue back-
29 ground and display the text "America250" centered on the bottom. The de-
30 sign shall be acceptable to the Idaho heritage trust and shall be approved
31 by the department. Beginning January 1, 2027, the text "America250" shall no
32 longer be required. Initial costs of the plate program, including costs of
33 plate design, shall be paid by the Idaho heritage trust.
34 (6) Sample America250 license plates may be purchased for a fee of
35 thirty dollars ($30.00), ten dollars ($10.00) of which shall be deposited
36 in the state highway account and twenty dollars ($20.00) of which shall be
37 transferred to the Idaho heritage trust.

8 SECTION 5. That Section 67-7602B, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 67-7602B. FUNDING. The Idaho heritage trust shall receive funds col-
11 lected in section 49-450 pursuant to section 49-416A, Idaho Code, in the
12 amount of fifty cents (50¢) per plate for the use of the copyrighted design
13 provided in section 49-443(10), Idaho Code. The Idaho transportation de-
14 partment shall collect such funds and distribute them to the Idaho heritage
15 trust fund quarterly. The role of the heritage trust is to accept proposals
16 from the public requesting funds for heritage preservation projects. The
17 proposals are evaluated on established criteria, and if in the opinion of the
18 heritage trust they qualify, a grant may be awarded subject to the availabil-
19 ity of funds. The heritage trust shall insure that the following occurs in
20 respect to the management of funds:
21 (1) Funds earned from the use of the motor vehicle license plate design
22 shall be deposited directly into the trust fund where it will earn interest
23 that will be used for heritage preservation projects. Contributions from
24 private fund raising efforts may also be deposited to the trust fund.
25 (2) Only the interest earned from the trust fund shall be expended, and
26 the trust fund shall remain as a permanent endowment generating income in
27 perpetuity for heritage preservation.
28 (3) The Idaho heritage trust shall require project sponsors to match
29 the funds granted for each project, so that no more than half the monetary
30 support for any project shall come from the proceeds of the trust fund.
