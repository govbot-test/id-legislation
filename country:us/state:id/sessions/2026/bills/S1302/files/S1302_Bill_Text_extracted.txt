38 in a county with a population of more than two hundred thousand (200,000)

1 No precincts shall be divided. A highway district commissioner shall be a

4 each subdistrict. County commissioners, mayors and city council members
5 shall not be eligible to hold office as a countywide highway district com-
6 missioner. At the election held in 1998, commissioners representing subdis-
7 tricts two and five shall be elected for two (2) year terms and commissioners

9 terms. Thereafter, all commissioners shall be elected for four (4) year
10 terms. Any incumbent in office on the effective date of this act may complete

27 34-905A. NONPARTISAN BALLOTS FOR ELECTION OF HIGHWAY DISTRICT COM-

29 nonpartisan ballot for the election of highway district commissioners in
30 each highway district. The ballot shall designate the highway district
31 commissioners subdistrict, and the names of all candidates for that office
32 shall be listed thereon. The ballot shall also contain the words: "Vote for
33 One," followed by the names of the candidates for the office. For candidates
34 running for highway district commissioner of a single countywide highway
35 district office pursuant to section 40-1404A, Idaho Code, the ballot shall
36 list the party affiliation or status as unaffiliated for each candidate as
37 indicated on each candidate's declaration of candidacy. The candidate with
38 the most votes shall be declared the successful candidate.
