Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 540

1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE PUBLIC RECORDS ACT; AMENDING SECTION 74-105, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 PROVIDE THAT CERTAIN RECORDS OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUVENILE CORRECTIONS
