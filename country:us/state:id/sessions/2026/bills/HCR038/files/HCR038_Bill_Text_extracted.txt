LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 38
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND PROVIDING FOR THE ADDITION OF A NEW
3 JOINT RULE 24 OF THE JOINT RULES OF THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESEN-
4 TATIVES OF THE STATE OF IDAHO.
5 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, members of the Idaho Legislature are responsible for translat-
7 ing the public will into public policy for the state; and
8 WHEREAS, the state is divided into 35 legislative districts, each rep-
9 resented by one senator and two representatives; and
10 WHEREAS, many legislative districts are distant from the state capitol
11 in Boise, making it difficult for legislators from those districts to meet
12 with their constituents during a regular legislative session; and
13 WHEREAS, the House of Representatives and the Senate deem it necessary
14 and desirable to add a new Joint Rule 24 of the Joint Rules of the Senate and
15 the House of Representatives.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Second Regular Ses-
17 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and
18 the Senate concurring therein, that the Joint Rules of the Senate and the
19 House of Representatives shall be amended by the addition thereto of a new
20 Joint Rule 24 to read as follows:
21 JOINT RULE 24
22 Constituent Days During Regular Sessions. -- Weeks of a regular leg-
23 islative session shall be identified as odd and even, consecutively. Each
24 Friday during an even-numbered week shall be a constituent day. Neither
25 house of the Legislature shall transact business on a constituent day.
