LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 580
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO CRIMES AND PUNISHMENTS; REPEALING CHAPTER 69, TITLE 18, IDAHO
3 CODE, RELATING TO THE IDAHO ANTI-CAMCORDER PIRACY ACT; REPEALING CHAP-
4 TER 76, TITLE 18, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE TAPE PIRACY ACT; REPEALING
5 CHAPTER 77, TITLE 18, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE MOTION PICTURE FAIR
6 BIDDING ACT; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
7 DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Chapter 69, Title 18, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
10 hereby repealed.
11 SECTION 2. That Chapter 76, Title 18, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
12 hereby repealed.
13 SECTION 3. That Chapter 77, Title 18, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
14 hereby repealed.
15 SECTION 4. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
17 July 1, 2026.
