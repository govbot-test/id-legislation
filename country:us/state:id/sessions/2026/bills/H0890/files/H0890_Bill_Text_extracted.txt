LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 890
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO STATE GOVERNMENT; REPEALING SECTION 67-5709A, IDAHO CODE, RE-
3 LATING TO THE SALE, TRANSFER, OR DISPOSITION OF STATE ADMINISTRATIVE
4 FACILITIES; AMENDING SECTION 58-331, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE A CODE REF-
5 ERENCE; AMENDING SECTION 67-2024A, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE A PROVISION
6 REGARDING THE DISPOSAL OF STATE SURPLUS REAL PROPERTY; AND DECLARING AN
7 EMERGENCY.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Section 67-5709A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
10 repealed.
11 SECTION 2. That Section 58-331, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
12 amended to read as follows:
13 58-331. DESIGNATION OF SURPLUS REAL PROPERTY. Real property of the
14 state of Idaho, the use of which by any department, officer, board, com-
15 mission or other administrative agency of the state shall be terminated by
16 law, and real property in the custody and control of any such agency which
17 the agency shall declare to be no longer useful to or usable by it, shall be
18 deemed surplus, and, except as set forth in section 67-5709A, Idaho Code,
19 custody and control thereof shall thereupon be vested in and title be trans-
20 ferred to the state board of land commissioners, subject to disposition by
21 said board in accordance with the provisions of this act.
22 SECTION 3. That Section 67-2024A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
23 amended to read as follows:
24 67-2024A. AUTHORIZATION FOR DISPOSAL OF STATE SURPLUS PROPERTY. The
25 state board of examiners shall authorize:
26 (1) The disposal of state surplus real property classified as "state
27 administrative facilities" in accordance with section 67-5709A, Idaho Code,
28 excluding real property as set forth in section 58-331, Idaho Code; and
29 (2) The the disposal of state surplus personal property in accordance
30 with section 67-5732A, Idaho Code, and in compliance with the internal
31 management policies and procedures for state surplus personal property as
32 adopted by the board.
33 SECTION 4. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
34 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
35 passage and approval.
