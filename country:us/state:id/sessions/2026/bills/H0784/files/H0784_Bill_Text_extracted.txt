Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 784
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING CHAPTER 16, TITLE 33, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 33-1639, IDAHO CODE, TO REQUIRE PUBLIC SCHOOLS
4 TO PROVIDE DAILY RECESS FOR STUDENTS IN KINDERGARTEN THROUGH GRADE FIVE
5 AND TO ENCOURAGE SCHOOLS TO PROVIDE UNSTRUCTURED ACTIVITY BREAKS FOR
6 STUDENTS IN GRADES SIX THROUGH EIGHT; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
7 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Chapter 16, Title 33, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
10 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
11 ignated as Section 33-1639, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
12 33-1639. DAILY RECESS AND UNSTRUCTURED ACTIVITY. (1) Each school dis-
13 trict, specially charted district, and public charter school shall provide
14 to students in kindergarten through grade 5 a period of daily recess out-
15 doors, guaranteeing every student time to move, play, and grow. Such recess
16 period shall occur daily and outdoors and shall be for not less than twenty
17 (20) minutes, weather and scheduling permitting. If weather does not permit
18 students to be outdoors, then daily recess shall be accommodated indoors,
19 such as in a gymnasium. Daily recess provided pursuant to this section shall
20 not be withheld for any disciplinary purposes.
21 (2) Each school district, specially charted district, and public char-
22 ter school is encouraged to provide to students in grades 6 through 8 daily
23 unstructured activity breaks.
24 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
25 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
26 July 1, 2026.
