LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 637
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO GOLD AND SILVER COIN AND SPECIE; AMENDING SECTION 67-9803, IDAHO
3 CODE, AS ENACTED BY SECTION 2, CHAPTER 107, LAWS OF 2025, TO REDESIGNATE
4 THE SECTION AND TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING GOLD AND SILVER COIN AND
5 SPECIE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 67-9803, Idaho Code, as enacted by Section 2,
8 Chapter 107, Laws of 2025, be, and the same is hereby amended to read as fol-
9 lows:
10 67-9803 67-9903. GOLD AND SILVER COIN AND SPECIE. (1) To the full ex-
11 tent allowed by clause 1, section 10, article I of, and the tenth amendment
12 to, the constitution of the United States, gold and silver coin and specie
13 minted domestically foreign or domestic shall be legal tender in the state of
14 Idaho under the laws of this state. The state may also elect to use gold and
15 silver coin and specie in conducting its business.
16 (2) Unless expressly provided by statute or by contract, no person or
17 other entity may compel another person or other entity to tender or accept
18 gold or silver coin or specie unless agreed upon by the parties.
19 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
21 July 1, 2026.
