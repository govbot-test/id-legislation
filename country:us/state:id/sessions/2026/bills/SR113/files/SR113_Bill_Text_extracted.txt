LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 113
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE, CONDEMNING POLITICAL VIOLENCE, HONORING THE
3 LIVES, LEADERSHIP, AND LEGACIES OF VICTIMS OF POLITICAL VIOLENCE, AND
4 CALLING UPON ALL IDAHOANS, REGARDLESS OF BACKGROUND, PARTY AFFILIA-
5 TION, OR CREED, TO REJECT ALL FORMS OF POLITICAL VIOLENCE TODAY AND IN
6 THE FUTURE.
7 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, the foundation of a free and civil society is the ability to en-
9 gage in open and respectful political discourse without fear of violence or
10 retribution; and
11 WHEREAS, increased political violence has occurred throughout the
12 United States; and
13 WHEREAS, the recent murder of political activist and leader Charles
14 "Charlie" James Kirk while speaking at a peaceful political event at Utah
15 Valley University in Orem, Utah, was unconscionable; and
16 WHEREAS, the recent murder of Minnesota State Representative Melissa
17 Hortman and her husband Mark Hortman in their home as part of a strategic plan
18 to kill elected officials in Minnesota was reprehensible; and
19 WHEREAS, our own state is not immune from such acts of violence; and
20 WHEREAS, in Idaho, law enforcement, first responders, public and civil
21 servants, and elected officials are subjects of targeted attacks by those
22 who wish them harm, thus eroding civility and creating a barrier between the
23 people and their government.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
25 in the Second Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that
26 the Senate hereby condemns all forms of political violence and commends and
27 honors the dedicated law enforcement and emergency personnel for their tire-
28 less efforts in finding the suspects responsible and the courts involved for
29 bringing swift legal justice.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate honors the life, leadership, and
31 legacy of the victims of political violence and calls upon all Idahoans,
32 regardless of background, party affiliation, or creed, to reject all forms
33 of political violence, to recommit to respectful debate, to uphold American
34 values, and to respect and honor one another as fellow Americans today and in
35 the future.
