LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 875
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE STATE BUDGET; AMENDING CHAPTER 35, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY
3 THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 67-3512B, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR A CAL-
4 CULATED REDUCTION FROM THE GOVERNOR'S BUDGET DOCUMENT; AND DECLARING AN
5 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 35, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 67-3512B, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 67-3512B. CALCULATED REDUCTION FROM THE GOVERNOR'S BUDGET DOCU-
11 MENT. The legislative services office shall provide the joint finance-ap-
12 propriations committee with a calculated reduction from the governor's bud-
13 get document provided for in section 67-3506, Idaho Code. The calculated re-
14 duction shall include each of the changes for employee benefit costs, health
15 insurance costs, and employee compensation for all entities that receive an
16 appropriation by the legislature pursuant to 67-3514, Idaho Code, and that
17 are authorized to have fifty (50) or more full-time equivalent positions. A
18 reduction shall be calculated using a five (5) year average of actual person-
19 nel cost expenditures divided by total personnel costs originally appropri-
20 ated. If the entity has been in existence for less than five (5) years, the
21 reduction shall be an average of all years in existence. The joint committee
22 may include the calculated reductions as part of each entity's appropriation
23 bill. The calculated reduction shall be applied to all fund types.
24 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
25 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
26 July 1, 2026.
