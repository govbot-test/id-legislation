25 SECTION 1. IDAHO CODE CLEANUP -- LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent
26 of the Legislature to ensure that the state laws provided in Idaho Code are
27 streamlined, up-to-date, and essential for the citizens of Idaho, while best
28 serving the public health, safety, and welfare. In accordance with the Idaho
29 Code Cleanup Act, following comprehensive review, the Legislature has iden-
30 tified the provisions of this act as obsolete, outdated, or unnecessary.

29 33-3304. APPROPRIATION FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION AT IDAHO STATE
30 UNIVERSITY. Fifty percent (50%) of all the moneys that now are in or which
31 may hereafter accrue to the normal school income fund are hereby appropri-
32 ated and set apart for the support and maintenance of the department of edu-
33 cation at Idaho State University, the same to be available for such purpose
34 immediately upon their being credited to the fund. Should the legislature,
35 by adoption of a concurrent resolution in both houses, approve a memorandum
36 of understanding negotiated by the Idaho department of parks and recreation
37 between the city of Albion and other public or private agencies interested
38 in cooperative management of an Albion Normal School Field Institute within
39 an Albion State Normal School state park complex, the percentage share for
40 the department of education at Idaho State University shall be reduced from
41 fifty percent (50%) to forty-seven percent (47%). In the event that the mem-
42 orandum of understanding is not approved, section 33-3305, Idaho Code, shall
43 apply.
