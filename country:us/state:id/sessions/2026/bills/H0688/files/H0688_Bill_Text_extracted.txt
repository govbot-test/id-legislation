LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 688
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO MOTOR VEHICLES; REPEALING SECTION 49-967, IDAHO CODE, RELATING
3 TO AIR BAGS AND AIR BAG SYSTEMS; AMENDING CHAPTER 9, TITLE 49, IDAHO
4 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 49-967, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH
5 PROVISIONS REGARDING AIR BAGS AND AIR BAG SYSTEMS; AND DECLARING AN
6 EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 49-967, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 repealed.
10 SECTION 2. That Chapter 9, Title 49, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
11 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
12 ignated as Section 49-967, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
13 49-967. AIR BAGS AND AIR BAG SYSTEMS -- PROHIBITED ACTS -- PENALTY. (1)
14 It shall be unlawful for any person to knowingly or intentionally:
15 (a) Manufacture, import, distribute, offer for sale, sell, or lease,
16 or install or reinstall into any motor vehicle, a counterfeit automo-
17 bile supplemental restraint system, a nonfunctional air bag, or other
18 object that replaces an automobile supplemental restraining component
19 that fails to meet the federal motor vehicle safety standards as pro-
20 vided in 49 CFR 571.208, for the make, model, and year of the motor vehi-
21 cle;
22 (b) Offer for sale, sell, lease, or otherwise transfer ownership of
23 a motor vehicle with a counterfeit automobile supplemental restraint
24 system, a nonfunctional air bag, or other object that replaces an au-
25 tomobile supplemental restraining component that fails to meet the fed-
26 eral motor vehicle safety standards as provided in 49 CFR 571.208, for
27 the make, model, and year of the motor vehicle; or
28 (c) Install or reinstall any object, component, part, or device as
29 part of an automobile supplemental restraint system that causes a motor
30 vehicle's diagnostic system to fail to warn the motor vehicle operator
31 that an air bag is not installed, a nonfunctional air bag is installed,
32 or a counterfeit automobile supplemental restraint system component is
33 installed that does not comply with 49 CFR 571.208, for the make, model,
34 and year of the motor vehicle. A violation of this paragraph shall not
35 occur until the installation work is complete and one of the following
36 conditions occurs:
37 (i) The motor vehicle is returned to the operator; or
38 (ii) The title to the motor vehicle is transferred.
39 (2) Any person convicted of a violation of the provisions of this sec-
40 tion shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
41 (3) As used in this section:

2
1 (a) "Air bag" means an inflatable restraint device for occupants of a
2 motor vehicle that is part of an automobile supplemental restraint sys-
3 tem.
4 (b) "Automobile supplemental restraint system" means a passive in-
5 flatable crash protection system that a vehicle manufacturer designs
6 to protect occupants of a motor vehicle in conjunction with a seat belt
7 assembly, as defined in 49 CFR 571.209, that has one (1) or more air bags
8 and all components required to ensure that each air bag:
9 (i) Operates as designed in a motor vehicle crash; and
10 (ii) Meets federal motor vehicle safety standards for the spe-
11 cific make, model, and year of manufacture of the vehicle in which
12 the air bag is installed.
13 (c) "Counterfeit automobile supplemental restraint system component"
14 means a replacement component, including an air bag, that displays a
15 trademark that is identical to or substantially similar to the manufac-
16 turer's or supplier's trademark without authorization from a manufac-
17 turer or a person that supplies parts to the manufacturer.
18 (d)(i) "Nonfunctional air bag" means a replacement air bag that:
19 1. Was previously deployed or damaged;
20 2. Has a fault that a motor vehicle's diagnostic system de-
21 tects once the air bag is installed;
22 3. May not be sold or leased pursuant to 49 U.S.C. 30120(j);
23 or
24 4. Includes a counterfeit automobile supplemental re-
25 straint system component or other part or object that is
26 installed for the purpose of misleading a motor vehicle
27 owner or operator into believing that a functional air bag is
28 installed.
29 (ii) "Nonfunctional air bag" does not include a deployed air bag
30 that has not been repaired or an air bag that is installed in a mo-
31 tor vehicle when:
32 1. Such vehicle has been declared a total loss or is other-
33 wise a totaled vehicle;
34 2. The owner was issued a salvage title certificate in Idaho
35 or a similar title from another state; or
36 3. The air bag is visible in its deployed condition.
37 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
38 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
39 July 1, 2026.
