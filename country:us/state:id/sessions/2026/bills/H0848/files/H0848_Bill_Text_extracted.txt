1 SECTION 2. EXEMPTIONS FROM EXPENSE CLASS AND PROGRAM TRANSFER LIMI-
2 TATIONS. The Legislative Services Office and Office of Performance Evalua-
3 tions are hereby exempted from the provisions of Section 67-3511 (1), (2),
4 and (3), Idaho Code, allowing unlimited transfers between expense classes
5 and between programs, for all moneys appropriated to them for the period July
6 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027. Legislative appropriations shall not be
7 transferred from one fund to another fund unless expressly approved by the
8 Legislature.
9 SECTION 3. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR THE ARPA STATE FISCAL RECOV-
10 ERY FUND. There is hereby reappropriated to the Legislative Technology Pro-
11 gram any unexpended and unencumbered balances appropriated or reappropri-
12 ated to the Legislative Technology Program from the ARPA State Fiscal Recov-
13 ery Fund for fiscal year 2026, to be used for nonrecurring expenditures for
14 the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027. The Office of the State Con-
15 troller shall confirm the reappropriation amount, by fund, expense class,
16 and program, with the Legislative Services Office prior to processing the
17 reappropriation authorized herein.
18 SECTION 4. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
19 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
20 strictions specified in this act.
21 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2026.
