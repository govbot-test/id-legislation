11 SECTION 1. That Chapter 57, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
12 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
13 ignated as Section 67-5775A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
14 67-5775A. PRIVATE INSURANCE COVERAGE AND CLAIMS FOR STATE PROP-
15 ERTY. (1) In the event of damage to or loss of state property that results in
16 the state filing a formal claim with a private insurer, the following shall
17 be completed prior to the state's acceptance of any settlement offer or pay-
18 out for a property claim or damage exceeding one hundred thousand dollars
19 ($100,000):
20 (a) Procurement of law enforcement reports, surveillance footage, or
21 eyewitness statements confirming any third-party liability, if appli-
22 cable;
23 (b) Assessment of damages conducted by a state engineer, certified ap-
24 praiser, or independent adjuster with valuations based on prevailing
25 market replacement costs at the time of the loss, regardless of whether
26 the state utilizes internal inventory or state-contracted pricing to
27 effectuate the repair, including:
28 (i) All recoverable soft costs, including but not limited to ar-
29 chitectural and engineering fees, project management oversight,
30 specialized permit fees, and the cost of temporary relocation or
31 bypass measures required to maintain state services during the re-
32 pair period; and
33 (ii) All costs associated with ordinance or law requirements.
34 If the repair of state property triggers a mandatory upgrade to
35 meet current building, safety, or environmental codes, such costs
36 shall be documented and included as a mandatory component of the
37 claim.
38 (c) Review of the insurance policy limits and exclusions to ensure the
39 state is pursuing the maximum possible recovery across all possible
40 payout scenarios; and
41 (d) Verification that no other secondary insurance or bonds are avail-
42 able to cover the loss.
