LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 850
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO MEDICAID; AMENDING SECTION 31-3502, IDAHO CODE, TO REMOVE A CODE
3 REFERENCE; REPEALING SECTION 56-267, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO MEDICAID
4 ELIGIBILITY EXPANSION; REPEALING SECTION 56-2205, IDAHO CODE, RELATING
5 TO LEGISLATIVE APPROVAL AND MEDICAID EXPANSION LIMITS; AND PROVIDING AN
6 EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 31-3502, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 31-3502. ELIGIBILITY FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE. (1) Notwithstanding
11 any provision of law or rule to the contrary, no person eligible for medic-
12 aid pursuant to section 56-254 or 56-267, Idaho Code, shall be eligible for
13 financial assistance pursuant to this chapter as it existed on March 1, 2022.
14 (2) Notwithstanding any provision of law or rule to the contrary, no
15 person eligible for health insurance shall be eligible for financial assis-
16 tance pursuant to this chapter as it existed on March 1, 2022.
17 (3) Notwithstanding any provision of law or rule to the contrary, no
18 person in a commitment proceeding pursuant to chapter 3, title 66, Idaho
19 Code, who is eligible for medicaid or eligible for health insurance shall be
20 eligible for financial assistance pursuant to this chapter as it existed on
21 March 1, 2022.
22 (4) A board of county commissioners, the board of the catastrophic
23 health care cost program, or the department of health and welfare receiving
24 an application for assistance for services received through March 31, 2022,
25 shall process such application pursuant to this chapter as it existed on
26 March 1, 2022.
27 (5) It is the intent of the legislature that moneys saved by counties
28 pursuant to this section may be used for additional county aid to public
29 health districts as required by section 39-424A, Idaho Code.
30 SECTION 2. That Section 56-267, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
31 repealed.
32 SECTION 3. That Section 56-2205, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
33 repealed.
34 SECTION 4. This act shall be in full force and effect on and after Jan-
35 uary 1, 2028.
