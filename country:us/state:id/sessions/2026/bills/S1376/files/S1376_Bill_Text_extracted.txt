LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1376
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ELECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 34-1404, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE
3 PROVISIONS REGARDING DECLARATIONS OF CANDIDACY; AND DECLARING AN EMER-
4 GENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 34-1404, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 34-1404. DECLARATION OF CANDIDACY. (1) Candidates for election in any
9 political subdivision shall be nominated by nominating petitions, each of
10 which shall bear the name of the nominee, the office for which the nomination
11 is made, the term for which nomination is made, and the signature of not less
12 than five (5) electors of the candidate's specific zone or district of the
13 political subdivision and shall be filed with the county clerk of the politi-
14 cal subdivision.
15 (2) The clerk of each political subdivision shall provide to the county
16 clerk an accurate and detailed map of all subdistricts of the political sub-
17 division no later than January 1 of each year in which an election may be
18 held.
19 (2) (3) The form of the nominating petition shall be as provided by the
20 county clerk and shall be uniform for all political subdivisions.
21 (3) (4) The nomination petition shall be filed beginning at 8:00 a.m.
22 on the twelfth Monday before the election and no later than 5:00 p.m. on the
23 tenth Friday before such election. The county clerk shall notify the clerk
24 of the political subdivision shall notify the county clerk within twenty-
25 four (24) hours of receipt of the filing and verify the qualifications of the
26 nominees and shall, no more than seven (7) five (5) days after the close of
27 filing, certify the nominees to be placed on the ballot of the political sub-
28 division.
29 (5) Within forty-eight (48) hours of receipt of such notification, the
30 clerk of the political subdivision shall review and certify to the county
31 clerk the nominees to be placed on the ballot of the political subdivision.
32 (4) (6) Nominating petitions shall include campaign contact informa-
33 tion for candidates, including phone numbers and email addresses.
34 (5) (7) All information in nominating petitions shall be made publicly
35 available upon request.
36 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
37 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
38 July 1, 2026.
