LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 707
BY BUSINESS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PLATS AND VACATIONS; PROVIDING LEGISLATIVE INTENT; AMENDING
3 CHAPTER 13, TITLE 50, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION
4 50-1335, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING AN ADMINISTRA-
5 TIVE PROCESS FOR THE DIVISION OF LAND; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
6 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the Legislature to
9 permit homeowners, builders, and lenders to separate ownership or financing
10 of land containing an approved or existing accessory dwelling unit or simi-
11 lar secondary structure without requiring a full subdivision process and to
12 allow individuals to more easily split lots so that mortgages or financing
13 can be accessed. It is the intent of the Legislature to resolve financing
14 barriers for permitted alternative accessory dwelling units and secondary
15 dwellings. It is not the intent of the Legislature to facilitate outward
16 development, subdivisions by another name, or agricultural land fragmenta-
17 tion.
18 SECTION 2. That Chapter 13, Title 50, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
19 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
20 ignated as Section 50-1335, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
21 50-1335. DIVISION OF LAND -- ADMINISTRATIVE PROCESS. (1) Notwith-
22 standing any other provision of this chapter to the contrary, cities or
23 counties may establish an administrative process for land division, which
24 shall not be subject to subdivision platting requirements, for the pur-
25 pose of facilitating a mortgage or financing division of land that contains
26 an existing or approved accessory dwelling unit or secondary residential
27 structure. The need for a mortgage or financing division of land shall be ev-
28 idenced by a letter from a lender or mortgage underwriter demonstrating that
29 parcel separation is required for financing. Such process may be used if:
30 (a) The division is solely for mortgage or financing purposes and does
31 not create additional residential entitlements or increase allowable
32 density beyond what is permitted by applicable zoning regulations;
33 (b) The division does not authorize additional dwelling units or fur-
34 ther land divisions on either parcel created by the division and the di-
35 vision attaches to the original parcel and runs with the land; and
36 (c) Each resulting parcel has adequate legal access and utility service
37 in accordance with applicable local standards.
38 (2) Nothing in this section shall be construed to permit serial or re-
39 cursive division through conveyance, reconfiguration, or recombination and
40 re-split methods.

2
1 (3) Divisions made pursuant to the process provided in this section
2 shall be recorded with the county recorder and shall include a declaration
3 restricting further division or development inconsistent with this section.
4 (4) Nothing in this section shall be construed to alter or supersede
5 agricultural protections provided for bona fide divisions of agricultural
6 land under this chapter or to authorize the creation of new building lots in
7 violation of local zoning or density standards.
8 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
10 July 1, 2026.
