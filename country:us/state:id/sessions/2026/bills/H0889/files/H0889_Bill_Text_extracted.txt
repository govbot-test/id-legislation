4 SECTION 4. That Section 67-9210, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
5 amended to read as follows:
6 67-9210. AWARD OF CONTRACT. (1) The administrator shall award con-
7 tracts to, and place orders for property with, the lowest responsible
8 bidder. Qualifications for responsibility shall be prescribed by rule.
9 However, environmental, social, and governance standards may not be used as
10 a qualification for responsibility. Prior performance on state contracts
11 may be used to determine qualifications.

13 preference shall be given to property of local and domestic production and
14 manufacture or from bidders having a significant Idaho economic presence as
