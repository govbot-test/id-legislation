LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 950
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE LEGISLATURE; APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL
3 MONEYS TO THE LEGISLATURE FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; PROVIDING REAPPROPRIA-
4 TION AUTHORITY FOR THE LEGISLATURE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PRO-
5 VIDING EFFECTIVE DATES.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Legislature $200,000 from the General
9 Fund to be expended for operating expenditures for the period July 1, 2025,
10 through June 30, 2026.
11 SECTION 2. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY. There is hereby reappropriated
12 to the Legislature any unexpended and unencumbered balances appropriated
13 or reappropriated to the Legislature from the General Fund for fiscal year
14 2026, to be used for nonrecurring expenditures for the period July 1, 2026,
15 through June 30, 2027.
16 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, Section 1 of this act shall be in full force and effect
18 on and after passage and approval, and Section 2 of this act shall be in full
19 force and effect on and after July 1, 2026.
