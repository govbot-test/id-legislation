LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 877
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DIVISION OF VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION;
3 APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE DIVISION OF VOCATIONAL REHABIL-
4 ITATION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING
5 AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Division of Vocational Rehabilitation
9 for the Council for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing Program $2,100 from the Gen-
10 eral Fund to be expended for operating expenditures for the period July 1,
11 2026, through June 30, 2027.
12 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
14 July 1, 2026.
