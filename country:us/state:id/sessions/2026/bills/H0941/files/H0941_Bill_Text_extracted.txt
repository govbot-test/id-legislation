LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 941
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ADMINISTRATIVE RULES; AMENDING SECTION 67-5202, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE OFFICE OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE RULES CO-
4 ORDINATOR; REPEALING SECTION 67-5292, IDAHO CODE, RELATED TO THE PERI-
5 ODIC REVIEW OF ADMINISTRATIVE RULES; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 67-5202, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 67-5202. OFFICE OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE RULES COORDINATOR. (1) There is
10 hereby established the office of the administrative rules coordinator in the
11 division of financial management legislative services office. The coordi-
12 nator shall be a nonclassified employee and shall be appointed by and serve
13 at the pleasure of the administrator of the division of financial management
14 director of the legislative services office. All other employees of the of-
15 fice of the administrative rules coordinator shall be nonclassified posi-
16 tions, and any persons employed to fill positions in the office of the admin-
17 istrative rules coordinator thereafter shall be exempt from the provisions
18 of chapter 53, title 67, Idaho Code.
19 (2) The coordinator shall have the authority to make clerical revisions
20 or to correct manifest typographical or grammatical errors to both proposed
21 and existing rules that do not alter the sense, meaning or effect of such
22 rules changes to both proposed and existing rules prior to publication in the
23 administrative bulletin pursuant to section 67-5203, Idaho Code.
24 (3) The coordinator shall receive all notices and rules required in
25 this chapter to be published in the bulletin or the administrative code.
26 The coordinator shall prescribe a uniform style, form, and numbering system
27 which shall apply to all rules adopted by all agencies. The coordinator
28 shall review all submitted rules for style, form, and numbering and may re-
29 turn a rule that is not in proper style, form, or number.
30 SECTION 2. That Section 67-5292, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
31 repealed.
32 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
33 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
34 passage and approval.
