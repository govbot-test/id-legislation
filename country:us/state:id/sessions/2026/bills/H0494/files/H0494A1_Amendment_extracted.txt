IN THE SENATE
SENATE AMENDMENT TO H.B. NO. 494
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 3
2 On page 4 of the printed bill, delete lines 12 through 26, and insert:
3 "32-1805. TEMPORARY CARE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS. (1) A For
4 a temporary care assistance program, the department of health and welfare
5 shall check against the state's sex offender registry operated by the Idaho
6 state police and against the national sex offender public website operated
7 by the United States department of justice that checks names and addresses
8 in the registries before allowing someone to become a temporary caregiver
9 within the program's service. The process must include a criminal history
10 and background check conducted by the department of health and welfare
11 comparable to the background check processed for foster parents. The back-
12 ground check shall be fingerprint-based, requiring that the fingerprints
13 of temporary caregivers be submitted to the federal bureau of investigation
14 and the Idaho state police bureau of criminal identification to complete a
15 national criminal history background check., pursuant to chapter 25, title
16 56, Idaho Code, which shall be conducted in the same manner as background
17 checks for foster parents. The background check shall be fingerprint-based
18 and shall require that the fingerprints of temporary caregivers be submitted
19 to the Idaho state police to process the background check through the state
20 criminal history repository and the federal bureau of investigation for
21 a national criminal history search. The department of health and welfare
22 shall not charge a fee for background checks required by this section.".
