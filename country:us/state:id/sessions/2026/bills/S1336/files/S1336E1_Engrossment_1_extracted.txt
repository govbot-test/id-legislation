31 SECTION 2. That Chapter 1, Title 33, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
32 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
33 ignated as Section 33-118C, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
34 33-118C. CURRICULAR MATERIALS -- HISTORY AND CIVICS. (1) Notwith-
35 standing the provisions of section 33-118, Idaho Code, the legislature finds
36 that to promote the stability of America's republican form of government and
37 to establish and maintain a general, uniform, and thorough system of public,
38 free common schools, as provided in section 1, article IX of the constitution
39 of the state of Idaho, all curricular materials adopted by school districts
40 or public charter schools in the subjects of history, American government
41 and civics, and social studies shall:
42 (a) Align with the provisions of section 33-1602, Idaho Code;
43 (b) Align with state content standards established by the state board
44 of education;
45 (c) Be academically rigorous;
46 (d) Be historically and factually accurate;
47 (e) Directly utilize or otherwise promote engagement with primary
48 sources and documents;
49 (f) Inspire patriotism;

7
1 (g) Contribute to the cultivation of good moral character and the
2 virtues necessary for self-government and human flourishing;
3 (h) Cultivate knowledge and appreciation for the nation's history and
4 cultural heritage as derived from the tradition of Western civiliza-
5 tion; and
6 (i) Foster an appreciation for and attachment to America's republican
7 form of government, along with a proper understanding of the pupils'
8 rights and duties as citizens of the state of Idaho and the United
9 States.
10 (2) School districts and public charter schools shall have the discre-

13 terials align with the requirements provided in subsection (1) of this sec-
