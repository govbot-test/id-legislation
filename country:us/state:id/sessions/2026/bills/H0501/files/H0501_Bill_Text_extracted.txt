9 34-1807. CIRCULATION OF PETITIONS -- PAID SIGNATURE GATHERERS --
10 VERIFICATION OF PETITION AND SIGNATURE SHEETS -- CERTIFICATION OF PETITION
11 SIGNERS -- CERTAIN PETITIONS AND SIGNATURES VOID. (1) Any person who cir-
12 culates any petition for an initiative or referendum shall be a resident
13 qualified elector of the state of Idaho and at least eighteen (18) years of
14 age.
15 (2)(a) Any person who accepts payment to circulate a petition for an
16 initiative or referendum shall, prior to accepting a signature:
17 (i) Verbally notify any potential signatory that such person is
18 accepting payment to gather signatures and provide the name of the
19 person or entity providing payment to such person; and
20 (ii) Cause to be printed on each page of any petition for an ini-
21 tiative or referendum a prominent and legible declaration that
22 such person is receiving payment in exchange for circulating the
23 petition and the name of the person persons or entities providing
24 payment to such person.
25 (b) At all times a person is circulating a petition pursuant to para-
26 graph (a) of this subsection, such person shall wear a badge that is
27 clearly visible to any potential signatory and that contains the words
28 "paid petition circulator" on it as well as the name of the persons or
29 entities providing payment to such person.
30 (c) The Idaho secretary of state shall promulgate rules to implement
31 the provisions of this subsection, which shall provide details re-
32 garding the font specifications that shall be required for the printed
33 declaration required under paragraph (a)(ii) of this subsection and the
34 specifications regarding font, shape, color, and size requirements for
35 the badge required under paragraph (b) of this subsection.
