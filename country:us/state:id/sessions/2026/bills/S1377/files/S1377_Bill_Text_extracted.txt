LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1377
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PUBLIC NOTICES; AMENDING CHAPTER 1, TITLE 60, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 60-106B, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS
4 ALLOWING CERTAIN GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES TO PUBLISH ABBREVIATED PUBLIC
5 NOTICES IN NEWSPAPERS IN LIEU OF FULL PUBLIC NOTICES AND REQUIRING CER-
6 TAIN GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES TO PUBLISH FULL PUBLIC NOTICES ON THE NEWS-
7 PAPER ASSOCIATION OF IDAHO'S WEBSITE FOR PUBLIC NOTICES; AND DECLARING
8 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
10 SECTION 1. That Chapter 1, Title 60, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
11 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
12 ignated as Section 60-106B, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
13 60-106B. PUBLICATION OF NOTICES -- ABBREVIATED NOTICES ALLOWED. (1)
14 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any county,
15 city, or school district board of trustees required or provided by the laws
16 of the state of Idaho to publish legal notices, advertisements, or publica-
17 tions of any kind in a newspaper of general circulation in accordance with
18 section 60-106, Idaho Code, may elect to have an abbreviated version of such
19 notice published in accordance with the provisions of this section. The
20 abbreviated public notice shall include, at a minimum:
21 (a) The name of the governmental entity issuing the public notice;
22 (b) A title and description of the contents of the public notice suffi-
23 cient to inform the public of such contents;
24 (c) The date, time, and location of any meeting, hearing, or action, if
25 applicable; and
26 (d) Either an internet link to the website address where the full public
27 notice can be found or a quick response code that links to the website
28 address where the full public notice can be found, or both.
29 (2) If all requirements of subsection (1) of this section are met, an
30 abbreviated public notice published pursuant to this section shall have the
31 same legal force and effect as full public notices published in a newspaper
32 pursuant to section 60-106, Idaho Code.
33 (3) Nothing in this section prevents a county, city, or school district
34 board of trustees from electing to publish a full public notice in a newspa-
35 per of general circulation.
36 (4) Whether a county, city, or school district board of trustees elects
37 to publish an abbreviated public notice or a full public notice in a newspa-
38 per of general circulation, such entity shall send to the newspaper a copy of
39 the full, unabbreviated public notice to be electronically published on the
40 public notices website of the newspaper association of Idaho.
