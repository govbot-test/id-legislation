Sixty-eighth Legislature Second Regular Session - 2026
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1347
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO HOUSING AND FINANCE ASSOCIATION; AMENDING SECTION
3 67-6223, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING REPORTING RE-
4 QUIREMENTS FOR THE IDAHO HOUSING AND FINANCE ASSOCIATION; AND DECLARING
5 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 67-6223, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 67-6223. BORROWING POWER -- FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE -- COOPERATION WITH
10 STATE AND FEDERAL GOVERNMENT. (1) In addition to the powers conferred upon
11 the association by other provisions of this chapter, the association is em-
12 powered to administer any other state, private or federal assistance program
13 including without limitation all tax credit programs, guaranty, loan or in-
14 vestment funds and block grants and to borrow money or accept contributions,
15 grants or other financial assistance or investment from private sources or
16 from the state or federal government for or in aid of any housing project,
17 nonprofit facility, economic development project or agricultural facility
18 within its area of operation, to take over or lease or manage any housing
19 project, nonprofit facility, economic development project or agricultural
20 facility or undertaking constructed or owned by the federal government, and
21 to these ends, to comply with such conditions and to make such trust inden-
22 tures, leases or agreements as may be necessary, convenient or desirable.
23 It is the purpose and intent of this act to authorize the association to do
24 any and all things necessary or desirable to secure the financial aid or co-
25 operation of the state or federal government in the undertaking, construc-
26 tion, maintenance or operation of any housing project, nonprofit facility,
27 economic development project or agricultural facility by the association.
28 The association is specifically authorized to work with the Idaho department
29 of agriculture in connection with any loan for an agricultural facility and
30 the Idaho department of agriculture shall assist in the provisions of such
31 loans.
32 (2) On or after July 1, 2026, the association shall report on any fed-
33 eral funds received through the continuum of care (COC) program and the emer-
34 gency solutions grant program provided in 24 CFR 578 and 24 CFR 576, respec-
35 tively, and shall report outlays and outcomes of spending of all recipient
36 and subrecipient awards, except when data is not reportable from victim ser-
37 vice providers, as follows:
38 (a) For each recipient awarded program funding, the report shall in-
39 clude the following information:
40 (i) The organization's name and address;
41 (ii) The total award amount;
