1 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary,
2 the appropriation made to the Department of Environmental Quality for the
3 Water Quality Program is hereby reduced by $181,700 from the Department of
4 Environmental Quality (Federal) Fund from personnel costs for the period

6 SECTION 3. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
7 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
8 to the Department of Environmental Quality is hereby increased by four
9 (4.00) for the period July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027.
10 SECTION 4. CASH TRANSFER FOR AIR PERMITTING FEES. There is hereby ap-
11 propriated and the Office of the State Controller shall transfer the balance
12 of the DEQ Air Permitting Fees Account to the Idaho Air Quality Permitting
13 Fund on July 1, 2026, or as soon thereafter as practicable for the period July
14 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027, to consolidate the two funds and cease to use
15 the DEQ Air Permitting Fees Account.
16 SECTION 5. CASH TRANSFER FOR DRINKING WATER FEES. There is hereby ap-
17 propriated and the Office of the State Controller shall transfer the balance
18 of the DEQ Drinking Water Fees Account to the Public Water System Supervision
19 Fund on July 1, 2026, or as soon thereafter as practicable for the period July
20 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027, to consolidate the two funds and cease to use
21 the DEQ Drinking Water Fees Account.
22 SECTION 6. CASH TRANSFER FOR SOLID WASTE. There is hereby appropriated
23 and the Office of the State Controller shall transfer $400,000 from the
24 Hazardous Waste Emergency Fund to the Solid Waste Regulatory Fund on July
25 1, 2026, or as soon thereafter as practicable for the period July 1, 2026,
26 through June 30, 2027.
27 SECTION 7. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
28 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
29 July 1, 2026.
