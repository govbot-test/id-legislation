Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 914
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL LICENSES; AMEND-
3 ING CHAPTER 26, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION
4 67-2616, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR A TEMPORARY MORATORIUM ON RULEMAK-
5 ING AND FEES; AMENDING CHAPTER 26, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION
6 OF A NEW SECTION 67-2617, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE FOR THE MIGRATION OF
7 CERTAIN ADMINISTRATIVE RULES INTO STATUTE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY
8 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
10 SECTION 1. That Chapter 26, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
11 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
12 ignated as Section 67-2616, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
13 67-2616. TEMPORARY MORATORIUM ON RULEMAKING AND FEES. (1) Notwith-
14 standing any other provision of law to the contrary, for the period July 1,
15 2026, through June 30, 2031, the division of occupational and professional
16 licenses, including the administrator of such division and the boards and
17 commissions within such division, shall not be authorized to promulgate any
18 additional administrative rules or to increase any occupational licensing
19 fee.
20 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection (1) of this section,
21 the division of occupational and professional licenses, including the ad-
22 ministrator of such division and the boards and commissions within such di-
23 vision, shall be authorized to conduct rulemaking for the purpose of repeal-
24 ing existing regulations or removing a regulatory burden.
25 SECTION 2. That Chapter 26, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
26 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
27 ignated as Section 67-2617, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
28 67-2617. MIGRATION OF DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL LI-
29 CENSES RULES INTO STATUTE. No later than December 31, 2026, the division
30 of occupational and professional licenses shall submit to the legislative
31 services office proposed legislation that transfers all division of occu-
32 pational and professional licenses administrative rule chapters less than
33 eight (8) pages into statute.
34 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
35 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
36 July 1, 2026.
